## **Top 7 Popular AI Characters of 2026: Top AI Influencers Overview**
AI is evolving rapidly and continues to change the face of marketing. Therefore, the number of AI influencers is increasing rapidly as well. It may not be possible to keep track of every new influencer on a daily basis. But there are definitely a handful of individuals who have made an impact on both our society and our culture. Regardless of who you are or where you live, you will likely have encountered at least one AI influencer at some point since 2020. Here are the top AI influencers who have captured attention across Instagram, TikTok, and even LinkedIn in 2026. Each influencer has a unique story and aesthetic to bring to the table, which is why brands find these individuals appealing to work with.
### **1. Lil Miquela**
She is undoubtedly one of the top global AI creators. Combining real-life, emotional storytelling and visual imagery, Lil Miquela provides a humanized virtual presence to users. She is evolving and demonstrating that AI-based personas have the ability to pivot and evolve.
### **2. Aitana Lopez**
With her bright colors and vivacious nature, this AI figure is becoming an Internet celebrity. The character is a fitness and lifestyle influencer, sharing a variety of gym activities, wellness methods, and encouraging messages. More than just her appearance, Aitana serves as a model for how an AI can create a brand based on authenticity, regardless of its type. A business wanting to create trust and engage customers will find an AI influencer very attractive.
### **3. Imma**
She has created an experience through her unique aesthetic and style that incorporates both digital and Japanese clothing as two distinct things. Her work is characterized by a feminine approach to futurism that creates an ethereal quality to her work. Through all of her mediums, she creates a soft and futuristic feel.
### **4. Noonoouri**
This AI influencer embraces luxury fashion and high-end brands entirely. She does not attempt to appear human; rather, Noonoouri is heavily stylized and editorially based, which helps create a distinct identity for her. Noonoouri has worked with many of the world’s leading fashion designers throughout the years, establishing that AI characters can be successful within the highest levels of fashion.
### **5. Shudu**
The digital world’s premier supermodel, Shudu, has set a precedent for what can be achieved through hyper-realism in Computer Generated Imagery (CGI) and Generative Design. As a visual representation of elegance, she has garnered numerous collaborations with leading fashion brands, including those in the high fashion industry, as well as with numerous other influencers across multiple industries.
### **6. Kuki AI**
Although Kuki does not possess the realistic appearance of a real person. It is her ability to hold conversations that allows for greater interactivity with Kuki. Initially developed as a chatbot, she has developed her own identity and has created a social audience that she engages with via chatbots, livestreams, and entertaining forms of content. Kuki demonstrates the power of AI-powered influencers who focus upon creating an interactive relationship as opposed to simply providing visual images.
### **7. Zed: The AI Tech Educator**
This AI influencer is a relatively new player in the AI industry. However, he has established himself as an authority through the development of a large online following via social media outlets. He has developed an audience for himself based on the value he provides in terms of educational content around automation and digital innovation. As a result, Zed’s reputation has been established, and people are more receptive to him on LinkedIn than they would be for many other AI authors/experts.
