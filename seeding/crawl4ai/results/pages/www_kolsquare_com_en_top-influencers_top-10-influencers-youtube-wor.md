## **The Top 10 Biggest and Most Successful YouTubers in the World**
The ranking of the top 10 largest YouTube channels in 2023 is based on Kolsquare data. Our database includes more than three million influencers who have 5K or more followers on Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, X, or YouTube. Only the genres "influencers", "bloggers," and "videographers" were considered for the list. You also get exclusive information about:
  * **Credibility score from 70/100:** Kolsquare's credibility score is a number from 0 to 100 that indicates how trustworthy an influencer is based on the authenticity of his audience. The fewer fake followers a YouTube channel has, the higher the score ( 70 and above = good).
  * **Engagement rate** : The percentage of engagement is calculated from reactions, comments, shares, likes, etc. per post. Today, this rate is one of the most important key figures for influencer marketing.
  * **Earned Media Value** : Maybe you're wondering how much you should pay your influencers? The Earned Media Value (EMV) will tell you.


**Kolsquare Insight:** We collected the data on October 17, 2023 and compiled it for you, but it may vary from day to day. Why not check out[ our platform](https://www.kolsquare.com/en/influencer-marketing-platform) for yourself and create your own rankings of the most successful influencers?
