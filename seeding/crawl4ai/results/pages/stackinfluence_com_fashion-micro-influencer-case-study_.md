### Anne Klein’s**Growth**
Anne Klein’s influencer campaign delivered measurable traffic and engagement outcomes. Influencers generated **37,254 Instagram Story impressions** and **4,592 swipe-up link clicks** , driving qualified traffic directly to Amazon listings. A total of **90 influencers completed story posts** , reaching a combined audience of **1,516,582 followers**. With a **90% campaign completion rate** , the program demonstrated reliable execution and strong influencer participation.
### Anne Klein's **User Generated Content**
Beyond traffic, the campaign produced long-term brand assets and social proof. Anne Klein accumulated **90 reusable digital video assets** and an estimated **44 organic product testimonials** from influencer participants. These assets provided ongoing value for eCommerce merchandising, paid media, and brand storytelling while reinforcing credibility through authentic influencer testimonials.
Anne Klein wanted to increase visibility and traffic for a specific line of watch products on Amazon while maintaining a premium, elegant brand aesthetic. The challenge was sourcing influencers who not only met a minimum follower threshold but also aligned visually and demographically with Anne Klein’s established brand identity. Additionally, the brand needed influencers capable of driving measurable off-platform traffic through swipe-up links, while ensuring reliable campaign execution and on-time completion across a fixed launch window.
