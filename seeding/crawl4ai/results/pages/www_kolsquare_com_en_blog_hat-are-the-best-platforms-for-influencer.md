##  **How does influencer marketing software differ in terms of pricing and features?** ‍
Influencer marketing platforms cluster into three clear segments:
### **1. Enterprise suites**
Platforms such as **Traackr** and **CreatorIQ** sit at the high end, priced between €25k and €55k per year for 5–10 seats. They combine deep listening capabilities, logo detection, AI matching, payment systems and end-to-end tracking. They’re designed for global corporations but can be overly complex and expensive.**Takeaway** : Enterprise tools deliver precision analytics, but their complexity often limits agility. Kolsquare’s strength lies in giving brands both performance and usability without the enterprise friction.
### **2. Mid-market tools**
Solutions like **Lefty, Campaygn, and Upfluence** sit between €8K and €19K per year. They offer solid discovery, analytics, and cross-platform integrations (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Twitch).
Still, many of these platforms sacrifice advanced campaign management or payment automation for cost efficiency.
**Takeaway** : Mid-market tools provide reach and flexibility but often lack full-funnel functionality. Kolsquare bridges this gap with end-to-end campaign management, making influencer operations efficient and scalable.
