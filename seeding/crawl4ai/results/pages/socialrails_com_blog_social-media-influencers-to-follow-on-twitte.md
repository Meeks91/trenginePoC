##  How to Get Value from Following
###  Create Twitter Lists
Organize by category:
  * **Daily reads:** Top 10 must-follows
  * **News:** Platform updates
  * **Inspiration:** Big-picture thinking
  * **Tactics:** How-to content


###  Engage Thoughtfully
**Do:**
  * Reply with genuine insights
  * Share their content with your commentary
  * Ask thoughtful questions


**Don't:**
  * Ask them to do things for you
  * Reply "great post!" without substance
  * DM with pitches

##  Red Flags to Unfollow
**Unfollow if they:**
  * Constantly self-promote without value
  * Share outdated advice
  * Create controversy for engagement
  * Give generic advice without specifics
  * Only sell courses, no free value

##  FAQ
How many accounts should I follow?
50-200 max that you actually read. Use Twitter Lists to organize. Quality beats quantity.
Should I turn on notifications?
Only for 5-10 accounts max. Too many notifications become noise. Use Lists instead.
Do influencers respond to followers?
Many do, especially to valuable replies. Reply early to new posts with insights. Don't take silence personally.
How do I find new accounts to follow?
Look at who your favorites engage with. Check "Who to follow" suggestions. Search industry hashtags. Review conference speaker lists.
