Australian food creators produce a wide range of content, such as:
Restaurant and café reviews
Cooking tutorials & recipe videos
Budget-friendly meals and weekly prep ideas
Fine dining & tasting menu experiences
Local producer spotlights

Restaurant openings & menu launches

Audience relevance: Do followers match your target market (location, age, interests)?
