## What’s a Lifestyle Influencer?
There are around **600K creators on Instagram** who are classified as lifestyle influencers. 
According to recent studies, the [**lifestyle influencer**](https://www.statista.com/statistics/1191930/main-influencer-categories-uk/) comes out on top as the most popular category of influencer.
**In fact, the term has been growing exponentially on Google in the last few years:**
The truth is… just about anyone can be a lifestyle influencer!
But what do lifestyle influencers do? Well – the great thing about this category of creator – think of Instagram lifestyle influencers as related to the YouTube vlogger. 
**They document their daily lives and product recommendations.**
From _#OOTD_ posts (Outfit of The Day,) to what they had for breakfast… 
Instagram lifestyle influencers know how to create submersive content that drives engagement! They are usually a great fit for luxury brands.
