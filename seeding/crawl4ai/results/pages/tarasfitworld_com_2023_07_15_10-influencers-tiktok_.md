# 10 Best Female Fitness Influencers to Follow on Tiktok
2023-08-19T15:35:01+00:00
_Hey guys,_
Today’s blog post is about the **10 best female fitness influencers to follow on Tiktok**. In this blog post, I go over _who these fitness influencers are_ and what makes their profiles unique! 
The benefits of following fitness influencers on social media platforms include _increased motivation, productivity and longevity_ in fitness. 
Make sure to** _unfollow_** any account that dissuades you from becoming your _healthiest_ and _strongest_ self! 
Without further ado, here are** _10 female fitness influencers to follow on Tiktok to inspire your best self_**! 
## 1) [Demi Bagby](https://www.tiktok.com/@demibagby)
### Number of followers: 
14.4M followers
### About Demi:
Demi Bagby is a **22 year old** American _crossfit athlete, bodybuilder_ and _social media personality_.
Her Tiktok videos include her performing _extreme stunts_ , _showing off her flexibility_ and _strength_ as well as inspiring others to get fit by performing **multiple sports**. 
From _kickboxing, lifting, sprinting,_ to _swimming, doing backflips_ and _skydiving_ , a daily dose of Demi inspires us all to get**active.**
### Why should you follow her? 
Demi is super _positive_ and _inspiring_. Her [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/demibagby/?hl=en) bio lists **#PowerfullyPositive** and good vibes on top of _racing cars, sports_ and _fitness_. 
She makes being fit seem **fun** with the variety of sports she showcases on her profile. 
This is the complete opposite of any typical**bodybuilding** account that fixates our attention towards _repetition, calories_ and _exercises_! 
## 2) [Rebecca Louise ](https://www.tiktok.com/@rebeccalouisefitness?_t=8eI5YbOu03k&_r=1)
### Number of followers: 
525.k followers 
### About Rebecca: 
Rebecca Louise is an online _health_ and _wellness_ _coach_ with a passion for **motivating women** to achieve their best health. 
With over _500k followers_ on TikTok and Instagram, Rebecca’s content is both **inspirational** and **educational** at the same time. 
> [@rebeccalouisefitness](https://www.tiktok.com/@rebeccalouisefitness?refer=creator_embed)
### Why should you follow her? 
Follow Rebecca for _lifestyle_ and _mindset_ tips, _healthy recipes_ and _workout_ challenges. She also documents her journey of climbing **Mount Everest**. 
## 3) [Rachel Dillon ](https://www.tiktok.com/@racheljdillon?_t=8eI5b5tFIuO&_r=1)
### Number of followers: 
952.4 k followers 
### About Rachel: 
Rachel Dillon is a _WBFF bikini world champion, fitness entrepeneur, fitness model_ and _social media celebrity_. She is the founder of both an [app](https://movewithus.com.au) and an [athleisure](https://au.shopcsb.com/) company. 
> [@racheljdillon](https://www.tiktok.com/@racheljdillon?refer=creator_embed)
### Why should you follow her? 
Aside from being _body goals_ , fitness model Rachel Dillon’s content is both _aspirational_ and _informative_. 
Her videos range from tips on how to overcome _gym anxiety_ to her favorite _booty, pilates_ and _warmup_ exercises.
She lets us follow along a _day in her life_ and go through her **meals** and**training** sessions. Dillon’s _expert_ content can truly transform our physiques into our most **feminine** and **strong** ones.

## 5) [Kayla Itsines](https://www.tiktok.com/@kayla_itsines?lang=en)
### Number of followers: 
83.6K followers
### About Kayla:
Kayla Itsines is a _fitness trainer_ and _founder_ of the [Sweat App](https://www.sweat.com/) and community. Kayla’s career began in 2008 and grew a loyal fanbase on. 
After her social media _success_ , she launched her Sweat App in 2015 which was vote the **_#1 fitness app i_** n the App Store that same year. 
Kayla regularly hosts live _fitness events_ , relishes in _entrepeneurship_ and is a _mother._ She also creates **viral** and**inspirational** content on her _Tiktok, Youtube_ and other social media platforms.
### Why should you follow her?
Kayla posts regular workouts and challenges including _squat, cardio_ and _abs_ challenges, exercise tutorials and _full length_ workouts. 
## 6) [The Daily Victorian](https://www.tiktok.com/@thedailyvictorian)
### Number of followers:
836.6k followers
### About Victoria:
Victoria is a _yogi, content creator, yoga teacher, corporate lawyer_ and _poet_. She also has her own [yoga app](https://playbookapp.io/creators/thedailyvictorian) that’s been featured on Vogue and Cosmopolitan.
### Why should you follow her?
Follow Victoria if you have a passion for _yoga_ , improving your _mental health_ and reconnecting to your _self_ and _spirituality_. 
## 7) [Alexia Clarke ](https://www.tiktok.com/@alexia_clark?_t=8eOZQHLc5nM&_r=1)
### Number of followers: 
189.7k followers
### About Alexia:
Alexia Clarke is a **social media celebrity** , _fitness icon_ and _model_. She is also known on the internet as ‘the queen of workouts’. 
### Why should you follow her?
Clark’s TikTok account is a great resource for anyone looking out for new _workouts_. From _arms_ , **glutes** or _abs_ , Clark has a workout made just for you.
## [8) Emily Skye](https://www.tiktok.com/@emilyskye?lang=en)
### Number of followers: 
30k followers
### About Emily: 
Emily Skye is an Australian _fitness influencer_ , _mother_ , _online creator_ and _health fanatic_. 
### Why should you follow her?
She gives us a glimpse into her life as a _fitness influencer_ as well as giving great tips to _aspiring mothers_ who want to stay fit during their pregnancies. 
Her **workouts** and **entertaining** content are well worth the follow! 
## [9) Gainsbykatt](https://www.tiktok.com/@gainsbykatt?lang=en)
### Number of followers: 
171.5k followers 
### About Katt:
Kat Poulopoulos is a _female bodybuilder_ who shares her journey to getting shredded on her Tiktok account. Not only is her physique **incredible** but she shares her tips on how to achieve it. 
She is also a brand _representative_ for the clothing brand and supplement brand [Legion Athletics](https://legionathletics.com). 
### Why should you follow her?
She will inspire you to reach your bodybuilding goals in both an **entertaining** and **aspirational** way. She is a source of inspiration for any woman trying to build a muscular physique without fear or diverting from _traditional gender roles_. 
Follow her for **bodybuilding** workouts and tips on how to develop your best _physique_. 
## [10) Minneninja (Jennifer Tavernier)](https://www.tiktok.com/@minneninja?lang=en)
### Number of followers: 
984.2K followers
### About Jennifer: 
Tavernier is a famous _reality star_ , _athlete_ and _fitness model_. She also competed in the show **American Ninja Warrior** and created her own calisthenics program app called the [Minnie Ninja app](https://www.aussieninjawarrior.com.au/minnie-ninja-app/#prettyPhoto). 
### Why should you follow her?
Follow Jennifer for her amazing _strength training workouts_ , plank challenges, _strength feats_ (like her hitting a deadlift PR) and _calisthenics_ exercises.
