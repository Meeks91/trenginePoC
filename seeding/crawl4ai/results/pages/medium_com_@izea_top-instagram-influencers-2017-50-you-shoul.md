# Top Instagram Influencers 2017: 50 You Should Follow
10 min read Oct 25, 2017
Listen
Share
Social media is always changing. Today’s top influencer is [tomorrow’s old news](https://izea.com/2017/01/10/top-instagram-influencers-2016/), while a relative unknown can move on up pretty quickly. Take a look at 50 top Instagram influencers for 2017. You’re likely to see a few familiar faces, plus a couple of newcomers.
## Selena Gomez
Pop singer [Selena Gomez](https://www.instagram.com/selenagomez/) has pushed her way to the top of the list of the top Instagram influencers 2017. With more than 128 million followers, she’s the most-followed influencer on Instagram. She posts about her music, political concerns and health issues. According to [Social Blade](https://socialblade.com/instagram/user/selenagomez), she gains an average of 56K followers per day, and more than one million per month.
## Ariana Grande
Another pop star, [Ariana Grande](https://www.instagram.com/arianagrande/) trails Selena on the list of the top Instagram influencers 2017 with 114 million followers. Her profile is full of photos of her at work, as well as posts about causes she cares about.
## Cristiano Ronaldo
Soccer star [Cristiano Ronaldo](https://www.instagram.com/cristiano/) has pushed his way into the number three spot of the top Instagram influencers this year, with 112 million followers (up from 86.2 million when the previous list was published).
## Beyoncé
OK, she did just give birth to twins, so it’s understandable that [Queen Bey](https://www.instagram.com/beyonce/) has fallen from the second spot to the fourth this year’s list of the top Instagram influencers. Her follower count is up, though, to 107 million.
## Taylor Swift
Last year’s top Instagram influencer, [Taylor Swift](https://www.instagram.com/taylorswift/) has fallen to the number five spot, with 104 million followers. She’s been working on a new album, which might explain her less frequent posting.
## Kim Kardashian West
[Kim Kardashian West](https://www.instagram.com/kimkardashian/) has also dropped a bit in the rankings, falling from number three to number six on our list of the top Instagram influencers. Still, she’s the most popular member of the Kardashian-Jenner clan on Instagram, with 103 million followers.
## Kylie Jenner
Model and cosmetics entrepreneur (and half-sister to Kim Kardashian), [Kylie Jenner](https://www.instagram.com/kyliejenner/) has 98.5 million followers, making her number seven on our list of 2017’s top Instagram influencers.
## The Rock
Former pro wrestler turned actor turned fitness inspo [the Rock](https://www.instagram.com/therock/) has 94.5 million followers, landing him at number eight on our list of 2017’s top Instagram influencers.
## Justin Bieber
Canadian pop singer and top Instagram influencer [Justin Bieber](https://www.instagram.com/justinbieber/) makes it to the list this year with more than 92 million followers. He posts photos of himself, his musician friends, and the occasional political commentary.
## Nicki Minaj
Hip-hop artist [Nicki Minaj](https://www.instagram.com/nickiminaj/) has 83.6 million followers and is another newcomer to the list of the top Instagram influencers 2017. Her profile is full of album promotion posts and photos of her wearing some great designer fashion.
## Kendall Jenner
Fashion model and top Instagram influencer [Kendall Jenner](https://www.instagram.com/kendalljenner/) has had a rough year. She’s fallen to the 11th spot (from number five), and caught a lot of flack for that failure of a [Pepsi ad](https://izea.com/resources/shareworthy-ep-3-shane-barker-interview/). She still has more than 83 million followers, though.
## Leo Messi
[Leo Messi](https://www.instagram.com/leomessi/) is another soccer star and top Instagram influencer. He’s fallen a bit in the ranking this year (from number six), but has 81.4 million followers.
## Khloe Kardashian
Yeah, pretty much every member of the Kardashian family is a top Instagram influencer. [Khloe Kardashian](https://www.instagram.com/khloekardashian/) joins our list this year with 69.4 million followers and her own app.
## Kourtney Kardashian
The least popular of the Kardashian/Jenner sisters on the list of the top Instagram influencers 2017 (sorry), [Kourtney](https://www.instagram.com/kourtneykardash/) has 58.9 million followers and her own app as well.
## Rihanna
Pop/hip-hop star and top Instagram influencer [Rihanna](https://www.instagram.com/badgalriri/) recently released a cosmetics collab with Fenty Beauty. Her Instagram is on point this year with more than 57 million followers.
## Zendaya
Another newcomer to the list of top Instagram influencers 2017, [pop star Zendaya](https://www.instagram.com/zendaya/) has almost 45 million followers, and a thoroughly “woke” profile.
## Cara Delevingne
Model, actor and Instagram influencer [Cara Delevingne](https://www.instagram.com/caradelevingne/) has gained some four million followers since last year (she now has more than 40 million). But she’s fallen 10 spots in our rankings, from number seven to number 17.
## Emma Watson
Actor, UN ambassador and Instagram influencer [Emma Watson](https://www.instagram.com/emmawatson/) doesn’t post that much, but each post she makes gets millions of likes. She currently has 40.1 million followers.
## Gigi Hadid
Model, Tommy Hilfiger collaborator and Instagram influencer [Gigi Hadid](https://www.instagram.com/gigihadid/) has 36 million followers. Her posts are a mix of high fashion and political commentary.
## Zayn
Singer and former One Direction-er [Zayn Malik](https://www.instagram.com/zayn/) has more than 24 million followers. He’s actually dropped in the Instagram influencer rankings this year, from number 11 to number 20. But hey, he’s pushed past his former bandmates.
## Harry Styles
[Harry Styles](https://www.instagram.com/harrystyles/) is another former One Direction member and Instagram influencer with 21.9 million followers.
## Huda Beauty
[Huda Kattan](https://www.instagram.com/hudabeauty/) is a beauty blogger, Instagram influencer and makeup artist who now has her own line of cosmetics. She’s got 21.8 million followers on Instagram, up from 16.4 million when she made our list last year.
## Cameron Dallas
[Cameron Dallas](https://www.instagram.com/camerondallas/) is an internet personality/comedian and a 2017 top Instagram influencer with 20.5 million followers. He’s dropped a bit in the rankings this year, from number 16 to number 23.
## Zach King
[Zach King](https://www.instagram.com/zachking/) is a funny guy and a 2017 Instagram influencer with 20.3 million Instagram followers.
## Niall Horan
[Niall Horan](https://www.instagram.com/niallhoran/) is another former member of the band One Direction. Since the band’s hiatus, his star and status as an Instagram influencer has fallen a bit, although he does still have 19.6 million followers and a new album on the way.
## Snoop Dogg
Rapper and Instagram influencer [Snoop Dogg](https://www.instagram.com/snoopdogg/) is a new addition to our list. He’s got 19.5 million followers.
## Lele Pons
[Lele Pons](https://www.instagram.com/lelepons/) has 19.1 million followers and is a recent addition to our list of Instagram influencers. Formerly famous on Vine, she’s found a new home for herself on the ‘gram.
## Lucy Hale
Actor [Lucy Hale](https://www.instagram.com/lucyhale/) is another newbie on our list Instagram influencers. She has 19 million followers.
## Alia Bhatt
Actor [Alia Bhatt](https://www.instagram.com/aliaabhatt/) regularly stars in Bollywood films and is another new addition to our list of Instagram influencers. She has 18.2 million followers and has recently been posting about the work she does with CoExist, an animal rescue organization.
## Shay Mitchell
Like her co-star Lucy Hale (on “Pretty Little Liars”), [Shay Mitchell](https://www.instagram.com/shaymitchell/) is a new Instagram influencer on our list. She has 18 million followers.
## Stephen Curry
Basketball player and Instagram influencer [Stephen Curry](https://www.instagram.com/stephencurry30/) has 17.7 million followers. He posts about the sport and his family life, including the projects his wife, Ayesha Curry, is working on.
## Michelle Lewin
[Michelle Lewin’s](https://www.instagram.com/michelle_lewin/?hl=en) Instagram influencer profile is all about fit-spo. She has more than 12 million followers.
## Zoella
British beauty/haul vlogger [Zoella](https://www.instagram.com/zoella/?hl=en) (aka Zoe Suggs) might be better known for her YouTube videos, but she’s got a sizable audience as an Instagram influencer. She has 11.2 million followers on the site.
## Chiara Ferragni
Last year, we ranked Italian fashion blogger and Instagram influencer [Chiara Ferragni](https://www.instagram.com/chiaraferragni/) at number 22. This year, she’s fallen to the number 34 spot, although her follower count has climbed to 10.5 million.
## Josh Ostrovsky
[Josh Ostrovsky](https://www.instagram.com/thefatjewish/?hl=en), aka “The Fat Jew,” is known for his humorous Instagram posts. He has 10.1 million followers, but has dropped from the number 19 spot to number 35 this year.
## Kayla Itsines
Fit-spo Instagram influencer [Kayla Itsines](https://www.instagram.com/kayla_itsines/?hl=en) has 7.6 million followers.
## Lilly Singh
Canadian entertainer Lilly Singh is definitely one of the top Instagram Influencers 2017 with her 6.9 million followers. Plus, she’s a new addition to our list this year.
## Camila Coelho
Brazilian beauty blogger and top Instagram influencer [Camila Coelho](https://www.instagram.com/camilacoelho/?hl=en) has 6.6 million followers, earning her the number 38 spot, down from number 25 last year.
## Joanna Gaines
Maybe it’s because her show got cancelled, but “Fixer Upper” and designer [Joanna Gaines](https://www.instagram.com/joannagaines/?hl=en) has dropped from number 30 to number 39 on our list of the 2017 Top Instagram influencers this year. She has 5.4 million followers.
## Ayesha Curry
Another newcomer to our list, [Ayesha Curry](https://www.instagram.com/ayeshacurry/?hl=en) is the wife of Stephen Curry, a cook, and an Instagram influencer with 5.2 million followers.
## Aimee Song
Fashion blogger and Instagram influencer [Aimee Song](https://www.instagram.com/songofstyle/?hl=en) joins our list this year with 4.6 million followers.
## Murad Osmann
Traveler, producer, photographer and 2017 top Instagram influencer [Murad Osmann](https://www.instagram.com/muradosmann/?hl=en) gained his fame thanks to the “follow me” shots he took, featuring his now-wife. He has 4.6 million followers, but has dropped from the 28th spot to number 42.
## Manny Gutierrez
Makeup artist [Manny Gutierrez](https://www.instagram.com/mannymua733/?hl=en) actually got his start as an Instagram influencer, and maintains a strong following there. Four million people follow him.
## Simeon Panda
[Simeon Panda](https://www.instagram.com/simeonpanda/?hl=en) is another newbie to our list of top Instagram influencers 2017. A fitness entrepreneur, he has 3.4 million followers.
## Grumpy Cat
Yes, he’s a cat who looks grumpy, but he’s still an Instagram influencer. [Grumpy Cat](https://www.instagram.com/realgrumpycat/?hl=en) has 2.4 million followers on Instagram.
## Ingrid Nilsen
Beauty blogger and Instagram influencer [Ingrid Nilsen’s](https://www.instagram.com/ingridnilsen/?hl=en) follower count has stayed the same between last year and this year: 1.7 million. But her ranking has dropped from number 37 to number 46.
## Meghan Reinks
Actor/food blogger [Meghan Reinks](https://www.instagram.com/meghanrienks/?hl=en)‘ follower count has also stayed the same over the course of a year, at 1.3 million. She’s dropped from the number 39 spot to number 47 on this year’s list of the top Instagram influencers.
## Amber Fillerup
Mommy blogger [Amber Fillerup](https://www.instagram.com/amberfillerup/?hl=en) has 1.3 million followers and is new to the list of top Instagram Influencers 2017. She’s been profiled in “Forbes” and the “Atlantic.”
## Julia Engel
Fashion and lifestyle blogger [Julia Engel](https://www.instagram.com/juliahengel/?hl=en) was ranked number 44 last year. This year, she’s grabbed the 49th spot and has 1.1 million followers.
## Deliciously Ella
Healthy living/food blogger [Ella Mills](https://www.instagram.com/deliciouslyella/?hl=en), aka Deliciously Ella, has moved to the 50th spot this year, from number 45. The Instagram influencer has pushed past the million follower mark, though, and now has 1.1 million followers.
