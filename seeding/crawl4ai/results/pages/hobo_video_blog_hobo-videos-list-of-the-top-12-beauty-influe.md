##  **1.**[**James Charles**](https://www.instagram.com/jamescharles/)
James Charles is a successful entrepreneur, social media influencer, YouTuber, and makeup artist from the US. At the age of just seventeen, he became the first male ambassador of the popular cosmetics brand [CoverGirl](https://www.allure.com/story/first-male-covergirl-james-charles).[ James Charles](https://www.morphe.com/collections/the-sister-collection) in collaboration with Morphe Cosmetics has launched a clothing line called [Sisters Apparel](https://sisters-apparel.com/) and a makeup collection called [The Sister Collection](https://www.morphe.com/collections/the-sister-collection). He was also the proud recipient of People’s Choice Awards under the category of Beauty Influencer in 2018 and 2020, Shorty Awards under the category of Breakout YouTuber in 2017, and many more. James Charles also hosts a new original series called [Instant Influencer](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mF70BjNN9IA) on YouTube. The series aims to spot the next great beauty influencer and comes with a cash prize of $50,000. He also appears in the shows The Secret World of Jeffree Star and Nikita Unfiltered.
