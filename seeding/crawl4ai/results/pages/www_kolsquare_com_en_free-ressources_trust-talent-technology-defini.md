## Creators are all-in. Now it’s time for brands to catch up.
The creator economy is entering a new chapter. Creators are more professional than ever, budgets for Influencer Marketing are rising fast, and brands are prioritising long-term partnerships, niche profiles and content that can scale across platforms.
At the same time, most creators still operate as “professional part-timers”, balancing full-time jobs with content creation while navigating unstable income, rising expectations, and tougher platform and regulatory environments.
Kolsquare’s new guide, **“Trust, talent & technology: Defining the creator economy in 2026,”** breaks down the forces shaping this fast-evolving ecosystem.
Based on insights from Kolsquare’s [_Voices of the Creator Economy_](https://www.kolsquare.com/en/free-ressources/voices-of-the-creator-economy-by-kolsquare-newtonx) and [_State of Influencer Marketing in Europe_ 2025](https://www.kolsquare.com/en/free-ressources/the-state-of-influencer-marketing-in-europe-2025) reports, it uncovers how creators and brands really work today — and what it takes to build fairer, more effective partnerships.
Inside, you’ll discover why:
  * Creator professionalism is rising, yet income instability remains widespread.
  * Brand budgets are growing faster than creator earnings, especially across paid media, UGC and long-term collaborations.
  * Micro- and niche creators now sit at the centre of influence, as relevance overtakes raw reach.
  * AI improves efficiency, but trust, authenticity and human connection still win.
  * Responsible influence — transparency, ethics, fair compensation — becomes non-negotiable.
  * Instagram, TikTok and short-form video continue to define campaign execution.


The guide also introduces **Kreators by Kolsquare** , a new app designed to give creators greater control over their data and professional identity while helping brands identify the right collaborators faster.
To top it off, you’ll find an exclusive **Top-3 creators ranking across Europe** in the fashion and beauty sectors — curated using Kolsquare’s listening tool, brand performance rankings and audience credibility scoring.
