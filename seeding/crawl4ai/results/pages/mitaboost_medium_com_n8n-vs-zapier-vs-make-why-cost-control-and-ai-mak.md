## Cost Savings: How n8n Pricing Model Beats Zapier and Make
**The Elephant in the Room: Pay-Per-Action vs. Pay-Per-Workflow** Zapier and Make operate on a **“pay-per-action”** model. Every email sent, database query, or Slack message burns a task or operation. For small businesses, this adds up _fast_. Let’s break it down:
  * [**Zapier**](https://zapier.com/): Starts at $19.99/month for 750 tasks. Need 100,000 tasks? That’ll cost you approximately $734/month.
  * [**Make**](https://www.make.com/): Slightly cheaper at $181/month for 100,000 operations.
