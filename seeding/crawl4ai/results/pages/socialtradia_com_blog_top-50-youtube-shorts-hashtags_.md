## What Are YouTube Shorts?
Before we go any further, we should clarify what YouTube shorts are. In simple words, they are vertical video content that can be up to 60 seconds, and you can record, edit, and add music or filters to your videos using your mobile phone.
Note that you can record multiple videos on your device, edit them to create engaging Shorts, and directly upload them to the YouTube application.
Users who see your shorts can share, like, comment, and dislike them, and if they find your videos attractive, they can subscribe to your channel while watching the video.
One of the most important reasons people love YouTube shorts is that they are not like Instagram or Snapchat stories. These videos won’t disappear after a specific time; they will remain on YouTube as long as you wish.
## How to Make YouTube Shorts?
If you want to create YouTube shorts but don’t know where to start, consider the following explanation:
Note that you can create YouTube shorts on both your mobile and desktop, which we will examine in this section.
### Creating YouTube Shorts on mobile
Step 1- Open the YouTube application on your mobile and sign in to your account.
Step 2- Tap on the plus (+) icon and select “Create a Short.”
Step 3- If you want to create a video longer than 15 seconds, tap the “15 seconds” button in the top right of the screen and change it to 60 seconds.
Step 4- Start recording the video by holding the record button.
Step 5- Now, you have to tap the “thick” icon to preview and edit it.
Step 6- Once you have applied the necessary changes, select “Next” to add a title and set the settings for your video.
Step 7- In this step, tap “Select Audience” to determine whether the video is suitable for children.
Step 8- That’s it! Select “Upload” to finish the process by sharing your shorts.
### Creating YouTube Shorts on a desktop
Step 1- Log in to the YouTube Studio.
Step 2- Tap on the “Create” button in the top right of the screen.
Step 3- Choose “Upload Videos.”
Step 4- Select the video file you want to share. Note that it must be up to 60 seconds and in vertical form.
Step 5- Make all the necessary changes and upload your videos to your audience.
