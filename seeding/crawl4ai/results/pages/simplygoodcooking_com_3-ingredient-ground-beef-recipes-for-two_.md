# 15 Most Popular 3 Ingredient Ground Beef Recipes for Two
December 26, 2024
Cooking a delicious meal for two doesn’t have to be complicated or expensive. With just three simple ingredients, you can whip up mouthwatering ground beef recipes that are perfect for dinner or lunch. If you’re short on time, looking to save money, or want easy-to-follow recipes, these dishes are sure to impress! 
From tacos to stir-fries, these recipes are simple yet flavorful, and they prove that good food doesn’t need a long list of ingredients.
Table of Contents
Ground beef is versatile, affordable, and cooks quickly, making it [ideal for small-batch meals](https://simplygoodcooking.com/crockpot-dip-recipes/). It pairs well with countless ingredients, allowing you to create an impressive variety of meals with minimal effort. 
If you’re making a quick dinner or prepping for a cozy date night, ground beef is your go-to option. Its quick cooking time and ability to absorb flavors make it the perfect ingredient for simple, hearty meals. 
Let’s try these amazing recipes and find out how three ingredients can transform your dinner table!
I love making these Ground Beef Tacos when I need something quick and delicious. The ground beef cooks up so easily, and the taco seasoning gives it the perfect flavor. I simply brown the beef, add the seasoning, and stuff it into taco shells. It’s such a simple yet tasty meal that I can have ready in no time. If I’m in the mood, I like to top my tacos with cheese, salsa, or lettuce. It’s an easy way to enjoy tacos, and it’s perfect when cooking for two!
**Prep Time: 5 minutes | Cook Time: 10 minutes | Serves: 2**
### Ingredients:
  * 1/2 pound ground beef
  * 1 tablespoon taco seasoning
  * 4 small tortillas


### Instructions:
  1. Heat a skillet over medium heat and cook the ground beef until browned, breaking it apart as it cooks.
  2. Sprinkle the taco seasoning over the cooked beef and stir well. Add a splash of water if needed, and let it simmer for 2 minutes.
  3. Warm the tortillas in a separate pan or microwave.
  4. Divide the seasoned beef between the tortillas and serve with your favorite toppings like shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, or sour cream.


Sloppy Joes are one of my go-to meals for an easy, comforting dinner. I love how quickly I can cook the ground beef, add the Sloppy Joe sauce, and serve it on soft hamburger buns. It’s so simple, yet incredibly tasty and filling. I love the messy, saucy nature of these sandwiches—it’s just pure comfort food! When I’m short on time, I make these Sloppy Joes because they only need a few ingredients, and they never disappoint. They’re perfect for a casual dinner or when I’m craving something a little messy but satisfying!
**Prep Time: 5 minutes | Cook Time: 10 minutes | Serves: 2**
### Ingredients:
  * 1/2 pound ground beef
  * 1/3 cup ketchup
  * 2 burger buns


### Instructions:
  1. In a skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat, breaking it up into small pieces.
  2. Once cooked, stir in the ketchup and let it simmer for 3 minutes to combine the flavors.
  3. Toast the burger buns lightly in a separate pan.
  4. Spoon the beef mixture onto the buns and serve warm.


This Ground Beef and Rice Skillet is one of my favorite go-to recipes when I’m looking for something hearty but simple. The ground beef and cooked rice come together in one pan, making cleanup a breeze. Adding beef broth gives it a savory, rich flavor that’s just perfect. I love how quickly this comes together, and it’s so filling! It’s a great meal when I don’t want to spend too much time cooking but still want something comforting and delicious. Plus, it’s so easy to customize with any extra seasonings or vegetables I might have!
**Prep Time: 10 minutes (if rice is pre-cooked) | Cook Time: 10 minutes | Serves: 2**
### Ingredients:
  * 1/2 pound ground beef
  * 1 cup cooked rice
  * 1 tablespoon soy sauce


### Instructions:
  1. Cook the ground beef in a skillet over medium heat until browned.
  2. Add the cooked rice to the skillet and stir to combine.
  3. Drizzle the soy sauce over the mixture and stir again. Cook for 2-3 minutes to heat through.
  4. Serve hot and enjoy this easy meal.


Stuffed Bell Peppers are such a fun, colorful meal that I love making for dinner. It’s so satisfying to stuff the peppers with the seasoned ground beef and rice mixture. Baking them in the oven makes everything nice and tender, and the peppers add a sweet flavor that balances out the savory filling. I always feel like I’m having a healthy and hearty meal when I make these, plus they’re so easy to prepare! It’s a great way to enjoy a simple, nutritious meal, and it’s one of my favorite quick recipes for two.
**Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 20 minutes | Serves: 2**
### Ingredients:
  * 2 large bell peppers
  * 1/2 pound ground beef
  * 1/2 cup marinara sauce


### Instructions:
  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Cut the tops off the bell peppers and remove the seeds.
  2. Cook the ground beef in a skillet until browned. Stir in the marinara sauce.
  3. Stuff the beef mixture into the hollowed bell peppers.
  4. Place the peppers in a baking dish and bake for 20 minutes, or until the peppers are tender.


Beef Quesadillas are my ultimate go-to when I’m craving something cheesy, crispy, and savory. The ground beef cooks quickly, and then I just toss it with some cheese and melt it all in between two soft tortillas. It’s a meal that never fails to satisfy! The cheese gets so gooey, and the beef adds such a great flavor. If I’m feeling a little adventurous, I might add some salsa or sour cream on the side. These quesadillas are so easy to make, and they’re the perfect solution when I need a quick, tasty meal for two.
**Prep Time: 5 minutes | Cook Time: 10 minutes | Serves: 2**
### Ingredients:
  * 1/2 pound ground beef
  * 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or mozzarella)
  * 2 large tortillas


### Instructions:
  1. Cook the ground beef in a skillet over medium heat until browned.
  2. Lay one tortilla in a clean skillet over low heat. Sprinkle half the cheese over the tortilla.
  3. Add half the cooked beef on top of the cheese and sprinkle the remaining cheese on top.
  4. Place the second tortilla on top and press gently. Cook for 2 minutes per side, or until golden brown.
  5. Slice into wedges and serve.


I’m a huge fan of hearty meals, and Beef and Potato Hash is one of my favorites. The combination of ground beef, potatoes, and onions gives this dish such a comforting, satisfying flavor. I love how crispy the potatoes get, and the beef just brings everything together. It’s so easy to make in one pan, which makes it a perfect choice for a busy night. I always enjoy this dish for breakfast or dinner, and it’s one of those meals that keeps me full for hours. Plus, it’s simple and requires minimal ingredients, making it a go-to recipe for me.
**Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 15 minutes | Serves: 2**
### Ingredients:
  * 1/2 pound ground beef
  * 1 large potato, diced
  * 1/2 onion, chopped


### Instructions:
  1. Heat a skillet and cook the ground beef until browned. Remove from the pan and set aside.
  2. Add the diced potato and chopped onion to the skillet and cook until golden and tender, stirring occasionally.
  3. Return the beef to the skillet, mix well, and cook for an additional 2 minutes.
  4. Serve hot and enjoy.


Cheeseburger Patties are such a classic, and I love how simple they are to make. I just form the beef into patties, cook them in a skillet, and top them with a slice of melty cheese. The result is a juicy, cheesy burger that’s perfect when I want a quick and satisfying meal. I usually serve them with toasted hamburger buns, but you can also add any toppings you like. It’s a simple yet flavorful meal, and I love how it doesn’t take much time to prepare but still tastes amazing!
**Prep Time: 5 minutes | Cook Time: 8 minutes | Serves: 2**
### Ingredients:
  * 1/2 pound ground beef
  * 2 slices of cheese (cheddar or American)
  * Salt and pepper to taste


### Instructions:
  1. Form the ground beef into two patties and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Cook the patties in a skillet over medium heat for 3-4 minutes per side, or until cooked to your liking.
  3. Place a slice of cheese on each patty and cover the skillet for 1 minute to melt the cheese.
  4. Serve with a side of salad or fries.


Beef Lettuce Wraps are a light, fresh, and delicious meal that I enjoy when I want something low-carb but still flavorful. I cook the ground beef with soy sauce, and it brings such a savory taste to the dish. Wrapping the beef in crunchy lettuce leaves is a great alternative to traditional tacos or wraps. I love how easy and quick these wraps are to prepare, and they’re perfect when I want something light but satisfying. They’re also customizable if I want to add extra toppings or seasonings for added flavor!
**Prep Time: 5 minutes | Cook Time: 10 minutes | Serves: 2**
### Ingredients:
  * 1/2 pound ground beef
  * 6 large lettuce leaves (such as romaine or butter lettuce)
  * 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce


### Instructions:
  1. Cook the ground beef in a skillet over medium heat until browned. Stir in the hoisin sauce.
  2. Wash and dry the lettuce leaves.
  3. Spoon the beef mixture into each lettuce leaf and fold like a taco.
  4. Serve immediately for a light and tasty meal.


Beef Pasta is a simple and satisfying dish that I make when I want a hearty, comforting meal. The ground beef adds great flavor and texture to the pasta, and the best part is how easy it is to prepare. I cook the beef with a bit of seasoning, then toss it with cooked pasta and some marinara sauce. It’s a perfect combination that’s filling and delicious, and it only takes about 20 minutes to make. I love serving this dish with a side of garlic bread or a sprinkle of cheese on top for extra flavor.
**Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 10 minutes | Serves: 2**
### Ingredients:
  * 1/2 pound ground beef
  * 2 cups cooked pasta (such as penne or spaghetti)
  * 1/2 cup marinara sauce


### Instructions:
  1. Cook the ground beef in a skillet until browned. Stir in the marinara sauce and simmer for 2 minutes.
  2. Toss the cooked pasta into the skillet and mix well.
  3. Serve hot with a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese, if desired.


Beef and Mushroom Stir-Fry is one of my favorite go-to meals when I’m craving something savory and quick. The combination of tender ground beef and earthy mushrooms creates such a delicious flavor that I can’t resist. I cook the beef until it’s browned, then add sliced mushrooms and let everything cook together in one pan. A splash of soy sauce adds an extra depth of flavor, and the whole dish comes together in just a few minutes. It’s the perfect meal for when I want something easy, healthy, and packed with flavor in no time!
**Prep Time: 5 minutes | Cook Time: 10 minutes | Serves: 2**
### Ingredients:
  * 1/2 pound ground beef
  * 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  * 1 tablespoon soy sauce


### Instructions:
  1. Heat a skillet over medium heat and cook the ground beef until browned. Remove and set aside.
  2. In the same skillet, add the mushrooms and cook until softened, about 4 minutes.
  3. Return the beef to the skillet and add soy sauce. Stir well and cook for 2 more minutes.
  4. Serve hot over rice or noodles.


Beef and Egg Scramble is one of my favorite quick and protein-packed meals. It’s perfect for breakfast, lunch, or dinner when I want something hearty and satisfying. I simply cook the ground beef in a pan until it’s browned, then scramble in a couple of eggs. The combination of the savory beef and soft, fluffy eggs is absolutely delicious. It’s such a versatile meal, and I can add whatever seasonings or extra ingredients I like, such as cheese or veggies
**Prep Time: 5 minutes | Cook Time: 10 minutes | Serves: 2**
### Ingredients:
  * 1/2 pound ground beef
  * 3 large eggs
  * 1/2 cup shredded cheese


### Instructions:
  1. Cook the ground beef in a skillet over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat if necessary.
  2. Whisk the eggs in a bowl and pour them into the skillet with the beef. Cook, stirring gently, until the eggs are set.
  3. Sprinkle shredded cheese on top and let it melt. Serve immediately.


Ground Beef Nachos are my favorite comfort food when I want something cheesy, crunchy, and loaded with flavor. I brown the ground beef in a pan with a bit of seasoning, then layer it over crispy tortilla chips. I top everything with a generous amount of shredded cheese and let it melt into the beef. It’s a quick and easy dish that’s perfect for a casual meal or even a fun snack. I love to add salsa, sour cream, or guacamole on the side for extra flavor
**Prep Time: 5 minutes | Cook Time: 10 minutes | Serves: 2**
### Ingredients:
  * 1/2 pound ground beef
  * 2 cups tortilla chips
  * 1 cup shredded cheese


### Instructions:
  1. Cook the ground beef in a skillet until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  2. Spread the tortilla chips on a large plate or baking sheet.
  3. Sprinkle the cooked beef over the chips, followed by the shredded cheese.
  4. Microwave for 1-2 minutes or bake in the oven at 350°F (175°C) until the cheese is melted. Serve warm.


Beef and Veggie Stir-Fry is one of my go-to meals when I want something healthy, quick, and packed with flavor. I love how easy it is to prepare—just brown the ground beef, then toss in whatever veggies I have on hand, like bell peppers, broccoli, or carrots. The beef adds a rich, savory flavor, and the veggies provide a nice crunch. A bit of soy sauce ties everything together with a deliciously salty kick. It’s a colorful, nutritious meal that I can whip up in under 20 minutes, making it perfect for busy weeknights!
**Prep Time: 5 minutes | Cook Time: 10 minutes | Serves: 2**
### Ingredients:
  * 1/2 pound ground beef
  * 1 cup mixed vegetables (fresh or frozen)
  * 1 tablespoon soy sauce


### Instructions:
  1. Cook the ground beef in a skillet over medium heat until browned. Remove and set aside.
  2. Add the vegetables to the skillet and cook until tender, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Return the beef to the skillet and add soy sauce. Stir to combine and cook for 2 more minutes.
  4. Serve over rice or noodles for a complete meal.


Mini Meatballs are the perfect bite-sized treat that I love to make for a quick meal or snack. I simply mix ground beef with some breadcrumbs and seasonings, then roll them into small balls and cook them in a skillet. The result is tender, juicy meatballs that are full of flavor. I like to serve them with a side of marinara sauce for dipping or toss them in sauce for a more hearty meal.
**Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 15 minutes | Serves: 2**
### Ingredients:
  * 1/2 pound ground beef
  * 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  * 1 tablespoon marinara sauce


### Instructions:
  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). In a bowl, mix the ground beef, breadcrumbs, and marinara sauce until well combined.
  2. Form the mixture into small meatballs, about 1 inch in diameter, and place them on a baking sheet.
  3. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until cooked through.
  4. Serve with additional marinara sauce for dipping.


Ground Beef Chili is one of my favorite comfort foods, especially when I need something warm and hearty. The rich, savory flavor of the ground beef pairs perfectly with the bold spices and beans. I start by browning the beef, then add chili seasoning and canned tomatoes for that perfect base. Letting it simmer for a bit allows all the flavors to come together, and it’s just so satisfying. I love topping my chili with a little shredded cheese, sour cream, or even some crunchy tortilla chips. It’s an easy and filling meal that’s perfect for cozy nights!
**Prep Time: 5 minutes | Cook Time: 15 minutes | Serves: 2**
### Ingredients:
  * 1/2 pound ground beef
  * 1 cup canned beans (kidney or black beans)
  * 1/2 cup diced tomatoes


### Instructions:
  1. In a skillet, cook the ground beef until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  2. Add the beans and diced tomatoes to the skillet. Stir well.
  3. Simmer the chili for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, to let the flavors combine.
  4. Serve hot with a side of bread or crackers.
