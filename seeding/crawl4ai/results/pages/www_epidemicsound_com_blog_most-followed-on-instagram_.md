### 1. Instagram – 700 million followers
The [social media](https://www.epidemicsound.com/blog/tag/social-media/) giant boasts millions more followers than anyone else mentioned here, and is the only brand on the list. But why?
It helps that [the official Instagram account](https://www.instagram.com/instagram/) has been active since early 2012, around a year after the platform launched. At the time, Insta had more than 10 million monthly active users — that might not sound like much compared with today’s 3 billion, but the competition was smaller. If you were on the platform back in the day, chances are you followed this account. 
But that can’t sustain growth for more than a decade. The official Instagram account thrives on a blend of content covering platform-based updates, up-and-coming creators, topical posts, celebrity- and [influencer](https://www.epidemicsound.com/blog/how-to-become-an-influencer/)-endorsed posts, and good old-fashioned memes. Instagram is the most-followed Instagram account? It makes sense. 
### 2. Cristiano Ronaldo – 672 million followers 
While he’s not the most-followed account, [Cristiano Ronaldo](https://www.instagram.com/cristiano/) is comfortably the most-followed _user_ on Instagram. The Portuguese footballer publishes a regular smattering of personal content, sponsored posts, and training footage.
It doesn’t hurt when he collabs with some of the internet’s biggest names, either — in late 2024, he dropped a video with the world’s [most-subscribed YouTuber,](https://www.epidemicsound.com/blog/most-subscribed-and-viewed-youtube-channels/) MrBeast. Ronaldo’s not reinvented the wheel, but he didn’t need to.
### 3. Lionel Messi – 511 million followers 
The second most-followed person on Instagram is another footballer. [Lionel Messi](https://www.instagram.com/leomessi/) is one of the GOATs, and similar to Ronaldo, his account’s peppered with sponsored content, training footage, and the occasional personal post here and there. The star usually writes [captions](https://www.epidemicsound.com/blog/instagram-captions/) in his native Spanish, but includes English-language translations to boost inclusion. 
### 4. Selena Gomez – 415 million followers 
[Selena Gomez](https://www.instagram.com/selenagomez/) is more than whatever you think she is. Whether you know her for her music, acting, cosmetics brand, mental health advocacy, or other charity work, she covers all bases. 
Her Instagram reflects that. One day, you might see a glossy magazine shoot, stills from a roundtable interview, or a carousel of her latest press trip. The next day, it could be a behind-the-scenes clip from her [makeup](https://www.epidemicsound.com/music/themes/fashion-beauty/makeup-skincare-routine/) business, Rare Beauty, or candid snapshots with her family.
### 5. Kylie Jenner – 391 million followers 
Similar to Gomez, [Kylie Jenner](https://www.instagram.com/kyliejenner/) can be classed as many things: socialite, influencer, businesswoman. Unlike Gomez, Jenner’s popularity often stems from controversy. That comes as no surprise for a member of the Kardashian-Jenner family, but her account is popular for a reason — she sells a lifestyle. 
Jenner’s sponsored posts and branded content usually arrive in the form of a selfie or photoshoot carousel, urging followers to check out the latest drop. It’s a great strategy, keeping her business, family, and personal brand in the spotlight.
### 6. Dwayne “The Rock” Johnson – 390 million followers 
People love [Dwayne “The Rock” Johnson.](https://www.instagram.com/therock/) Even though he made his name beating people to a pulp, audiences couldn’t get enough of his charismatic, quippy energy. That charm has carried his Hollywood career, delighting viewers with a well-placed one-liner or a raise of the eyebrow.
The Rock’s Insta feed does numbers because it’s so clearly _him_ behind it. He writes honestly about box office figures, [workouts,](https://www.epidemicsound.com/music/themes/workout-wellness/) award ceremonies, and more. You don’t have to scroll long before you see him mingling with fans — he puts the time in with the people who matter, which just makes them love him more. 
### 7. Ariana Grande – 371 million followers 
[Ariana Grande’s](https://www.instagram.com/arianagrande/) popularity can’t be overstated. She’s a pop star, sure — she also took the world by storm with Wicked, does mountains of charity work, and founded her own makeup line, R.E.M. Beauty. 
She posts the usual mixture of business and pleasure, but when Grande’s promoting a new project, you can’t _move_ for it. Check out her feed during the Wicked press cycles — if you find anything other than pink dresses and green witches, you’ve got better eyes than us. 
### 8. Kim Kardashian – 353 million followers 
Everyone knows who [Kim Kardashian](https://www.instagram.com/kimkardashian/) is, and everyone has an opinion. She’s the definition of “influencer,” however you choose to use that term. But the way she wields that influence is what makes her one of the world’s most-followed people on Instagram. Just look at what she’s done for awareness of the Armenian genocide and US criminal justice reform. 
Like Jenner, Kardashian’s feed offers aspiration. It doesn’t matter if she’s posting creepy Halloween outfits, advertising her Beats collab, or driving sales for her shapewear brand, Skims — she’s got your attention, regardless of your opinion. The fact that she’s sustained this popularity throughout and beyond The Kardashians’ TV run is testament to Kim K’s staying power.
### 9. Beyoncé – 307 million followers
OK, there’s a noticeable trend running through this list: pretty much everyone has more than one hustle. [Beyoncé](https://www.instagram.com/beyonce/) is a music icon in her own right, and that’s before you even consider her philanthropy, business ventures, and [filmmaking.](https://www.epidemicsound.com/blog/tag/filmmaking/)
Beyoncé revolutionized the music industry, and people want to know _how_ she does things — what makes her tick? You’re unlikely to find the secret sauce on her Instagram, but a carefully curated grab-bag of self-portraits, professional shoots, promo materials, and candids is more than enough. 
### 10. Khloé Kardashian – 299 million followers
[Khloé Kardashian](https://www.instagram.com/khloekardashian/) draws our most-followed on Instagram list to a close, and she’s no slouch. The media personality’s business ventures, family snaps, and more drive home that larger-than-life quality shared with the rest of her family. 
What do all of these accounts have in common? Instagram itself aside, they’re all massive celebrities — people you could pick from a police lineup without a moment’s hesitation.
