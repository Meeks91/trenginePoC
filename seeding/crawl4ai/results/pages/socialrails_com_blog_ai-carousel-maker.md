##  Anatomy of a High-Performing Carousel
###  Slide 1: The Hook
Your first slide decides everything. It needs:
  * Bold headline
  * Visual intrigue
  * Promise of value
  * Reason to swipe


Weak Hook | Strong Hook  
---|---  
"Marketing Tips" | "The $0 Strategy That Grew My Audience 10x"  
"Things I Learned" | "5 Mistakes That Cost Me $50K (So You Don't Have To)"  
"Important Info" | "Stop Doing This On LinkedIn Immediately"  
###  Slides 2-8: The Value
Each middle slide should:
  * Deliver one clear point
  * Use 20-40 words max
  * Lead naturally to the next slide
  * Include visual hierarchy


###  Final Slide: The CTA
Your last slide converts attention into action:
  * Summarize key takeaway
  * Clear call-to-action
  * Ask for save/share/comment
  * Include follow prompt or next step

##  Carousel Formulas That Work
###  The Listicle
  * **Slide 1:** "7 [Topic] Mistakes Killing Your [Result]"
  * **Slides 2-8:** One mistake per slide
  * **Slide 9:** Summary + CTA


###  The Tutorial
  * **Slide 1:** "How to [Result] in [Timeframe]"
  * **Slides 2-7:** Step-by-step process
  * **Slide 8:** Final result
  * **Slide 9:** CTA


###  The Myth Buster
  * **Slide 1:** "Everything You Know About [Topic] Is Wrong"
  * **Slides 2-7:** Myth vs. Reality pairs
  * **Slide 8:** What to do instead
  * **Slide 9:** CTA


###  The Data Story
  * **Slide 1:** Surprising statistic
  * **Slides 2-6:** Data points with context
  * **Slide 7:** What it means
  * **Slide 8:** Takeaways
  * **Slide 9:** CTA

##  Common Mistakes
Mistake | Why It Fails | Fix  
---|---|---  
Weak first slide | No one swipes | Spend extra time on the hook  
Too much text | Overwhelming, people leave | Max 40 words per slide  
No clear CTA | Missed conversion opportunity | Always ask for action  
Inconsistent design | Looks unprofessional | Use templates  
Boring headline | Gets scrolled past | Use numbers, questions, or bold claims  
##  Measuring Carousel Performance
Track these metrics:
  * **Saves:** High saves = reference-worthy content
  * **Shares:** Content worth spreading
  * **Swipe-through rate:** How many reach the last slide
  * **Comments:** Quality engagement


Compare carousel performance to single-image posts. If carousels consistently win, create more of them.
