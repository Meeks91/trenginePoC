### **1. Sommer Ray**
With over 26 million followers, Sommer is one of the most recognizable faces in fitness. She's known for her standout glutes, playful personality, and candid, funny posts. She’s also built a booming activewear business and often features her equally fit mom.
If you aren’t following her already, check her out [@SommerRay](https://www.instagram.com/sommerray "Sommer Ray Instagram"). We guarantee you have some friends following her already.
[Sommer Ray's TikTok](https://www.tiktok.com/@sommerray? "Sommer Ray Tiktok")[Sommer Ray's YouTube Channel](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UC4usqlPfjsJs0XgxclDN4Tw/videos "Sommer Ray YouTube")
###  **2. Kayla Itsines**
The Aussie trainer behind SWEAT, Kayla is all about fast, effective workouts. Her 28-minute programs and post-pregnancy routines have helped millions get fit. She’s practical, uplifting, and one of TIME's most influential people online.
Follow her on Instagram [@kayla_itsines](https://www.instagram.com/kayla_itsines/ "Kayla Itsines Instagram") for fun workout videos, powerful quotes, and mouthwatering food pics.
[Kayla Itsines Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/k.itsines "Kayla Itsines Facebook")[Kayla Itsines YouTube Channel](https://www.youtube.com/user/kaylaitsines "Kayla Itsines YouTube")[Kayla Itsines TikTok](https://www.tiktok.com/@kayla_itsines? "Kayla Itsines TikTok")
### **3. Anllela Sagra**
A former Colombian model who switched to lifting and never looked back. Known for her sculpted abs and dedication to bodybuilding, she’s a fierce competitor and motivator.
Join her massive following on Instagram [@anllela_sagra](https://www.instagram.com/anllela_sagra/ "Anllela Sagra Instagram").
[Anllela Sagra Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/AnllelaSagraOfficial "Anllela Sagra Facebook")[Anllela Sagra TikTok](https://www.tiktok.com/@anllela_sagraa? "Anllela Sagra TikTok")
### **4. Valentina Lequeux**
Valentina blends strength with honesty. As a coach and fitness influencer, she’s built a strong community by encouraging self-love and mental health alongside tough training.
Follow her on Instagram [@valentinalequeux](https://www.instagram.com/valentinalequeux/ "Valentina Lequeux Instagram") for inspiring photos and real talk about positive body image.
