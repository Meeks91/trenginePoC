## What Are Influencer Outreach Tools?
As you could have assumed from the name, these are **tools that help with influencer marketing outreach,** meaning that they are essentially **software for finding influencers.**
They’re a much faster alternative than, say, going through Google or social media platforms in order to find the right partner or their contact info. 
Most of these tools have filters to help you find who you’re looking for much easier. You can filter by **audience, platform, age, location,** and more. Of course, the features of each tool differ, but we’ll get to that in a minute. 
Now, the next thing we wanted to discuss is…
## The Benefits Of Using Influencer Software Tools
The main goal of using any kind of marketing tool is to **streamline the campaign planning process.** Naturally, the same goes for influencer outreach software, but that’s not the only benefit they promise. 
Here’s what you stand to gain:
### 1. Easy Discovery
The whole point of working with this kind of platform is that you want to be able **to find influencers quickly.** So, they usually have entire **databases of influencers** where you can browse and choose the one that best aligns with your needs. 
Most of them have **filter systems in place** to make this easier and work _way_ faster than scouring social media platforms to find what you’re looking for.
### 2. Access to Your Target Audience
You know how important it is that your and your IGC creator’s niches overlap. We’ve said it before, and we’ll say it again – **their audience is your audience, too.**
An influencer outreach platform will give you an **overview of your future partner,** where you’ll be able to see who their audience is and decide whether they’re the same people who you want to promote your products to.

### 3. Communication Management
Communication is key in any business partnership, but that doesn’t mean it’s easy. In fact, a lot of problems can arise from it, which makes collaboration more challenging than it needs to be. 
But, an outreach tool allows you to **connect and communicate directly within the app itself.**
So, since everything is in one place, it’s much easier to keep track of messages and ensure nothing gets lost along the way.
### 4. Tracking & Analytics 
Okay, so you’ve found your influencer, agreed on the terms, and finally launched the campaign. Now, you want to see **how it’s performing.**
Luckily, most of these platforms will give you access to **tools for tracking metrics** such as **engagement or conversion rates.** Then, you’ll know exactly what works and what doesn’t. 
Have we said enough to convince you to give influencer outreach tools a try? Yes? Then you’ll be happy to know we’re finally moving on to…
## The Top 10 Influencer Outreach Platforms
Here, we’re going to discuss our top picks for the best tools, outline their **features,** and, of course, tell you all about their **pricing models.**
### #10 BuzzSumo
[BuzzSumo](https://buzzsumo.com/) is an all-in-one tool for content and influencer marketing. It helps you **discover influencers** that match your brand, **explore trending topics,** and **track campaign performance** – all in one place.
**Key Features:**
  * **Influencer discovery:** a database to find and analyze influencers,
  * **Content insights:** trending topics and top-performing content,
  * **Campaign analytics:** tracking campaign success with performance metrics,
  * **User-friendly interface.**


**Pricing:**
  * Content: $199/mo (1 user),
  * PR & Communications: $299/mo (5 users),
  * Suite: $499/mo (10 users),
  * Enterprise: $999/mo (30 users).


### #9 Klear (now Meltwater)
[Klear,](https://www.meltwater.com/en/blog/klear-is-officially-part-of-meltwater?utm_source=direct&utm_medium=klear) who is now part of[ Meltwater,](https://www.meltwater.com/en) is an influencer marketing tool that just makes discovering and connecting with influencers easier.
Here’s what they have to offer:
**Key Features:**
  * **Influencer discovery:** with the ability to search using AI and advanced filters,
  * **Analytics:** reach, audience demographics, and engagement,
  * **Relationship management:** communication with influencers,
  * **Campaign tracking:** mentions, ROI, and best-performing content.


**Pricing:** available on request, but there are **three packages:** Essentials, Suite, and Enterprise
### #8 Pitchbox
[Pitchbox](https://pitchbox.com/) is an outreach platform specifically for SEO services, so they’re mostly aimed at **text-based influencers.** But they’ve been helping brands scale their campaigns and manage workflows in more ways than that.
**Key Features:**
  * **Contact discovery:** contact details for bloggers and influencers,
  * **CRM:** team communication and tasks,
  * **Reports:** insights into campaign performance,
  * **Outreach workflow:** for segmenting & delegating tasks.


**Pricing:**
  * Professional: starts at $550/mo,
  * Enterprise: starts at $1,500/mo.


### #7 Sprout Social
[Sprout Social,](https://sproutsocial.com/influencer-marketing/) formerly [Tagger,](https://www.taggermedia.com/) is an influencer marketing platform & software with an **affinity algorithm** that’s there to help you find influencers whose communities and branding align with your own.
