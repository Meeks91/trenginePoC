## How to Find Trending Songs for Instagram Reels?
Let’s see the best ways to find the latest trending songs for your Reels on Instagram.
### Instagram Inspiration
This feature is only available for professional accounts and helps businesses and creators find trending audio and Reels that your followers are interested in. 
How to access this section? Follow these steps:
Step 1- Open Instagram and go to your profile
Step 2- Tap “Professional dashboard.”
Step 3- Select “Inspiration” from the menu.
Step 4- Tap “Audio” to see a list of trending songs that are popular with your followers.
### Reel explore page
Instagram has a separate section from the Explore page that allows users to scroll only through Reels, which is a perfect source for accessing the top songs for your videos.

## How to Add Trending Songs to Instagram Reels?
Follow the steps below to easily add trending songs to your Reels on Instagram.
Step 1- Open Instagram and tap on the + between the magnifier and the Reel icon.
Step 2- Choose “Reel” from the options in the bottom left.
Step 3- Select the existing video or desired content and tap “Next.”
Step 4- Adjust the desired timing for your content and tap “Next.”
Step 5- Tap “Edit video” or swipe up the menu.
Step 6- Choose “Tap to add audio.”
Step 7- Select your favorite trend audio from the suggested list or saved songs.
Step 8- Adjust necessary edits and tap “Done.”
Step 9- Tap on the blue next icon and tap “Share” to finish the process. (You can also save the video as a draft to make further changes if needed).

## Tips for Using Trending Songs on Instagram Reels
Follow these tips to effectively use trending songs in your Instagram Reels.
### Ensure the audio is licensed. 
Instagram respects both users’ and artists’ rights, which is why you must ensure the audio you are using is licensed and has the name of the artist and the track. Otherwise, your content will be removed or played with no sounds.
### Match the song’s vibe to your content.
Use the right song on your video that matches the vibe and feel of your content. For example, if you use a happy, trendy song on a video that has a sad mood, it seems unprofessional and may not give you the desired results. 
### Edit it like a pro
You don’t need to be a professional editor to create engaging videos; however, by paying attention to small details, your Reels get more interactions. Keep this tip in mind, especially if you want to share a lipsync video with a trending song.
Make sure your video’s timing and your performance are synced to the audio. 
### Use relevant hashtags
Using relevant hashtags for a trending challenge or song in your Instagram Reels increases your account’s reach and shows it to a target audience.
