# Who’s Who in Future of Work? Top 50 Influencers to Follow
### by | Jan 24, 2023
By definition, Future of Work is a projection of how work, workers and the workplace will continue to evolve. While a lot of the focus has been placed on developing technology (like AI, for example), other factors such as the gig economy and remote working are playing a big role in the way the working world is developing.
This report highlights the Top 50 influencers who are driving the most engagement on the topic of Future of Work across LinkedIn & Twitter. There’s a variety of different influencer personas you can discover in this list ranging from Academics & Researchers, Event Speakers, Journalists & Contributors to Content Creators, and more!
