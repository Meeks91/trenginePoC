## 🌟 How to Use Mid-Journey to Create AI Art
### 1. Joining Mid-Journey Beta Group
To access Mid-Journey, you'll first need to join their beta group. Simply click on the provided link and follow the instructions to create your account. Once you're logged in, navigate to the Discord platform by clicking on the designated button.
### 2. Accessing Mid-Journey on Discord
On the left-[HAND](https://www.toolify.ai/tool/hand) side of the Discord interface, you'll find various rooms to join. It doesn't matter which one you choose, as they all serve the same purpose. By joining one of these newbie rooms, you'll gain access to other users' images and creations.
### 3. Using Mid-Journey to Create Images
To create your first image, [Type](https://www.toolify.ai/tool/type-ai) "/imagine" in the chat box and select a prompt from the list provided. The prompt should reflect the kind of image you want to create. You can include specific keywords related to your desired image, such as "futuristic city spacecraft robots colorful." Add "--v4 --aspect ratio 3:2" to ensure you're using the latest version of Mid-Journey.
### 4. Upscaling Images with Mid-Journey
Once the image is generated, Mid-Journey will provide you with several variations to choose from. You can select the one that best suits your needs. If you want to upscale the image for better quality, simply click on the "U2" option and wait for the process to complete.

## 🔍 Reverse Engineering Images with Clip Interrogator
### 1. Taking Screenshots of Inspirational Images
If you come across an image that you adore and want to recreate, simply take a screenshot of it. Save the image to your desktop or desired location.
### 2. Using Clip Interrogator to Generate Prompts
Clip Interrogator is a free tool that helps reverse engineer images by providing prompts. Open the tool and upload the image you captured. Clip Interrogator will scan the image and generate a prompt that you can use with Mid-Journey. Copy the prompt for further use.
### 3. Creating Variation of Images with Mid-Journey
Return to Mid-Journey on Discord and enter the "/imagine" command. Paste the prompt generated by Clip Interrogator. Mid-Journey will then create a variation of the image you provided, allowing you to recreate and modify it according to your preferences.
