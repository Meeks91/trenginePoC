Since the end of 2022, the sensational arrival of OpenAI's ChatGPT has transformed the technological landscape, arousing both admiration and concern. However, despite the impressive innovations, AI is not new to social media. Sophisticated algorithms have long been analysing user behaviour to personalise feeds and promote the most relevant content.
