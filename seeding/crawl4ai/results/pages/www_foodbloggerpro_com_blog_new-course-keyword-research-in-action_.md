We have an exciting update for ya from FBP HQ today: we have a _brand new course_ all about a topic that we get _tons_ of questions about…
[Keyword research](https://www.foodbloggerpro.com/courses/building-traffic/keyword-research/)!
We are _so_ incredibly excited to have this course available for our Food Blogger Pro members today. It’s a bit different from our other courses because it will give you _an inside look_ into how three different people do keyword research for their businesses.
You’ll essentially watch “over the shoulder” of these three people as they do keyword research for their respective businesses/bands:
  * Aleka from [Keywords with Aleka](https://keywordswithaleka.teachable.com/)
  * Jenna from [Pinch of Yum](https://www.pinchofyum.com)
  * Emily from Food Blogger Pro (_that’s us!_)


In this course, you’ll learn:
  * How to use [KeySearch](https://www.keysearch.co/) and [Ahrefs](https://ahrefs.com/) for keyword research
  * How to keep user intent in mind when doing keyword research
  * What volume and keyword scores you may want to target
  * Why search volume doesn’t necessarily equal expected website traffic
  * And more!


Plus! You’ll get to download our **Keyword Research Glossary** as a little “cheat sheet” for you as you watch through the course.
It’s an awesome course, and a big thanks goes to our fabulous Digital Content Manager, Emily, for spearheading this course! 🎉
