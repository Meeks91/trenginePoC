Instagram can be a fantastic place to share content as a food creator, but it can sometimes feel like a black box of sorts.
Instagram Reels! Images on your feed! Instagram Stories! Story Highlights! Captions! Hashtags! There are _so_ many things to grasp when it comes to this social media platform, and it feels like Instagram is always adding new features for creators to use. 
That’s why we decided to update our existing [Instagram for Food Creators course](https://www.foodbloggerpro.com/courses/social-media/instagram-for-food-bloggers/) with brand-new strategies, tips, and tutorials! 
As you go through this course, you’ll get up-to-date information about what’s new on Instagram, and you’ll gather all of the knowledge you need to create an effective Instagram strategy for your brand. 
In this course, you’ll learn:
  * How to **set up your profile** on Instagram
  * How the Instagram **feed** and **algorithm** work 
  * All of the different ways you can **share content on Instagram**
  * How **engagement** works on Instagram 
  * How to **earn money** on Instagram
  * How **Instagram analytics** work 
  * How to **create a rock-solid Instagram strategy** in 2025 (with advice from [Andrea Balogun](https://www.foodbloggerpro.com/experts/andrea-balogun/), our Social Media Strategy Expert) 
  * **And more!**


All in all, this is a really powerful course that will give you the inspiration and confidence you need to create a strong presence on Instagram as a food creator. We hope you enjoy it!
