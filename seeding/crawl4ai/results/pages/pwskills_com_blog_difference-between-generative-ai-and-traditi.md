## **What is Traditional AI?**
Traditional AI is a rule based system or algorithm approach designed to perform a set of specific tasks within the boundary of predefined rules and logic. These systems rely on traditional data structures, algorithm, and human defined programming, deterministic decision making, etc.
We can make algorithms for automated decisions making in controlled scenarios, and predictive models using structured databases, medical diagnosis software, etc.
  * Traditional AI works on a deterministic approach and works under predefined conditions.
  * It is a pre-trained using a supervised machine learning approach.
  * Traditional AI produces consistent outputs based on a given set of inputs.
  * It works on a set of rule based systems and algorithms to derive a conclusion.


## **Pros and Cons of Traditional AI Techniques**
Let us understand some of the most important advantages and disadvantages of using traditional ai technique.
### **Pros:**
  * Traditional AI is based on rules and logic which generates more predictable and reliable content. 
  * Traditional AI works on a deterministic approach to produce more predictable tasks. 
  * It is more transparent and interpretable


### **Cons:**
  * It offers very limited flexibility and adaptability, making them less creative than generative AI.
  * It sometimes becomes difficult to handle the complexity and ambiguity of the application based on predefined algorithms.
  * Traditional AI offers very limited scalability options
  * Traditional AI being constrained by a set of rules and algorithms does not offer much creativity and innovation


## **What is Generative AI?**
Generative AI is an improved version of traditional AI which is not limited by ruled based functioning or algorithms. It is a rapidly evolving branch of artificial intelligence focused on creating new content with minimum human intervention. It is capable of creating new content from text, images, audio, video, etc based on pre-trained data. 
  * Generative AI models are trained using a non-supervised approach.
  * It uses deep learning methods to predict unforeseen outputs capable of producing highly varying results every time, even on the same input.
  * It can learn from data and solve complex patterns more accurately. 
  * It offers less interpretability and more adaptability.


## **Pros and Cons of Generative AI Techniques**
Let us understand some of the most important advantages and disadvantages of using generative ai techniques. 
### **Pros:**
  * Generative AI offers more versatility and adaptability.
  * Easy to make new and creative content for generative ideas, artworks, music, poetries, solutions, coding, etc.
  * Provide more data driven insights to uncover complex patterns added to provide more informed decision making.
  * Generative AI provides more human like content generation


### **Cons:**
  * Less predictable which can lead to potential unauthorised access, fairness concerns, discrimination, etc.
  * Lack of transparency and interpretability
  * It can have more potential for misuse if not monitored properly
  * It offers more ethical and legal challenges


## **Traditional AI vs Generative AI: Common Techniques**
The major difference between Generative AI and traditional AI lies in their approach and techniques used.
### **Traditional AI Techniques & Approach**
It uses a definite set of deterministic rules based algorithms to solve a set of problems and structured tasks. It only automates a set of specific tasks meant for a set of tasks. Traditional AI relies on structured or labeled datasets. 
It was the first step in the world of artificial intelligence meant to generate specific outputs for specific inputs i,e. Deterministic approach. For example, personalised recommendation systems, spam detection, customer support automation, etc.
### **Generative AI Techniques & Approach**
Generative AI utilises [deep learning](https://pwskills.com/blog/deep-learning-in-ai-how-it-works-whos-using-it-why-is-it-important/) and reinforced learning approaches. It works on creating new data by utilizing large and unstructured datasets. The generative AI models are generally trained on a non-supervised machine learning approach and are hence adaptive and flexible.
Generative AI is an improvement in traditional AI with the scope of improvements in content creation, [chatbots](https://pwskills.com/blog/chatbot-ai/), etc. For example, ChatGPT, DALL-E, MidJourney, etc
## **Traditional AI vs Generative AI: Common Examples**
Traditional AI came first in the field of artificial intelligence and to comply with the principles and possibilities of Artificial intelligence, generative AI replaced the position. Check some of the important examples of both these technologies below.
### **Traditional AI Examples**
Earlier [Artificial intelligence](https://pwskills.com/blog/what-is-artificial-intelligence/) was based only on providing a set of specific rule based systems and personalised recommendation systems.
  * **Spam Filters:** Identify and block spam emails using predefined rules or machine learning algorithms trained on labelled data.
  * **Netflix or Amazon Recommendations:** Suggest movies, shows, or products based on historical user behaviour and collaborative filtering techniques.
  * **Basic AI chatbots:** It use predefined scripts and decision trees to respond to common customer queries.
  * **Automation** : It is used in automatic robot assembly in manufacturing companies.
  * **Diagnostic Systems** : Healthcare systems and disease diagnosis improved since the availability of traditional AI techniques and methods.


Traditional AI also was capable of determining the relevance of web pages based on traditional keywords and could forecast stock prices, and patterns based on the available data. 
### **Generative AI Examples**
The recent advancements in generative AI expanded its capabilities and features. It can create new content ideas, generate human-like responses, and use descriptions to create multimedia images, videos, etc.
  * **ChatGPT:** Generates human-like responses to prompts, assists with creative writing, and answers questions dynamically.
  * **DALL-E:** Creates images from text descriptions, enabling artistic and design innovations.
  * **DeepArt:** Generates art styles by applying neural network transformations.
  * **Simulations** : Generates real world simulations for research and training fields. 


Generative AI requires less human intervention and can create a diverse set of output. It can detect patterns, compose music, and create synthetic media content ideas.
