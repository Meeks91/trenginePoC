### About Connecting Research
The Connecting Research blog showcases the breadth and richness of research at the [University of Reading](https://www.reading.ac.uk/). Here you can find short and accessible reads on topics across our research themes of Agriculture, Food & Health, Environment, Heritage & Creativity, and Prosperity & Resilience. In these blogs, researchers at all stages of their careers offer evidence-based commentary and analysis on topical issues and societal problems, explore the contexts and collaborations which inform their work, and explain the impact and benefits of their research.
**Are you a Reading researcher and interested in writing a blog? Contact the Research Communications & Engagement team at research@reading.ac.uk.**
