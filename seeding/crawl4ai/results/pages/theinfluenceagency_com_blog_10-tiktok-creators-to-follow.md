# 10 TikTok Creators You Need To Follow Right Now
Last Updated
May 17, 2022
Originally Published
June 26, 2020
[ Written by Tanya Cruz Director of Communications ](https://theinfluenceagency.com/author/tanya-cruz)
There’s no denying that [TikTok](https://theinfluenceagency.com/services/tiktok-marketing) is taking the world of social media by storm. It's breaking user growth records as we know it as the ultimate platform for never-ending, bite-sized video content that’s showing no signs of slowing down. As of November 2019, TikTok has reached over [1.5 billion downloads](https://www.businessinsider.com/tiktok-hits-15-billion-downloads-outperforming-instagram-2019-11) across the App Store and Google Play. Moreover, it was the third most downloaded app only behind WhatsApp and Messenger, solidifying themselves as one of the top social media platforms in the world rivalling Facebook and Instagram as we know it.
With all the attention TikTok has been receiving the last few years, brands are certainly beginning to take notice. Between its top-secret algorithm, [high organic reach](https://theinfluenceagency.com/services/seo), and level of engagement, brands are eyeing TikTok as a lucrative avenue to amplify their[ influencer marketing](https://theinfluenceagency.com/services/full-service-influencer-marketing) efforts and [social media](https://theinfluenceagency.com/services/social-media-marketing) campaigns. It's easy to see why they want to take advantage of the vast networks that TikTok creators have carved out for themselves.
TikTok offers a wide roster of easy-to-use tools for content creation. The availability of music, effects, and sound bites put creativity back into the hands of the creators who have skyrocketed to fame with their unique, entertaining, and innovative content.
We’ve compiled a list of some of our current favourite TikTok creators on the emerging platform. Whether you enjoy comedy skits, dance challenges, recipes, beauty, [fashion](https://theinfluenceagency.com/influencer-marketing/fashion), travel, or [lifestyle content](https://theinfluenceagency.com/influencer-marketing/lifestyle), TikTok has something for you. Keep on reading to see who we just can’t get enough of right now!
