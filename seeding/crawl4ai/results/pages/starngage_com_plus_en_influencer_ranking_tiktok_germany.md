Germany has some of Europe's strictest disclosure requirements for influencer marketing, making compliance essential for both brands and creators:
  * **Mandatory Disclosure:** Any paid or incentivized content must be clearly marked as advertising ("Werbung" or "Anzeige") at the beginning of posts
  * **Placement Requirements:** Disclosure must be immediately visible without requiring user interaction (no hiding in hashtags)
  * **Broad Definition:** German courts consider even gifted products as requiring disclosure
  * **Significant Penalties:** Violations can result in fines up to €500,000 and legal actions from competitors
  * **Editorial Freedom:** Recent rulings require disclosure only for direct commercial influence, not when sharing genuine opinions


Working with knowledgeable German legal experts before campaign launch is strongly recommended, as regulations continue to evolve through court precedents.
