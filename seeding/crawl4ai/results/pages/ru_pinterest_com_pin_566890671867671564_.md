Favorite thoracic spine mobilization with a yoga block! 💥 Here’s how it goes: Lie on your back with your knees bent and feet flat on the floor. Bring your arms into T arms with a yoga block in your right hand. Then reach your yoga block across your body and deliver it to your left hand. In order to execute this task, your arm will pull your shoulder blade and then your rib cage up off the floor, rolling your upper and mid back to the left.
