### 1. [Modash](https://www.modash.io/)
Modash is a powerful **influencer marketing platform designed for brands of all sizes,** offering a comprehensive suite of tools to find, analyze, and manage influencers at scale. It simplifies the entire campaign workflow, from discovery to payment and reporting.
With a database covering every creator on Instagram, **YouTube, and TikTok** with over 1,000 followers, Modash ensures you can find the perfect partners for your campaigns, making it a valuable asset for [enhancing marketing effectiveness](https://aimojo.io/ai-tools-digital-marketing/) across various industries.
**Key Features:**
**Extensive Influencer Database:** Lists every creator on Earth with over 1,000 followers on major platforms.
**Advanced Search Filters:** Find influencers by location, follower count, engagement rates, and audience data.
**In-Depth Profile Analysis:** Check for fake followers, audience demographics, and engagement rate accuracy.
**Automated Content Monitoring:** Automatically tracks every post from your influencers in a single dashboard.
**Best For** Modash is ideal for brands and agencies that need a scalable solution for global influencer discovery and management. Its massive database and detailed analytics make it particularly useful for companies targeting [niche](https://www.shopify.com/blog/niche-markets) audiences or running campaigns in multiple countries.
**How It Works?** The platform works by crawling and indexing publicly available social media data, similar to how a search engine operates. This allows it to offer an extensive and up-to-date database.
Users can apply various filters to pinpoint suitable creators, analyze their profiles for authenticity and audience alignment, and then monitor all campaign content automatically through a centralized dashboard.
