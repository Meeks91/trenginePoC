Businesses can find success when they have others to back them and support them; to show some belief in what they’re trying to achieve. John Hills, Head of Marketing dives into how the company has experienced significant growth due to the company’s marketplace model, where companies looking to raise funds and investors looking to invest in those companies are the supply and demand sides of the marketplace, respectively. Their marketing efforts are focused on educating companies on the benefits of equity crowdfunding, and they use these companies to acquire more investors.
