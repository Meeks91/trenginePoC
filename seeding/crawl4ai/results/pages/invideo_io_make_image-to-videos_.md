## Learn more on Image to Video AI Tool
What is image to video AI generator? 
An AI image to video generator is a tool that transforms static photos into animated videos using artificial intelligence. Instead of manually creating animations frame-by-frame or learning complex video editing software, you simply upload a photo and the invideo automatically adds motion, camera movements, and cinematic effects using AI. AI video generator from image on invideo works by analyzing your image's composition, detecting objects and depth, then applying intelligent animation that makes your photo come alive. All Image to video models on invideo are trained legitimately on licensed and public data. 
Does AI image to video really work without requiring editing skills? 
Yes, our AI photo to video tool is completely automated and you don't need any editing skills to use it. Invideo handles scene transitions, background music matching, and professional effects. Image to video generations are commercially safe to use.
How fast do AI tools generate videos from images? 
Our AI image-to-video generates video within 60 seconds.
What's the video quality like from AI image generators? 
The video quality from AI image generators is Full HD, optimized for social media platforms like Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Facebook, and LinkedIn.
How can I transform an image to video? 
Start by uploading your photo in any common format like JPG, PNG, or WEBP. Next, write a text prompt describing what you want in the video and how you want your image to move, whether that's a smooth zoom, cinematic pan, or any other motion style you are thinking of. Then select your preferred video duration (up to 8 seconds) and choose the aspect ratio that matches your target platform: 16:9 for YouTube, 9:16 for TikTok, Instagram, and Youtube Shorts. Invideo analyzes your image, interprets your prompt, and automatically generates a professional-quality animated video in just minutes.
Can I regenerate if I don't like the first result? 
Yes - It will consume 2 credits per generation and a different AI interpretation for unique results every time.
Where do I enter prompts for image generation? 
Simple prompt box right below your uploaded image:Example Prompts That Work:"Zoom in slowly with gentle camera movement""Make the clouds move and birds fly across""Add falling snow with soft wind effect""Pan across the image from left to right""Create dynamic lighting changes like sunset"
