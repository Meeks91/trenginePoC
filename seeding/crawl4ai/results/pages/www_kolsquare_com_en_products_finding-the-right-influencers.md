Instagram’s Explore Page is designed to show users content tailored to their interests, making it a useful tool for finding influencers. To leverage this feature, create an Instagram account that mirrors your target audience’s behavior. For example, if you’re a wellness brand targeting users aged 25-35, engage with wellness-focused content and follow relevant creators to influence Instagram’s recommendations.

**2. Search industry hashtags or keywords**
Instagram’s hashtag search is a direct route to finding creators in your niche. Use relevant industry hashtags to locate influencers who are already active in your market. For example, searching “#cleanbeauty” or “#wellnesscoach” can help you identify creators with similar content and follower demographics.
