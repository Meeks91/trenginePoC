### Frequently Asked Questions
#### How accurate is this TikTok username checker?
Our tool opens the actual TikTok profile page, giving you real-time accuracy. Always verify availability directly on TikTok before creating your account.
#### Can I reserve a TikTok username for later?
TikTok doesn't allow username reservations. Once you find an available username, create your account immediately to secure it.
#### What should I do if my brand name is taken on TikTok?
Try adding "official", "hq", or your location. For trademark issues, TikTok has policies to help legitimate businesses reclaim usernames.
#### Can I change my TikTok username later?
Yes, TikTok allows username changes every 30 days. However, choose carefully as frequent changes can confuse your audience and hurt discoverability.
#### Should I use the same username across all social platforms?
Yes, consistent usernames across platforms help build brand recognition and make it easier for your audience to find you everywhere.
