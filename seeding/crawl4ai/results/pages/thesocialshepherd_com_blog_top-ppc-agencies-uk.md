## What Makes a Top PPC Agency?
We’re not offering a random list of PPC agencies that we picked from a quick Google Search. That defeats the purpose of helping you find professionals who can deliver and help you hit your business goals.
Whether you are a multi-million brand or a small business testing the waters, you need to set your goals before running PPC campaigns or hiring a PPC agency to help you out.
For example, do you want to get more leads? Are you hoping to sell more of a particular product from your catalogue? Or perhaps you want to increase traffic to one of your landing pages?
When you know what you want to achieve, you have a good foundation for collaborating with a reputable PPC agency online.
Here are some things to look for when hiring a PPC agency:
  * **They Understand Data and Know How to Use It:** Whatever data you gather during a retail ad campaign is a treasure trove. Using data, you can better understand how your customers are behaving. You will know what products they want and what products they ignore. A good PPC agency will help you adjust your campaigns to improve your ROI and capitalise on data so you can convert more leads.
  * **Excellent Keyword Research Strategy:** Knowing how keywords work is the lifeblood of PPC campaigns. Ask an agency how they decide what keywords to use, what research tools they use, and how they know if a particular keyword is performing well. If they have a good grip on keyword research, there’s a good chance they can help you achieve your goals.
  * **Know Local Search and Mobile Search Like the Backs of Their Hands:** If you aim to drive foot traffic to your store, doing well in local search is a must. When you are in the process of shortlisting PPC agencies, find out how they can help you reach out to a specific community or how they can help you achieve your local search objectives. Likewise, mastery of [mobile search](https://www.webfx.com/internet-marketing/mobile-marketing-statistics.html) is essential since 52% of website traffic comes from smartphones, and about 61% of consumers say that they are likely to buy from mobile-friendly websites.
  * **Communication and Collaboration:** Simplify the process and have a specific point of contact at a PPC agency. Observe how quickly they can answer your queries, as this gives you an idea of their work process and organisation. Are they proactive in giving you suggestions on improving your website traffic or perhaps even improving your ad copy? These small details count because they form part of the bigger picture of your brand’s success.
