##  How to Use This Micro Influencer List
###  For Brands & Marketers
**Step 1: Identify Your Niche**
  * Find the category that matches your product/service
  * Review all creators in that category
  * Look for audience alignment, not just follower count


**Step 2: Vet Each Influencer**
  * Visit their profile
  * Check recent engagement (likes, comments, saves)
  * Read comments for quality (real engagement vs. bots)
  * Review their previous brand partnerships


**Step 3: Check Audience Fit**
  * Do their followers match your target demographic?
  * Is their content style aligned with your brand?
  * Do they share your brand values?


**Step 4: Prioritize by Engagement Rate**
  * 10%+ = Exceptional (prioritize these)
  * 7-9% = Very good (strong partnerships)
  * 5-6% = Good (still worth reaching out)
  * 3-4% = Acceptable (proceed with caution)


**Step 5: Reach Out**
  * Personalize your outreach (mention specific posts)
  * Be clear about compensation
  * Propose collaboration ideas
  * Ask for their media kit


###  For Aspiring Influencers
**How to get ON this list:**
**Build engaged community (not just followers):**
  * Focus on 1 specific niche
  * Post consistently (minimum 3x/week) — scheduling tools like [SocialRails](https://socialrails.com/) make this easier by letting you batch-create and schedule posts in advance
  * Respond to every comment
  * Create valuable, helpful content
  * Show up in Stories daily


**Improve your engagement rate:**
  * Ask questions in captions
  * Use polls and interactive stickers
  * Create shareable content
  * Post when your audience is active
  * Use strong calls-to-action


**Attract brand partnerships:**
  * Work with 1-2 small brands first (build portfolio)
  * Create professional media kit
  * Include "work with me" in bio
  * Share UGC (user-generated style) content
  * Be open about partnerships (brands notice this)


**Timeline to 10K+ followers:**
  * Months 1-3: 0 → 1K (niche clarity, consistency)
  * Months 4-6: 1K → 3K (content refinement)
  * Months 7-9: 3K → 6K (community engagement)
  * Months 10-12: 6K → 10K+ (momentum kicks in)

##  Red Flags to Avoid
**When vetting influencers from this (or any) list:**
❌ **Fake followers:**
  * Check follower growth (sudden spikes = purchased followers)
  * Use tools: HypeAuditor, IG Audit
  * Look for engagement-to-follower ratio (if 50K followers but 200 likes = suspicious)


❌ **Engagement pods/bots:**
  * Read comments (generic "nice pic!" = bots)
  * Check if same accounts comment on everything
  * Look for meaningful conversations


❌ **Inconsistent content:**
  * Erratic posting (nothing for weeks, then 10 posts in a day)
  * Constantly changing niches (no clear audience)
  * Overly promotional (every post is a sponsored ad)


❌ **Controversy/brand safety issues:**
  * Google their username + "controversy"
  * Check past brand partnerships (any scandals?)
  * Review their values alignment with yours


**Trust your gut.** If something feels off, it probably is.
