## FAQs
How do you shortlist creators for my brand?
We filter by category fit, audience location, language, brand safety, past performance, and creative style. You review & approve the final list.
What if the campaign under-delivers?
We actively optimize mid-flight (timings, hooks, thumbnails) and can re-post/boost best performers. Final report includes learnings & next steps.
Can we focus on specific regions or languages?
Yes. We can target creators by city/region and run multi-language content as needed.
What are the payment terms?
Standard: 50% to kick off, 50% on delivery. GST invoices provided. Custom terms available on Enterprise.
Do prices include ad spend?
No. Package prices cover creators and execution. Paid media budgets are separate; you can add Ad Rights to amplify best posts.
