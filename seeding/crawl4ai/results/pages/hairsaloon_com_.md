## About
While today’s world is filled with numerous clip joints and fancy salons, Hair Saloon is building something better. 
Since 1997, The Hair Saloon has been helping to recapture this tradition. We’re filling a void in American life that has as much to do with the restoration of men as it does with the business of haircutting. We call it an Honorable Rebellion Among Men®.

## Services
Men Deserve Better®, that’s why every haircut comes with on-the-house amenities. 
Every perfect haircut service comes with a complimentary beverage, complimentary shampoo, complimentary hot towel and mint to finish, and a complimentary shoeshine.
Prices vary by location. Check out [your saloon’s page](https://hairsaloon.com/locations) for more details. 
#### Classic Men's Cut
Includes on-the-house amenities and a perfect haircut 
#### Premium Men's Cut
The Classic _plus_ a therapeutic scalp massage 
#### Executive Men's Cut
The Premium plus a neck shave, an extended therapeutic scalp massage, and an extended stress-relieving neck and shoulder massage 
#### Jr. Men's Cut
Includes on-the-house amenities (minus the shampoo) and a perfect haircut for the young men 18 and under 
#### Traditional Straight Razor Face or Head Shave
Includes on-the-house amenities, consultation, hot towel treatment, pre-shave oil application, Saloon shave cream application, razor shave and after-shave balm ﬁnish _By appointment only with a certified PHD barber_
#### Beard Trim and Beard Lining
Includes on-the-house amenities, consultation, hot towel treatment, pre-shave oil application, Saloon shave cream application, razor shave and after-shave balm finish _By appointment only with a certified PHD barber_
