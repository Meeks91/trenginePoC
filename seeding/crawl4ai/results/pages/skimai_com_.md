## Frequently Asked Questions
### How is AI used in industries?
AI is utilized across various industries to help automate processes, gain insights from data, enhance decision-making, and improve overall business efficiency. AI learns from the data you feed it, so, with the right AI model and data set any industry can benefit.
### What is the impact of AI on industries?
AI is able to streamline business operations and automate mundane processes. AI can also run in-depth analyses, read, assess, and summarize massive amounts of data. AI is enabling all industries to grow more efficiently and effectively—making them more profitable at a lower lift.
### What are the 4 types of AI?
There are four types of AI: reactive machines, limited memory, theory of mind, and self-aware AI. Reactive machines have low-to-no memory and have no understanding outside of immediate context. Limited memory AI can retain a restricted amount of learning to improve actions. The theory of mind understands how a mind should work & can mimic that. Self-awareness is the highest level of intelligence & is able to possess close to a human level of consciousness.
### What types of industries can AI be used in?
AI can be used in all industries. A few industries using AI the most today are: food and beverage, EdTech, MedTech, Legal, Content, New Media, Photo and Film, Hospitality, PR, and Due Diligence industries.
