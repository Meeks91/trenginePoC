## Frequently asked questions
### How to find influencers for my brand?
Use Popular Pays’ influencer search tool to find the influencer that’s the perfect fit for your campaign.
### Can I find influencers in a specific niche or industry?
Yes! Our search tool allows you to specify exactly what you are looking for, and offers the ability to search for influencers based off the smallest of details.
### Can I get influencers to promote my product in other countries?
Currently, our search tool only provides solutions for US based creators and influencers.
### What are the minimum followers that Popular Pays influencers have?
You can easily find both macro and micro influencers on our platform. The minimum follow number to be eligible however is 10K.
### On which social media platforms can influencers promote my campaign?
We offer a range of platform options, with the main channels being TikTok, Facebook, Instagram and YouTube.
### How does the influencer Lookalike tool work?
Type in the Popular Pays creator's handle and our algorithms will work their magic to return more creators in the Popular Pays community that match key characteristics.
### What are the pricing options for using the influencer search platform?
Pricing can vary depending on many factors. Check out our pricing plan page or set up a call with one of our team members.
