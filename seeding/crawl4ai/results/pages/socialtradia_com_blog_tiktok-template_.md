## What is a TikTok template?
One of the best features of TikTok for video marketing is that you can utilize the platform’s templates to create your videos.
A TikTok video template is like a structure you can customize with your own videos and filters. This is a great chance to save time and include attractive visual basics that have already appealed to many users.
The main goal is to simplify the video creation process on TikTok and help content creators, especially those new to TikTok. If you’re a beginner, you might be concerned about the style and the template of your TikTok videos, so it’s great for starting from scratch.
Of course, professional users also use templates as they’re really helpful in reducing the time spent on some details. In fact, anyone struggling to develop ideas for content creation and preparing the basics can benefit from using templates.
If you use them properly, your video might even appear on TikTok’s For You Page. Generally, getting on this page helps your content be recommended to users who are highly likely to enjoy it. As a result, the chance of receiving likes, shares, or comments for your TikTok videos.
Another advantage of using a **TikTok template** is that they are a great approach to reducing the risk of being the target of TikTok’s content censorship.

## How to use a free TikTok template to create a video
Making a [TikTok video](https://socialtradia.com/blog/best-time-to-post-on-tiktok/) using a free template is really straightforward. Here’s how:
  1. Open your TikTok page on your smartphone.
  2. Click on “Post” at the bottom of your screen.
  3. Click on “Templates” at the bottom-right corner.
  4. Swipe left to see available templates on TikTok.
  5. Check the name of each template at the top of it. 
  6. Check the number of images you have to upload for each template.
  7. Tap “Select photos” after finding your exciting template.
  8. Browse and select the pictures you want to add from your gallery.
  9. Click on “Ok” after selecting all the required photos.
  10. Check the preview of your TikTok video in the editor.
  11. Use the tools TikTok provides you with at the right of your screen to increase the aesthetic aspects of your TikTok video.
  12. Tap “Next.”


You’ll then be taken to the “Post” window.
  1. Add your favorite caption and hashtags.
  2. Tag your friend or any other TikTok account.
  3. Preview your YikTok video.
  4. Select a cover for your video.
  5. Add text to your cover.
  6. Tap “Save” to get back to the editor page, and tap “Post.”

Vimeo’s search features are quite advanced compared to many other platforms available out there. You just need to enter a term like “birthday,” which recommends the best TikTok template for videos.
Additionally, it has Guided Templates features. If you want to take it this way, you will need to provide answers to a few simple questions so that it can build a TikTok video for you that you can then modify as you wish.

Most of these free templates are for advertising sales, making them a great choice for marketers. Therefore, the next time you need to publicize and advertise a deal, be sure to go through Clipchamp’s collection.

### Canva
Canva is a well-known tool in the realm of graphic design. They provide more than 3,000 templates, particularly for TikTok videos. Fortunately, more than 500 templates are free, making it a great place for TikTok marketers.
