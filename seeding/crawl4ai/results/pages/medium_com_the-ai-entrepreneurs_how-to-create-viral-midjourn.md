## Step-by-Step: Make Videos in Midjourney
  1. **Pick Your Image**


  * Create a bold image in Midjourney via Discord or the web app.
  * Or upload a high-res image you’ve got ready.


2. **Hit the Web App**
  * Go to [midjourney.com/imagine](https://midjourney.com/imagine).
  * Log in with Discord (“Continue with Discord”).


3. **Upload & Lock Image**
  * Click the image icon in the “Imagine” panel.
  * Upload or select an image from your Midjourney gallery.
  * Tap the “lock” icon to reuse it for different video vibes.


4. **Animate It**
  * Click “Animate Image” and pick:
  * Auto: Smooth, no-fuss animation.
  * Manual: Add a custom prompt for unique motion.
  * Output: A 5-second video (e.g., 624x624px for square or 832x464px for widescreen).


**5. Choose Motion Style**
  * — motion low: Gentle zooms or pans for a clean, dreamy feel.
  * — motion high: Bold sweeps or fast action for high-energy impact.


Example: A glowing forest with — motion low sways softly, while a neon racer with — motion high zooms with flair.
