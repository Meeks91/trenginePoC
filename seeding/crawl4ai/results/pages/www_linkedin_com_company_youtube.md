##  About us 
YouTube is a team-oriented, creative workplace where every single employee has a voice in the choices we make and the features we implement. We work together in small teams to design, develop, and roll out key features and products in very short time frames. Which means something you write today could be seen by millions of viewers tomorrow. And even though we’re a part of Google and enjoy all of the corporate perks, it still feels like you’re working at a startup. The future looks brighter than ever ▶️
As we enter 2026, the lines between creativity and technology are blurring, sparking a new era of innovation. This inflection point requires ambitious bets, and [YouTube](https://www.linkedin.com/company/youtube?trk=organization_guest_main-feed-card_reshare-text) has the scale, community, and technological investments to lead the creative industry into this next era. Here’s what we’ll focus on in 2026: 🌟 Reinventing Entertainment: Creators are the new stars & studios YouTubers are reinventing entertainment and building the media companies of the future, whether they’re on the Super Bowl sideline or bringing Taylor Swift fans together for a deep-dive on her latest album. People come to YouTube for this unmatched breadth of content and we’re seeing huge growth across all formats and screens. 💛 Building the best place for kids and teens Kids and teens around the world rely on YouTube every day to learn and connect. Our goal is to ensure YouTube remains a place for exploration while empowering parents to guide the journey. As one example, this year, we’ll introduce the ability for parents to control how much time their kids and teens spend scrolling Shorts. 💰 Powering the creator economy YouTube remains the original and largest creator economy. Creators call YouTube their home because we offer the most reliable and stable path to earn. In the past four years we’ve paid over $100 billion to creators, artists, and media companies. We’ll continue to invest in different ways for creators to earn. With over 500,000 creators already in YouTube Shopping, we’re focused on making the shopping experience seamless. We’re also focused on brand deals and making it easier for Influencer Marketing Agencies and brands to find and hire creators, and execute successful campaigns through our creator partnerships hub. ✨ Supercharging and safeguarding creativity For years, AI has been the quiet engine behind our most important innovations and we’ll build on this momentum in 2026. In December, more than 1M channels used our AI creation tools daily, and this year we’ll expand these capabilities. AI is also transforming the viewing experience: in December, more than 20M users relied on our Ask tool to learn more about the content they watched. As these capabilities grow, so does our commitment to ensuring this technology is used transparently and responsibly. We clearly label content created by YouTube’s AI products, and creators must disclose when they've created realistic altered or synthetic content. We’re also building on the foundation of Content ID, a system our partners have trusted for well over a decade, to equip creators with new tools to manage the use of their likeness in AI-generated content. To reduce the spread of low quality AI content, we’re actively building on our established systems that have been very successful in combatting spam and clickbait, and reducing the spread of low quality, repetitive content.
