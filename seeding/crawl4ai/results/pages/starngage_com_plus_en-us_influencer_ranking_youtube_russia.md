### Russian Influencer Marketing Regulations and Compliance
When working with Russian influencers, compliance with local regulations is essential. The Russian advertising law (Federal Law No. 38-FZ "On Advertising") and subsequent amendments govern influencer marketing activities:
  * **Disclosure Requirements:** All sponsored content must be clearly labeled (typically with hashtags like #реклама or #ad)
  * **Prohibited Products:** Strict regulations on promoting alcohol, tobacco, pharmaceuticals, and financial services
  * **Minor Protection:** Special restrictions apply when the audience includes significant numbers of viewers under 18
  * **Federal Antimonopoly Service:** This agency monitors advertising compliance and can impose substantial fines
  * **Platform-Specific Rules:** VK, Telegram, and other Russian platforms have their own advertising policies to follow


**Important Note:** As regulations frequently change, especially regarding digital advertising, working with locals who understand the current regulatory landscape is highly recommended. Non-compliance penalties can range from 100,000 to 500,000 rubles for organizations.
