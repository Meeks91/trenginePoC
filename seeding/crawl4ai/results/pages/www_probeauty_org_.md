#  PRO BEAUTY PULSE
PBA has partnered with our Business Member, Pivot Point International, to launch a quick monthly check-in to better understand what’s really happening across the beauty industry. The Pro Beauty Pulse is a simple, real-time snapshot of how schools, salons, and professionals are doing. What we learn will be shared with the broader professional beauty community to help spark ideas, shape decisions, and move our industry forward. Your perspective matters—whether you’re seeing growth, facing challenges, or somewhere in between. The survey takes just 2 minutes, and your input will help guide real support for everyone.

#  TAX FAIRNESS SECURED
PBA delivered one of the biggest legislative wins in beauty history—the new 45B FICA Tip Tax Credit. This long-overdue change gives salon, spa, and barbershop owners real tax relief on tipped income. A huge step forward for fairness, and a powerful result of PBA’s unrelenting advocacy.
