## Unveiling the Top 10 Beauty Titans Reigning TikTok in the USA
  * ### Addison Rae (@addisonre)


With a staggering 88.6 million followers (updated as of Mar 2024), [Addison Rae](https://starngage.com/plus/en/influencers/tiktok/addisonre)'s TikTok domain is a treasure trove of beauty revelations. From skincare secrets to makeup mastery, Addison's charismatic presence makes her an indispensable ally for brands looking to captivate the beauty community.
  * ### Kylie Jenner (@jamescharles)


Known for her impeccable glam transformations and expert makeup advice, [Kylie Jenner](https://starngage.com/plus/en/influencers/tiktok/kyliejenner)'s TikTok presence is a beacon of beauty inspiration. With her captivating content, she entertains and enlightens her vast audience, solidifying her position as one of the foremost beauty influencers on TikTok.
  * ### James Charles (@jamescharles)


Enter the realm of makeup sorcery with [James Charles](https://starngage.com/plus/en/influencers/tiktok/jamescharles), a maestro with 38.5 million followers (updated as of Mar 2024) under his spell. His transformative tutorials and avant-garde creations have solidified his status as a beauty luminary, offering brands an unrivalled platform to showcase their products.
  * ### Brooke Ashley Hall (@brookeashleyhall)


Prepare to be enchanted by [Brooke Ashley Hall](https://starngage.com/plus/en/influencers/tiktok/brookeashleyhall), a charismatic virtuoso boasting 14.3 million followers (updated as of Mar 2024). Her magnetic personality and unparalleled makeup prowess make her a beacon of creativity, offering brands a gateway to the hearts of beauty aficionados.
  * ### Nikita Dragun (@nikitadragun)


Fearless, fierce, and fabulously talented, [Nikita Dragun](https://starngage.com/plus/en/influencers/tiktok/nikitadragun) commands the attention of 14.2 million followers (updated as of Mar 2024) on TikTok. With her audacious beauty ventures and unapologetic attitude, Nikita serves as an inspiration for brands seeking to make a bold statement in the beauty arena.
  * ### Emmy Combs (@emmycombss)


Enter a realm of edgy elegance with [Emmy Combs](https://starngage.com/plus/en/influencers/tiktok/emmycombss), a rising star with 10.1 million followers (updated as of Mar 2024) under her command. Her refreshing authenticity and down-to-earth charm make her a standout in the beauty influencer landscape, earning her a well-deserved spot among the top 10 in the USA.
  * ### Sydney Morgan (@sydney_art)


Creativity meets beauty in the delightful world of [Sydney Morgan](https://starngage.com/plus/en/influencers/tiktok/sydney_art), whose creative flair has earned her 10 million followers (updated as of Mar 2024). With her infectious charm and relatable content, Sydney offers brands a unique opportunity to engage with beauty enthusiasts in a light-hearted yet impactful manner.
  * ### Huda Beauty (@hudabeauty)


[Huda Kattan](https://starngage.com/plus/en/influencers/tiktok/hudabeauty)'s larger-than-life personality and killer makeup skills have earned her 9.4 million followers (updated as of Mar 2024) on TikTok. From glamorous transformations to sharing professional makeup tips, her content consistently dazzles and educates her audience, cementing her status as one of the premier beauty influencers and makeup artists (MUAs) on TikTok in the United States.
  * ### Hyram (@hyram)


No compilation of beauty TikTok influencers is comprehensive without a nod to [Hyram](https://starngage.com/plus/en/influencers/tiktok/hyram). While he gained initial fame on YouTube with a viral review of Kylie Skin, it's TikTok where he truly shines. Hyram has single-handedly introduced the platform to the benefits of CeraVe, utilizing the Duets feature to react to celebrity skincare mishaps, users' horror stories, and dissect viral routines. Prepare to be both entertained and enlightened by this skincare guru's feed.
  * ### Charlotte Palermino (@charlotteparler)


[Charlotte Palermino](https://starngage.com/plus/en/influencers/tiktok/charlotteparler)'s passion lies in demystifying skincare science, particularly when it comes to retinol and SPF. If you're keen on delving into the skincare's historical roots, her account offers a wealth of knowledge, all presented with her signature positivity.
