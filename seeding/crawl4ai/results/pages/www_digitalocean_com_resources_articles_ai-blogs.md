##  [What are artificial intelligence (AI) blogs?](https://www.digitalocean.com/resources/articles/ai-blogs#what-are-artificial-intelligence-ai-blogs)
AI blogs are online publications that explore how [artificial intelligence](https://www.digitalocean.com/resources/articles/artificial-general-intelligence-agi), machine learning, and data science are shaping technology, business, and everyday life. They cover practical topics, such as AI tools, [coding assistants](https://www.digitalocean.com/resources/articles/best-ai-coding-assistant), and real-world use cases, as well as bigger picture ideas like ethics, regulation, and industry trends.
Some AI blogs are written for developers and delve into technical details, while others are more approachable for non-technical users, focusing on explaining trends, breaking down news, or sharing hands-on experiences with new products. Additionally, their associated companies can offer career advancement opportunities through courses, job boards, and virtual networking events.
Eager to learn more about foundational AI concepts and tools to use within your everyday work? Visit [The Wave](https://www.digitalocean.com/resources) for resources on AI tooling and product comparisons.
##  [How to find the right AI blog for you](https://www.digitalocean.com/resources/articles/ai-blogs#how-to-find-the-right-ai-blog-for-you)
You can find plenty of AI blogs options to consume, but just because it mentions AI or machine learning as part of its description doesn’t necessarily mean it’s going to have the content you want or deliver the value you need. Here are some questions to ask yourself as you come across AI blogs to follow:
  * **Contributor experience** : For company-run blogs, determine what their content can shed light on in terms of technological developments, product news, tutorials, and AI offerings. If it’s not a blog written by an AI company or vendors, check on how the main contributors and staff are involved within the AI space. Are they developers, AI researchers, journalists, or analysts? Do they actually use the technologies they write about? This helps you evaluate what specific information or commentary the content will provide on current developments in the AI space.
  * **Technical depth** : AI blogs can vary not only in the amount of knowledge required to understand the writing and concepts, but also in the level of detail the content covers. Consider if you prefer quick hits, like a listicle that you can scan in a few minutes to get the information you need, or if you are interested in diving into nitty-gritty technical details, theories, and how tos.
  * **Content frequency** : Some publications share daily updates, while others do so more sparingly. For those who want to stay highly informed, follow a mix of AI blogs that publish content at various frequencies.

##  [Best AI blogs FAQs](https://www.digitalocean.com/resources/articles/ai-blogs#best-ai-blogs-faqs)
**How do I find AI blogs?**
An excellent place to start is the DigitalOcean Community, which offers AI-focused content for all experience levels. You’ll find a wide range of topics and formats, including educational articles, hands-on tutorials, videos, product documentation, plus industry trends and commentary. You can also engage in DigitalOcean’s AI communities on [Discord](https://www.digitalocean.com/resources/articles/ai-discord-servers), X, and LinkedIn to find recommendations from peers.
**What are the best AI blogs to follow?**
For AI blogs that cover a variety of topics, check out the DigitalOcean Community, Towards AI, Hugging Face Community Blog, Towards AI, KDNuggets, and Analytics Vidhya. If you want machine learning specific blogs, there’s Machine Learning Mastery and Towards Data Science. You can also find research on the BAIR Blog, MarkTechPost, and Holistic AI. Company-based blogs include the OpenAI Blog and Anthropic’s Claude Blog.
**Are AI blogs useful for beginners?**
Useful AI beginner blogs include the DigitalOcean Community, Towards AI, the Hugging Face Community Blog, and Machine Learning Mastery. All of these resources offer introductory content on foundational AI concepts and learning materials to enhance your AI knowledge.
**What are some machine learning blogs to follow?**
Top blogs that follow machine learning research include the DigitalOcean Community, Berkeley Artificial Intelligence Research (BAIR) blog, the Hugging Face Community Blog, Towards Data Science, and KDnuggets.
**How often should I follow AI industry blogs?**
The AI and machine learning industries introduce new innovations on a daily basis. While not all AI blogs publish new content daily, if you want to keep informed about new models, technologies, and product news, plan to follow a mix of AI industry blogs that offer daily news updates and others that publish new content less regularly but dive deeper into topics of interest.
