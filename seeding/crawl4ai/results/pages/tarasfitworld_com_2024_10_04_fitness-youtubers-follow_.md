# 21 Best Female Fitness YouTubers to Follow
2024-10-04T21:54:38+00:00
_Hey loves,_
Today’s blog post goes over the **_21 best female fitness YouTubers to follow_**. In this post, I go over what makes these YouTube accounts _special_ and _worth following_. 
I also share my _favorite workouts_ from their channels that can truly help transform your physiques!
I’ve divided this list into two sport _categories_ , one is **strength training** , the other is **Pilates**. 
The YouTubers I have chosen cover a _multitude_ of different topics such as _nutrition_ , _lifestyle_ , _mindset_ and their _daily struggles and challenges_.
Finally, I cover the number of _followers_ these YouTubers have, what they’re _about_ and why you should _follow_ them!
## What is a YouTube influencer? 
A YouTube influencer is a person who _creates content on YouTube_ and has _established a following_ that they **influence** through _inspiring_ , _educating_ or _entertaining_ them.
## Strength training: 
## 1) [Train with Gains by Brains ](https://youtube.com/@trainwithgainsbybrains?si=RqSSSvE-qlQ2cHHq)
### Number of followers: 
799k followers
### About:
Gains by Brains’s real name is Sophie and she is active on _multiple social media platforms_. I first discovered her on Instagram and fell in love with her _training style_ and _workout videos_. 
She has a [fit thick ](https://tarasfitworld.com/2019/11/02/fit-thick-aesthetic/)body type and focuses mainly on building her _lower body_. She does _not_ skip upper body like a lot of other popular influencers. She is also always wearing _trendy workout sets_.
### Why should you follow her?
If you’re a fan of strength training and do not shy away from _heavy weights_ or getting _stronger_ , Sophie has the workouts for you.
Furthermore, her naturally attained fit thick body is a testament to how effective _strength training_ is to build _curves_.
## 2) [The Fitness Sisters- Rachel and Emma Dillon ](https://youtube.com/@thefitnesssisters-rachelem2659?si=zQ0yg0Ieue3HR4t1)
### Number of followers: 
127k followers
### About:
Rachel and Emma Dillon are sisters that are _fitness models_ and _entrepreneurs_. They are also active on _multiple platforms_ and I first discovered them on Instagram.
Their account consists of _“what I eat in a day”_ vlogs, _workouts_ and _challenges_. Their workouts are _amazing_ and target _every_ body part.
These gorgeous women are also WBFF pros and take their _fitness_ and _healthy lifestyles_ quite seriously. They are _forces of nature_ that _inspire_ us to want to be healthy with every one of their posts. 
### Why should you follow them?
If you want to live a healthy lifestyle and look _incredible_ , then you have to follow their YouTube channel! Their workouts are _challenging_ , _fun_ and lead to _visible_ _results_!

### Why should you follow her?
