## What is an AI Dance Generator
An AI dance generator is a tool that allows you to create a dance video from a photo, using choreography from a reference video or library. You upload a photo of a person (or even a pet), select the source choreography from a video or a preset library, and the AI will replicate those movements perfectly onto your character.
‍
Most AI dance generators use artificial intelligence to create a new video of you dancing from scratch. By 2026, these models had become so advanced that the results were almost impossible to distinguish from real videos, and they could capture every detail, including clothing, perfectly.
‍
## Why You Should Use an AI Dance Generator
Most people use AI dance markers for fun, either to create memes of famous people dancing awkwardly — have you seen videos of Elon Musk dancing in the Oval Office? — or to create dances with complex choreography that they can't quite pull off themselves, which they then post on social media to share with friends.
‍
Beyond that, dance generators are useful for brands, as they can create marketing campaigns featuring choreographed movements without having to hire professional dancers, which is much more expensive.
‍
You could also use an AI dance generator to create a budget music video, setting a character to dance to your tune. Instead of hiring a professional dance troupe or renting studio space, you can create a music video in seconds.

Finally, some people don't want to show their face or animate themselves, so they use an AI avatar, an illustrated character or an edited photo and make it dance.
