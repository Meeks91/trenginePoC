Artificial Intelligence (AI) is shaping the future of technology, and staying ahead of AI advancements requires insights from the top minds in the field. LinkedIn has become the ultimate platform for AI thought leadership, where researchers, entrepreneurs, and AI visionaries share their expertise, predictions, and ethical concerns.
Following the right AI influencers on LinkedIn allows professionals, businesses, and enthusiasts to stay informed about cutting-edge AI developments, practical applications, and industry shifts. Here are the top 8 AI influencers who are leading the conversation on AI innovation, research, and business strategy.
## 1. Yann LeCun – Chief AI Scientist at Meta
Yann LeCun is one of the pioneers of [deep learning](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Deep_learning) and a leading authority in AI research. As the Chief AI Scientist at Meta, he has played a significant role in developing convolutional neural networks (CNNs), which are fundamental in image recognition and AI-driven automation. 
On LinkedIn, LeCun frequently shares groundbreaking research papers, insights into AI ethics, and his vision for the future of artificial intelligence. His posts often spark high-level discussions among AI researchers, students, and industry professionals.
## 2. Bernard Marr – Futurist, Author, and AI Strategy Expert
Bernard Marr is a best-selling author, business strategist, and one of LinkedIn’s top 5 business influencers. His expertise lies in AI, big data, and digital transformation, helping companies integrate AI into their business models. His LinkedIn content provides valuable insights into AI trends, real-world use cases, and the impact of AI on business and society.
## 3. Steve Nouri – Founder of AI4Diversity and GenAI Works
Steve Nouri is a recognized AI thought leader and advocate for diversity in AI. He founded AI4Diversity, a global initiative promoting inclusive and ethical AI development. 
His LinkedIn presence is a mix of AI industry updates, ethical discussions, and insights into how AI can drive positive societal change. He also shares job opportunities, startup news, and technical breakdowns of the latest AI advancements, making him a great resource for professionals looking to stay ahead in the AI space.
## 4. Andrew Ng – AI Educator, Founder of DeepLearning.AI & CEO of Landing AI
Andrew Ng is one of the most influential figures in AI education and applied machine learning. As the co-founder of Coursera, founder of DeepLearning.AI, and CEO of Landing AI, he is dedicated to making AI education accessible to all. 
His LinkedIn posts cover AI innovations, practical applications of machine learning, and career advice for AI professionals. He frequently shares free AI courses, insights on how businesses can adopt AI, and updates on emerging AI technologies.
## 5. Demis Hassabis – CEO & Co-Founder of DeepMind
Demis Hassabis leads DeepMind, one of the world’s most innovative AI research labs, responsible for AlphaGo, AlphaFold, and other AI breakthroughs. 
His expertise spans AI, neuroscience, and cognitive science, and he is focused on how AI can solve real-world problems, particularly in healthcare. On LinkedIn, he shares insights into AI’s role in medical research, robotics, and the future of artificial general intelligence (AGI). 
## 6. Nathan Lambert – AI Ethics and Safety Researcher
Nathan Lambert is a research scientist at the Allen Institute for AI (AI2) and formerly led the RLHF (Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback) team at Hugging Face. 
His work centers around AI safety, alignment, and governance, making him one of the most important voices in AI ethics today. On LinkedIn, he discusses bias in AI, risks of AI misuse, and the importance of transparency in AI development. 
## 7. Pascal Bornet – AI Automation Expert & Best-Selling Author
Pascal Bornet is a globally recognized expert in AI-driven automation, intelligent process automation (IPA), and digital transformation. He has authored best-selling books on AI and automation, and he regularly speaks at top AI conferences. His LinkedIn content focuses on how businesses can leverage AI to improve efficiency, reduce costs, and drive innovation. 
## 8. Terence Mills – CEO of Veuu & Chairman of Aidotio
Terence Mills is a prominent AI entrepreneur and a recognized leader in AI, [blockchain](https://dictionary.cambridge.org/dictionary/english/blockchain), and VoiceAI. He is a member of the Forbes Technology Council and has been named a top AI and data influencer by Onalytica. 
His LinkedIn posts cover the intersection of AI, blockchain, and fintech, providing deep insights into how emerging technologies are shaping the future.
