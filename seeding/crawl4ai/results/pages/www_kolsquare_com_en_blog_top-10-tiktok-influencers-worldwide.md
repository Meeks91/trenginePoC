# Top 10 TikTok Influencers Worldwide
TikTok is successful, and so are the app's users. The promise of virality is something like the modern American dream: anyone can use the app's unique algorithm to quickly achieve great success and even become a mega-influencer. This is the case with the top 10 of the app's most successful influencers worldwide, which we present in this article.

## Key Takeaways
  * TikTok’s global success is shaped by its algorithm which promises quick fame and fortune for creators, making TikTok an attractive platform for both nano- and mega-influencers.
  * The recent research shows that TikTok users are willing to spend money on the platform. As a result, 38% of experts plan to increase their advertising spending on TikTok.
  * The ranking of the top 10 TikTokers in 2023 is led by Khaby.lame, who has 162 million followers on the platform. He is followed by Charli D’Amelio, Bella Poarch, Addison Rae Sterling, Kimberly Loaiza, and many more.

While macro- and mega-influencers are rising to real stars and have global prominence, in 2023 it is mainly the nano-influencers who are attractive cooperation partners for brands. ## The top 10 most famous and followed TikTokers worldwide.
Before we start, we’ll introduce our methodology that underlies all of our Top 10’s. So, to create the ranking of the top 10 TikTokers, we filtered the Kolsquare database of over three million KOLs with more than 5,000 followers according to the platform, their credibility score, and their job category, namely global influencers, bloggers, or videographers. 
  * With the **Kolsquare credibility score** , we show how trustworthy an influencer is in the eyes of the respective audience, where a higher percentage indicates a larger number of real followers (as opposed to fake or bought ones). We rate a score of 70 or higher (out of 100) as good, better, etc.
  * The **engagement rate** measures the effectiveness of influencers based on reactions, comments, shares, likes, and similar interactions per post and is therefore an important indicator for brands.
  * **Earned Media Value (EMV)** , which indicates the value of advertising and reporting, is also relevant in influencer marketing and can reflect KOL rates on platforms like TikTok or Instagram Reels.


Another small note: If platforms are not listed for some influencers, it means that they cannot be found on the respective network or there is no further information about them. However, it is also possible to add or merge accounts on Kolsquare. Accordingly, we are always happy to receive feedback on our platform and, of course, new customers with whom we can grow together.
