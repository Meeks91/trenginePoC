## Our unique 3D printing service levels
Our premium service is tailored for demanding applications that require precision, speed, and exceptional quality. Ideal for high-performance industries such as Formula One, automotive, and yacht racing, this service offers: 
  * **Precision and Quality:** Guaranteed tolerances of +/-0.5% (minimum 0.3mm). Higher tolerances can be agreed upfront based on individual project requirements
  * **Large Part Capability:** Accommodates larger components without compromising on quality.
  * **Advanced Post-Processing:** Custom options including inserts, sealing, sanded surfaces, and RF shielding.
  * **Rapid Turnaround:** Faster agreed dispatch dates for urgent projects, ensuring critical deadlines are met.
  * **Superior Aesthetic Standards:** Stricter quality control criteria to ensure the best finish.
  * **Engineering Support:** Dedicated consultation and technical guidance to optimise your project.
  * **Documentation and Compliance Support:** Full dimensional reports, PPAP and Certificate of Conformities.
  * **Design Review:** We review your design and tailor orientation and build parameters for each component to achieve optimal results.
  * **Access to Cutting-Edge Materials:** We match the latest advanced materials and 3D printing technologies to your project’s needs.
