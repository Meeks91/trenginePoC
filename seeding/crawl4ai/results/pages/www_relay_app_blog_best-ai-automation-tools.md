# Take your workflow automation to the next level with AI
If you’ve been following the AI boom, you’ve likely noticed there are a few different types of tools that let you harness artificial intelligence in various ways. Some of them (like [ChatGPT](https://chatgpt.com/) or [Claude](https://claude.ai/)) are chatbots that you can have conversations with, do research, and get on-demand answers. Others are in-product copilots, like [GitHub Copilot](https://github.com/features/copilot) or [Notion AI](https://www.notion.so/product/ai), that help you complete tasks within a product faster. But the most exciting category, in our view, is the budding new class of AI automation tools. They work on your behalf as you sleep and take care of all of the mundane, repetitive tasks in your business.But there are lots of them! And what’s more — new categories are already emerging within this group of tools. So we wrote this guide to dive into the ten best, grouped by primary use case, to help you choose the one that’s right for you. Whether you're a business owner looking to simplify your operations or an enterprise team aiming to scale your processes, chances are you’re going to want to use at least one of these tools.We'll start with a brief overview of what to look for in an AI automation tool, then walk through how to evaluate the emergent categories in this space, and finally dive into our top tool picks.

# How I evaluated these platforms
The main goal of an AI automation tool is to save you and your team time, money, and increase your productivity — but you have to pick the tool that’s the right match for your use case and has all of the capabilities you need.Let's quickly outline what makes a great AI automation tool:
  * **Ease of use:** A Ph.D. in computer science or an encyclopedic user manual isn’t needed to start building powerful, automated workflows.
  * **AI capabilities:** Advanced AI features like natural language editing and automated web browsing are native to the platform, and you can easily switch between multiple different models.
  * **Integrations:** Connections to your existing tools and platforms are a breeze and new integrations are consistently being added.
  * **Customization:** It’s flexible enough to handle more niche, specific use cases for your business without hacky workarounds, for example, by incorporating your internal knowledge base.
  * **Scalability:** Grows with your company. You won’t have to jump to a new platform in a few months or years as your task volume increases or you add more members to your team.
  * **Cost:** Doesn’t break the bank to automate all of your tasks and workflows.Not every AI automation tool will check all the boxes in this list, but these bullets provide a helpful high-level framework for evaluating these tools.In addition to these general requirements, new categories are emerging within this space that cater to specific business needs.The right category of tool for you depends on your specific automation needs, the scale of your operations, and your team's technical capabilities. Let’s dive into each category briefly.


### 🔀 AI-integrated workflow automation tools
These no-code tools are designed to connect a wide range of apps and automate complex workflows at scale — with AI capabilities available at every step. You might already be familiar with tools like Zapier and Make, but there are a host of new workflow builders developed with AI at their core that expand the definition of what’s possible with no-code workflow automation.Unlike standalone AI tools, which focus on specific verticals, industries, and tasks, AI-integrated workflow automation tools offer a more comprehensive and flexible option.You might want to look for a tool in this category if:
  * Seamless integrations are important to your business and you want to seamlessly connect a wide range of apps
  * You’re looking to automate complex workflows and need a more comprehensive and flexible option compared to more narrow-focused AI solutions
  * Having robust, advanced AI actions powered by a library of the top AI models, which you can easily swap in and out, is needed
  * Automation at scale is important — you’re looking to streamline a high volume of tasks and workflows


### 🧑‍💼 AI agent platforms
AI agent platforms let you create intelligent assistants that can autonomously complete tasks, make decisions, and interact with various systems on your behalf. Unlike traditional automation platforms, which are task- and rule-based, AI agent platforms are built to handle ongoing, adaptive processes that require continuous learning, complex reasoning, and human-like tasks.They are ideal for businesses that want to delegate entire processes or interactions to an AI agent rather than relying primarily on rule-based automations.You might want to look for a tool in this category if:
  * You need an AI-powered agent that can autonomously make decisions and take actions without constant human oversight
  * You want to integrate AI into more ambiguous workflows where adaptability and dynamic responses are helpful
  * You're looking to enhance customer engagement, sales, or virtual assistant tasks where AI agents capable of learning and improving over time are valuable


### 🌐 Browser-based AI assistants
Browser-based assistants are AI-powered tools that live within your web browser and help automate tasks across your open tabs. These assistants allow you to streamline repetitive actions, data entry, and other simple task-based automations without switching between different applications.They are especially useful if you want to automate tasks like filling out forms, extracting data, or writing communication without leaving your web browsing experience.You might want to look for a tool in this category if:
  * You spend a lot of time copying/pasting data across tabs in your browser and need to automate tasks like data entry or collection
  * You're looking for a simple, lightweight solution that integrates with your browsing experience without heavy setup or extensive app integration
  * You need real-time AI-powered assistance for tasks like scraping data, writing emails, or updating applications


### 📚 Enterprise knowledge management tools
Enterprise knowledge management tools are designed to help you efficiently organize, retrieve, and leverage critical information across various data sources.Unlike traditional knowledge management systems that rely on manual input and static information repositories, AI-powered knowledge management tools continuously update and intelligently process information from your data sources, making it easier for your teams to harness your business’s most relevant data in real time.These tools are ideal for teams managing complex information workflows across departments.You might want to look for a tool in this category if:
  * You need to centralize your organization's knowledge and make it easily accessible to all team members
  * You want to automate the discovery, categorization, and retrieval of information without relying on manual processes
  * You're looking to improve collaboration and decision-making by ensuring that the most accurate and up-to-date information is always available
  * You need to streamline information management at scale, especially for teams or enterprises handling large volumes of data and documentationNow that you have a better understanding of the AI automation landscape, let's dive into our list of the 10 best AI automation tools of 2026.
