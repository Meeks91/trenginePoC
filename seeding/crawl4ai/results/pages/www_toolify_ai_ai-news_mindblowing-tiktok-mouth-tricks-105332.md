**Trick 1: Seeing your breath when it's not cold outside**
Have you ever wondered how to see your breath when it's not cold outside? Well, TikTok has an answer for you. By following a few simple steps, you can [Create](https://www.toolify.ai/tool/autodevs-by-create) the illusion of visible breath. Then, pressurize the air inside your mouth, as if you're trying to blow it out but holding it in with your [HAND](https://www.toolify.ai/tool/hand). Finally, slowly release the air. **Trick 2: Whistling with your fingers**
Whistling is a skill many people wish they had but struggle to master. Thankfully, TikTok has an easy trick that can help you whistle with your fingers. To start, put your fingers together in a specific way. Then, fold your tongue back and place the tips of your fingers on the bottom tip of your tongue. Once you have your fingers in your mouth, find the sweet spot - a position that allows just enough airflow through your teeth. Now, purse your lips and blow, creating a sharp and clear whistle sound. It may take a few attempts to get it right, but once you do, you'll have a handy way to get someone's [Attention](https://www.toolify.ai/tool/attention) or impress your friends.
**Trick 3: Gleeking**
Gleeking, the act of projecting saliva from the salivary glands under your tongue, may sound strange, but it's a fun trick to try. To gleek, start by massaging the underside of your tongue with your mouth closed. Then, introduce something sour to activate the glands. Next, put the tip of your tongue on the roof of your mouth and push outward with your tongue. This action will stimulate the glands, causing saliva to squirt out. It may take some practice, but once you unlock this unique ability, you'll have a fascinating party trick up your sleeve.
**Trick 4: Pushing out the sides of your mouth**
