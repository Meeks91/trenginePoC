# 14 AI Design Tools for Creative Professionals
  * Published: July 29, 2024
  * 15 min read


The creative industry is undergoing a modern-day renaissance, and AI design tools (among other things) are responsible for the seismic shift. These tools and technologies push the boundaries of creativity, freeing up overloaded designers, artists, and photographers to explore new possibilities and _get more done with less_.
Whether you’re a graphic designer, video editor, or 3D artist, these AI design tools can help you streamline your workflow, improve your creativity, and stay ahead in the fast-evolving creative industry. Below, we’ll walk you through some of our favorite tools and applications that’ll change how you get creative work done.
  * AI design tools assist creative professionals by generating design suggestions (layouts, color schemes, fonts) and even creating graphics or UI elements automatically based on input criteria or content.
  * These tools can speed up workflows by handling tedious tasks like image background removal, auto-adjusting graphics to different sizes, or producing multiple design variations for A/B testing.
  * For designers, integrating AI tools means they can iterate faster and explore more concepts—using AI to handle repetitive work or to spark inspiration—while still applying their expertise to refine and finalize the creative output.


##  [What are AI design tools?](https://www.digitalocean.com/resources/articles/ai-design-tools#what-are-ai-design-tools)
AI design tools are software applications that [use artificial intelligence](https://www.digitalocean.com/resources/article/artificial-intelligence-in-business) to assist and improve design processes. These tools leverage machine learning, [natural language processing (NLP)](https://www.digitalocean.com/community/tutorials/how-to-work-with-language-data-in-python-3-using-the-natural-language-toolkit-nltk), and other AI technologies to automate repetitive tasks, generate creative ideas, and optimize design elements.
AI design tools can be used for everything from graphic design and web development to video editing and creative brainstorming. This automation and AI assistance helps designers work more efficiently and creatively.
##  [Benefits of AI design tools](https://www.digitalocean.com/resources/articles/ai-design-tools#benefits-of-ai-design-tools)
AI tools don’t _replace_ creatives—they _complement_ them. Here’s how AI design tools can help your modern designers get work done:
  * **Increased efficiency:** AI design tools automate time-consuming tasks, such as image editing, layout adjustments, and content creation. This allows designers to focus on more strategic and creative aspects of their work.
  * **More innovation:** These tools provide creative suggestions and generate new ideas based on user input to help designers explore different concepts and push the boundaries of traditional design methods.
  * **Improved accuracy:** AI design tools can analyze data and user behavior to make precise adjustments—this guarantees designs are aesthetically pleasing and optimized for performance and user experience.
  * **Streamlined workflows:** AI design applications integrate with other design software and tools to facilitate smooth and efficient workflows, reducing the need for manual interventions and minimizing errors.
  * **Accessibility:** AI design tools make advanced design capabilities accessible to a broader audience, including those with limited design experience. This accessibility empowers more people to create professional-quality work.


##  [What to consider when choosing an AI design tool](https://www.digitalocean.com/resources/articles/ai-design-tools#what-to-consider-when-choosing-an-ai-design-tool)
There’s not necessarily a one-size-fits-all AI design tool. That’s why you’ll need to consider a few factors before investing in any application:
  * **Price:** Evaluate the tool’s cost and whether it fits within your budget. Consider any subscription fees, one-time payments, or additional costs for premium features (or users).
  * **Features:** Look for tools that provide the specific functionalities you need, such as image editing, content generation, layout optimization, or animation.
  * **Scalability:** If you anticipate your projects growing in complexity or volume, choose a tool that can handle increased demands without performance issues.
  * **Integrations:** Check if the tool integrates seamlessly with other software and platforms you use, such as Adobe Creative Suite, Figma, or your preferred content management system.
  * **Ease of use:** Consider the user interface and learning curve. An intuitive and easy-to-use tool will save time and reduce frustration.
  * **Resources:** Explore the community around the tool. Active user communities, forums, and extensive documentation can provide valuable insights, tips, and problem-solving help.
  * **Security:** Verify the tool’s data protection measures and compliance with relevant regulations.
  * **Customization:** Flexible tools can adapt to unique workflows and preferences to provide greater control over the design process.

[Canva](https://www.canva.com/) is a widely used graphic design platform that integrates artificial intelligence to streamline the design process for users of all skill levels. It’s ideal for designers, marketers, and social media managers looking to improve creativity and productivity.
Canva’s AI capabilities [help automate](https://www.digitalocean.com/resources/article/cloud-automation) design tasks, provide smart suggestions, and generate high-quality graphics with minimal effort. This tool is perfect for creating social media graphics, presentations, posters, and more.
**Key features include:**
  * **AI-powered design suggestions:** Canva’s AI analyzes your design and suggests improvements to improve visual appeal. It also will create AI-generated images.
  * **Magic Resize:** Automatically resizes your design to fit different formats and platforms, saving time and effort.
  * **Background remover:** Uses AI to cleanly remove backgrounds from images.
  * **Template library:** Thousands of customizable templates powered by AI to match your design needs.


**Example use case:**
A digital marketing professional needed to quickly produce assets for an upcoming campaign. They used Canva’s AI-powered Magic Resize feature to transform visuals into social media posts, email banners, and promotional graphics. This streamlined process allowed them to focus more on the campaign strategy and less on repetitive design tasks.

###  [7. Stable Diffusion](https://www.digitalocean.com/resources/articles/ai-design-tools#7-stable-diffusion)
[Stable Diffusion](https://stability.ai/news/stable-diffusion-3) is an open-source AI model that generates high-quality images from text descriptions. It has gained popularity for its ability to create detailed and diverse visuals, making it accessible to artists, designers, and developers across various creative fields. The model uses a latent diffusion technique, which compresses the image into a smaller latent space, allowing for faster and more efficient image generation compared to some other methods.
**Key features include:**
  * **Text-to-image generation:** Create images from detailed text prompts, allowing for precise control over the output.
  * **Image-to-image transformation:** Modify existing images based on text instructions or style transfer.
  * **Inpainting and outpainting:** Edit specific parts of an image or expand it beyond its original boundaries.
  * **Multiple deployment options:** Run locally on consumer hardware or deploy on cloud services for increased performance.
  * **Active community:** Benefit from a large ecosystem of tools, modifications, and resources developed by the community.


**Example use case:**
A project management SaaS startup needed unique illustrations for their website to visualize concepts like task delegation, time tracking, and team collaboration. Using Stable Diffusion, they generated a set of playful, isometric-style images featuring animated characters interacting with oversized task boards, calendar elements, and productivity tools.

##  [Art design tool FAQs](https://www.digitalocean.com/resources/articles/ai-design-tools#art-design-tool-faqs)
**What are AI design tools and how do they help creative professionals?**
AI design tools are software applications that use artificial intelligence to assist and enhance design processes, leveraging machine learning, natural language processing, and other AI technologies to automate repetitive tasks, generate creative ideas, and optimize design elements. They help creative professionals by increasing efficiency through automated image editing and layout adjustments, providing creative suggestions and new ideas, and making advanced design capabilities accessible to those with limited design experience.
**What key benefits do AI design tools provide to designers and creative teams?**
AI design tools offer increased efficiency by automating time-consuming tasks like image editing and content creation, allowing designers to focus on strategic and creative aspects of their work. They provide improved accuracy through data analysis and user behavior insights to optimize designs for performance and user experience, while streamlined workflows integrate with other design software to reduce manual interventions and minimize errors.
**What should you consider when choosing AI design tools for your projects?**
Key considerations include price and budget constraints, specific features needed like image editing or content generation, scalability for growing project complexity, and integration capabilities with existing software like Adobe Creative Suite or Figma. You should also evaluate ease of use and learning curve, available community resources and documentation, security measures and data protection, and customization options to adapt to unique workflows and preferences.
**What are some popular AI design tools and their main capabilities?**
Popular tools include Canva for AI-powered design suggestions and magic resize features, Adobe Sensei for content-aware fill and smart cropping, and Midjourney for text-to-image generation with artistic flair. Other notable tools are Runway for AI-powered video editing and generative models, Prisma for artistic photo filters, and Stable Diffusion for open-source image generation with text-to-image capabilities and community-driven modifications.
