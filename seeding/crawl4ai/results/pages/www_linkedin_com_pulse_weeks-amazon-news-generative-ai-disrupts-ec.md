Amazon just killed virtual bundles....If you've been using virtual bundles to increase AOV, Amazon quietly moved them behind a toggle. Customers now have to manually enable "Show bundle deals" to see them. Which means 90% of shoppers will never see your bundle. Virtual bundles were a goldmine:
  * Increase average order value without holding extra inventory
  * Boost visibility by appearing in multiple searches
  * Differentiate from competitors with strategic bundling


Now they're essentially invisible. Virtual bundles are no longer a primary growth lever. They're a nice-to-have for the 10% of customers who toggle them on. If you were relying on bundles for revenue:
  * Focus on multi-packs instead
  * Create physical bundles if margins work
  * Invest more in core listing optimization
  * Build subscribe and save momentum
  * Use A+ content to suggest complementary products


The brands that win on Amazon don't rely on one feature, they diversify their approach.
