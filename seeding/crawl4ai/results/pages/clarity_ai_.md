  * ### What is Clarity AI, and who do we serve?
Clarity AI is a leading sustainability tech company with the mission to bring societal impact to markets. We empower financial institutions, corporates, governments, and retail platforms (including banks and e-commerce) to make sustainability part of how they think, decide, and grow. Our mission is to become the gold standard in sustainability intelligence—making it scalable, explainable, and embedded in everyday decisions.
  * ### How does Clarity AI ensure the quality and reliability of the data?
Data quality is our foundation. We combine proprietary collection systems, AI-powered processing, and rigorous expert validation to deliver data that is accurate, current, and fully explainable. With 98k issuers, 2.3M private companies, 450,000+ funds, and 400+ sovereigns, our coverage is unmatched—and we provide full traceability back to source, including transparency on confidence levels and methodologies.
  * ### What sets Clarity AI’s insights apart from traditional ESG providers?
Unlike legacy ESG ratings based on opaque analyst opinions, Clarity AI uses a hybrid intelligence model: our sustainability PhDs work hand-in-hand with data scientists and AI engineers to deliver insights that are transparent, scientifically grounded, and scalable. This ensures consistency and eliminates the subjectivity and blind spots that come from analyst-driven approaches.
  * ### Can Clarity AI adapt to my firm’s specific needs or methodology?
Absolutely. We don’t believe in a one-size-fits-all solution. Our platform is built for flexibility, enabling clients to apply their own methodologies or use our evidence-based models. Whether you’re a global asset manager or a retail bank, Clarity AI integrates into your workflow via APIs, datafeeds, a standalone web app, or even custom GPTs, delivering insights where and how you need them.
  * ### How does Clarity AI respond to the evolving nature of sustainability analysis and reporting?
Sustainability is dynamic, and so are we. Our systems are designed for continuous improvement—applying GenAI, NLP, and expert oversight to refine inputs, flag inconsistencies, and adapt to regulatory and market changes quickly. Rather than relying on static updates, we deliver continuously enhanced insights that reflect the most current validated data, helping clients stay ahead of both risks and opportunities.
