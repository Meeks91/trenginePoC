# Top Fitness Influencers to Follow on TikTok
August 3, 2023
As the fitness niche evolves, the fit influencers and creators behind the informative and engaging content have expanded to TikTok. These workout experts have found ways to share their exercise tutorials entertainingly. Because TikTok tends to have a younger audience than other platforms, according to [Statista](https://www.statista.com/statistics/1299771/tiktok-global-user-age-distribution/), these fitness influencers help brands reach more Gen Z audiences. TikTok has some interesting features that present challenges and opportunities for [fitness influencers](https://izea.com/resources/top-gym-influencers/) who want to use it to build audiences. The platform allows these creators to share their favorite workouts, healthy routines, useful products, and more, all in short-form videos. Follow along to see the top fitness influencers on TikTok sharing informative and highly energetic content.
## Top fitness influencers on TikTok
### Jordan Morello
Diagnosed with ADHD, Tourette syndrome and dyslexia at a young age, fitness trainer Jordan Morello began using [physical activity and healthy nutrition](https://www.tiktok.com/@jordanmorello/video/7239418161102064942?lang=en) to enhance his focus. Now Jordan hopes to share the benefits of exercise with [more than 181.2K followers](https://www.tiktok.com/@jordanmorello?lang=en). He also offers [personalized meal and workout plans](https://www.tiktok.com/@jordanmorello/video/7244678779493387566?lang=en) through his coaching program, The Fit Body. He also has a [nutrition and training app](https://getcooking.cookbuonappetito.com/).
### Seneda Greca
Fitness trainer Senada subscribes to the idea that fitness should go beyond short-term goals. With that in mind, she shares [exercise ideas and tips](https://www.tiktok.com/@senada.greca/video/7243549602635435307?lang=en) that help her followers turn working out into a [daily ritual](https://www.tiktok.com/@senada.greca/video/7242421837714541866?lang=en). Her videos [incorporate workout equipment](https://www.tiktok.com/@senada.greca/video/7247959968127814958?lang=en) such as resistance bands, exercise balls and hand weights. When Seneda isn’t sharing her workouts, she often posts [tutorials on how to recreate her favorite healthy meals](https://www.tiktok.com/@senada.greca/video/7247593642217508142?lang=en) for her [more than 2.2M followers](https://www.tiktok.com/@senada.greca?lang=en).
### Ulisses Jr.
Ulisses Jr. has earned plenty of bodybuilding accolades. He’s a [MuscleMania Pro World Champion](https://www.tiktok.com/@ulissesworld/video/7232627064082320666?lang=en) and a MuscleMania SuperBody Champion. Although his competitive career has ended, he [stays in the spotlight by posting fitness content](https://www.tiktok.com/@ulissesworld/video/7225515743842979098?lang=en) on TikTok. He now shares his [weightlifting routines](https://www.tiktok.com/@ulissesworld/video/7223816766131096837?lang=en) and his lifestyle outside the gym with [more than 2.9M followers](https://www.tiktok.com/@ulissesworld?lang=en).
### Antonie Lokhorst
Antonie is a refreshing face amongst the fitness niche of TikTok that not only shares his workout routine but also [brings a little fitness humor](https://www.tiktok.com/@antonielokhorst/video/7231566312823082241?lang=en) to the feeds of [more than 4.1M followers](https://www.tiktok.com/@antonielokhorst?lang=en). His feed is full of [popular workout challenges](https://www.tiktok.com/@antonielokhorst/video/7215618461756591361?lang=en), [exercises you need to include in your routine](https://www.tiktok.com/@antonielokhorst/video/7228617665374391553?lang=en), and nutrition advice.
### Stefana Avara
Stefana quickly rose to fame on TikTok for her [easy-to-follow and quick workout tutorials](https://www.tiktok.com/@stefana.avara/video/7210172647487950123?lang=en) that anyone can try at home. [More than 1M followers](https://www.tiktok.com/@stefana.avara?lang=en) enjoy her gym routines, which often feature [trendy workout sets](https://www.tiktok.com/@stefana.avara/video/7208703094316617002?lang=en) and behind-the-scenes looks at her workouts. When she isn’t sharing her [weightlifting and workout routines](https://www.tiktok.com/@stefana.avara/video/7210552221296004395?lang=en), Stefana posts [healthy meal inspiration](https://www.tiktok.com/@stefana.avara/video/7226479765459340587?lang=en) and her lifestyle outside of the gym.
### Rebecca Louise
Known to [more than 526.2K followers](https://www.tiktok.com/@rebeccalouisefitness?lang=en) as their “[workout and mindset girl](https://www.tiktok.com/@rebeccalouisefitness/video/7244580162304593194?lang=en),” Rebecca has grabbed the attention of many as she hosts workout challenges and offers expert advice. Her account is full of her favorite challenges featured on the Burn app, [advice on mindset shifts](https://www.tiktok.com/@rebeccalouisefitness/video/7246552873054702894?lang=en), and [her favorite go-to snacks of the summer](https://www.tiktok.com/@rebeccalouisefitness/video/7244238205233548587?lang=en).

Jesse is living proof that working out can be fun if you do it right.
