Interesting fact: almost four in five people in Ireland reported their health as being good or very good in 2023 – which was [the highest](https://www.irishtimes.com/health/2025/02/17/number-of-irish-people-who-regard-themselves-as-healthy-is-highest-in-eu/#:~:text=Almost%20four%20in%20five%20people,Jennifer%20Carroll%20MacNeill%20on%20Monday.) in all the European Union!
There are several reasons for this. One is that Ireland has a strong sporting culture with popular activities like Gaelic football, hurling, rugby, soccer, and running. Many people participate in from a young age, fostering lifelong physical activity.
The country’s scenic landscapes encourage outdoor activities like hiking, cycling, and swimming. There are abundant parks, trails, and coastal paths that promote outdoor exercise – while the moderate climate allows for year-round outdoor activity without extreme heat or cold (once you own a rain jacket).
And something that can’t be overlooked in 2025 is the rise of Irish fitness influencers and online coaches who are encouraging more people to adopt healthier lifestyles.

So, with Saint Patrick’s Day around the corner (and this writer feeling a little more patriotic than usual), we’ve decided to make a list of some of Ireland’s most popular fitness influencers, so that people from around the world can be inspired by their work and informed by their knowledge!
# **Paul Olima**
**TikTok followers:** 2.9M **Instagram followers:** 2.4M
Paul, a former pro athlete and sports model, has gained fame for his comedic online presence. His content masterfully combines humor with fitness, keeping his followers engaged and informed. He also leads the Omega Academy, offering expert fitness coaching to members.
# **James Doyle**
**TikTok followers:** 1.4M **Instagram followers:** 219K
Fitness enthusiast James went viral a few years back thanks to his thick accent and ‘Protein Bor’ videos. He started out with gym and food reviews and general fitness-related videos until his unique comedic style took centre stage.
# **Caroline Girvan**
**YouTube subscribers:** 3.7M **Instagram followers:** 776K 
Caroline is a personal trainer and fitness influencer best known for her hugely popular YouTube workouts. With millions of followers worldwide, she creates home-based strength and endurance programs like the EPIC Series. Her no-nonsense approach and focus on functional fitness have helped people of all levels build strength and confidence.
# **Brian Keane**
**TikTok followers:** 263K **Instagram followers:** 144K
Brian is an online fitness coach, nutritionist, and best-selling author. He provides his followers with valuable insights into fitness and having a healthy mindset. He also has a [podcast](https://briankeanefitness.com/podcast) and runs business courses for trainers.

# **Caroline O’Mahony**
**TikTok followers:** 36K **Instagram followers:** 825K
Caroline is an online fitness coach who puts a lot of emphasis on educating her clients, so not only are they able to get amazing results – they’re also understanding each process and how it affects their bodies. She loves to showcase client transformations, share nutritious meal plans and also showcase her humour.
# **Rob Lipsett**
**TikTok followers:** 36.6K **Instagram followers:** 682K
Rob is one of Ireland’s most well-known fitness influencers. With a background in bodybuilding, he shares workout plans, nutrition advice, and mindset coaching. Rob has authored a book on fitness and runs his own supplement and coaching business. His high-energy content and motivational approach make him a go-to source for fitness enthusiasts worldwide.
# **Maeve Madden**
**TikTok followers:** 13.6K **Instagram followers:** 367K
Maeve is a fitness influencer, personal trainer, and best-selling author known for her empowering approach to health and wellness. As the founder of Queens Don’t Quit, an online fitness community, she promotes body confidence and sustainable fitness. Maeve shares workouts, nutrition tips, and lifestyle content, inspiring thousands to embrace movement and self-love.
# **Seán Fitzpatrick**
**TikTok followers:** 663K **Instagram followers:** 255K
Seán is a personal trainer and fitness influencer best known for his practical and science-backed training advice. With expertise in strength training and fat loss, he provides coaching to help clients achieve long-term results. His content focuses on simplifying fitness, debunking myths, and making workouts accessible for all levels, making him a trusted voice in the Irish fitness industry.
# **Siobhán O’Hagan**
**TikTok followers:** 45.6K **Instagram followers:** 160K 
Siobhán is an online coach and influencer who promotes a balanced approach to health. Known for her transformation from corporate life to full-time fitness coaching, she shares workout routines, nutrition tips, and mindset advice. Siobhán’s content encourages flexibility and self-trust, moving away from rigid dieting. Through her online coaching programs, she helps clients achieve their goals while maintaining a sustainable and enjoyable lifestyle.
# **Sarah Godfrey**
**TikTok followers:** 5.8K **Instagram followers:** 110K
Sarah is an online coach and the co-founder of Studio 708. With a background in personal training, she shares workout tips, nutrition advice, and lifestyle content with her audience. She is best known for her relatable and motivational approach, empowering her followers to build strength and confidence.
