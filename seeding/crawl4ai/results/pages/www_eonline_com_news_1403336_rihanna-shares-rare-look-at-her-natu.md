In fact, just days before [launching Fenty Hair](https://www.eonline.com/news/1402935/rihanna-is-expanding-her-beauty-empire-with-fenty-hair), the multi-hyphenate made the rare move to [rock her natural curls](https://www.eonline.com/photos/331/rihanna-s-best-looks). To keep the _mane_ attention on her tresses, Rihanna's fashion moment was quite simple—well, for her standards.
After all, she donned a simple baby blue spaghetti strap top (with her black lace bra peeking through for extra oomph), black oversized pants and a brown fuzzy coat that she draped off of her shoulders.
She completed [her streetwear style](https://www.eonline.com/news/1400722/why-rihanna-skipped-met-gala-2024-at-the-last-minute) with sandal heels, a black-and-white graphic bag from Louis Vuitton, layers of gold necklaces, massive hoop earrings and black sunnies.
As for her glam? Rihanna let her natural beauty shine through, wearing little-to-no makeup.

The 36-year-old's decision to display her natural curls comes less than a week after [she announced her new haircare line](https://www.eonline.com/news/1338861/fenty-hair-is-coming-all-the-details-on-rihannas-trademark-filing).
"A new family is moving in!" Rihanna captioned her June 4 [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/reel/C7zL5H5OJ7Z). "Fenty Hair is pullin up and it's time to finally have the hair experience you've been waiting for."
BlayzenPhotos / BACKGRID
Since the "Diamonds" singer is known for [experimenting with her looks](https://www.eonline.com/news/1400897/rihanna-debuts-bright-pink-hair-ahead-of-2024-met-gala), she wanted her products to cater to any and all hair types.
"You know how much [switching my hair](https://www.eonline.com/news/1400362/rihanna-reveals-why-her-2024-met-gala-look-might-be-her-most-surprising-yet) up matters to me," she continued. "I've had almost every texture, color, length, from weaves to braids to natural—so I am launching a flexible line of products for not only every hair want, but every single product is designed to strengthen and repair all types of hair, which is what we truly need!"
As she put it, "It's time to play and get stronger by the style."
Fenty Hair will debut June 13.

## Rihanna
The one and only **Rihanna** changed the game when she came out with her [Fenty Beauty line](https://www.eonline.com/news/1134389/rihanna-s-fenty-beauty-items-everyone-should-have) in 2017. Not only did she release 40 foundation shades, forging a new industry standard, but she has since [created innovative and exciting products](https://www.eonline.com/news/1112249/why-rihanna-will-never-have-the-same-beauty-look-according-to-her-makeup-artist). She also released [Fenty Skin](https://www.eonline.com/news/1170589/omg-fenty-skin-is-coming) and Fenty Hair.
Tibrina Hobson/Getty Images
## Selena Gomez
Selena launched[ Rare Beauty](https://www.google.com/search?q=selena+gomez+rare+beauty+eonline&rlz=1C1GCEA_enUS853US853&oq=selena+gomez+rare+beauty+eonline&aqs=chrome..69i57j69i59l2j0l2j69i60l3.4438j1j7&sourceid=chrome&ie=UTF-8) in September 2020. The brand's initial release featured a 12-range lineup. From foundation, makeup tools and liquid lipsticks to liquid liner, blushes and more, [Rare Beauty has all your bases covered](https://www.eonline.com/news/1183368/how-selena-gomezs-mental-health-journey-inspired-rare-beautys-unique-vision).

## Beyoncé
The Grammy winner launched her new haircare line, Cécred, which features an eight-piece collection that helps cleanse, condition and repair tresses.
"My entire life and career, I've worn my hair in so many different ways: natural, flat-ironed, braids, colored, weaves, wigs," Beyoncé said in a press release shared with E! News. "I want everyone to have the freedom to express their hair in ways that make them feel good."

## Lady Gaga
Mother Monster delighted her fans when she released [her own cosmetics brand](https://www.eonline.com/news/1055532/lady-gaga-announces-new-beauty-line-haus-laboratories-with-cheeky-motto-our-haus-your-rules), Haus Laboratories, in 2019. While the songstress is no stranger to the beauty world, she decided to create something for _everyone_. From glimmering lipsticks to long-lasting shadows, her makeup has become a holy grail. And in June 2022, Lady Gaga "re-imagined" her brand with completely new products, a new retailer and more.

## Sofia Vergara
The _Modern Family_ alum debuted her beauty line called Toty with daily essentials—a CC cream, compact, serum and more—that prioritize suncare protection. "Growing up in Colombia, my life revolved around being outside in the sun," the actress said in a press release. "I loved the sun, but little did I know, the sun wasn't loving me back! Once I learned the true benefits of suncare and protecting my skin from sun damage and photoaging, I was hooked. I created toty with the idea that suncare should be an effortless part of your daily beauty routine—it's my biggest beauty secret."
