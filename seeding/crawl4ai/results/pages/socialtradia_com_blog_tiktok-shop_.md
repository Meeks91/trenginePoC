## **What is a TikTok shop?**
Suppose you own a business, sell products, and want to grow and develop your business. In that case, TikTok Shop is undoubtedly one of the best options for you to introduce your products to TikTok users creatively. And they will easily buy from TikTok shop seller like you.
With the help of this feature, TikTok users can purchase your product when they watch your product introduction videos in TikTok Shop without leaving the app or opening a new browser.
When a brand or business makes an online store on TikTok in order to sell its products, a shopping tab is displayed in its profile, which is similar to a bag or a shopping cart, and this icon informs users that you are selling products in your videos.
When users use TikTok and see your profile by clicking on the shopping cart icon (shopping tab), they can access a collection of your various products. Then, by tapping on the desired product, they complete the purchase and payment process and purchase quickly without leaving the app.
## **How to create a TikTok shop**
Creating a TikTok shop is very easy, but there are some tips you can follow to make a TikTok shop correctly; we will check them below.
Step 1: Before anything else, note that in order to have a TikTok shop, you must have a business account on TikTok, so if you still need to get a business account, create it and then go through the following steps.
Step 2: After creating a TikTok business account, go to TikTok Seller Center to sign up for your store. Then, upload all the necessary documents, such as website links, email addresses, and products. Now you need to link your bank account to your TikTok account to finish the process.
Step 3: Download TikTok Shopify; this app integrates with TikTok for Business Ads Manager, and with its help, you can create innovative advertising videos, track and check the performance of your store, and manage all your orders.
Remember that by using this application, you can choose for customers to pay directly on TikTok or redirect to your Shopify account.
Step 4: To get the best results from using TikTok Shop, in your bio profile, write a complete description of your business and the products you sell so that users will be attracted to your store and visit it by reading it.
After finishing these steps, you should know how to easily sell your products to more people, which we will explain fully in the next section.
## **How to sell on TikTok shop**
Selling your products in TikTok Shop is simple; in the following, we will give you some tips to help you sell more efficiently.
1) **Complete and confirm your account information** : After creating your TikTok shop, you must complete your user account information, which includes address, bank accounts, contact numbers, and the like. Then you have to confirm and specify this information. After these steps, choose and upload a suitable and high-quality photo for your profile to complete your profile.
2) **Add your products** : After your account is approved, you must upload photos of your products to your account. Try to take high-quality and creative pictures of your products that follow the policies of the TikTok app. Also, for more visibility, you can show your brand logo in the images to draw more attention to your business and show your ownership of the photos in this way.
3) **Create interesting and engaging content** : One of the things that will help you make more sales in TikTok Shop and attract more audiences is creating exciting and entertaining content. Because as we said before, most TikTok users are young people, so you have to be creative in this field in order to attract their attention.
To create engaging content, you must be innovative and up-to-date. First, determine your niche by searching for related topics on TikTok. Then, create attractive videos or [TikTok mockup](https://www.webdesignerhub.org/tiktok-mockups/) presentations to introduce your products. Finally, make them have high engagement and brand awareness.
4) **Collaborate with influencers** : As mentioned, many people earn money through TikTok; some of them are very famous and have many followers, so cooperating with them can help grow your brand and sell more of your products.
In order to be able to cooperate with influencers who get the best results, you can check TikTok Creators Marketplace and choose the person who is the best option to introduce your products.
In addition to increasing the number of your followers and customers, this will create a sense of trust among your customers and help your brand’s reputation.
5) **Tag your videos with your products** : One of the most important ways to help you promote and sell your products is to tag them on your videos. Users are very smart, and some don’t spend their time watching advertising videos, so you can make an attractive video and tag your products on it. With this, you have introduced your products and increased your engagement.
6) **Use paid TikTok ads and promote posts** : Another way to help your TikTok shop be seen more and have more customers is to use ad videos and [promotional posts](https://socialtradia.com/blog/best-time-to-post-on-tiktok/).
Follow these steps to promote your videos:
  * Tap the Me icon to go to your profile
  * Select the Settings options from the three-line menu
  * Choose Creator tools and then tap on Promote
  * Now, you need to choose the public video you want to promote from the Promotion page


Following these simple steps, you can easily promote your videos to reach more audiences.
As mentioned, you can also use paid ad videos to attract more attention to your TikTok shop and products. Still, some requirements for creating an advertisement video include image/video file, aspect ratio, brand name, ad description (caption), video duration, image resolution, etc.
By following these tips, you can have an excellent and complete TikTok shop and introduce your products well to attract a large audience and turn them into your customers. In addition, these items will also bring you brand awareness and engagement, which will help your business grow.
