#  Koen de Jong’s Post
💡 Most CEOs still think AI equals automation. It doesn’t. Automation follows rules. AI agents make decisions. At [virtualworkforce.ai](https://nl.linkedin.com/company/virtualworkforce-ai?trk=public_post-text), our AI email agents don’t just send prewritten replies — they understand context, use company data, and act across ERP, TMS, and CRM. At [visionplatform.ai](https://nl.linkedin.com/company/visionplatform-ai?trk=public_post-text), our vision agents don’t just detect — they interpret what cameras see and decide what to do next. We’re moving from workflows → to agents → to autonomous ecosystems. The CEOs who understand this shift early will lead the next decade of productivity. 🚀 The future of work isn’t automated. It’s agentic.
