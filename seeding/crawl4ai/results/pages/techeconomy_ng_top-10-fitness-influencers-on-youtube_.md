# Top 10 Fitness Influencers on YouTube
Reading Time: 2 mins read 
[ Fitness influencers on YouTube](https://techeconomy.ng/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/Fitness-influencers-on-YouTube.jpg)
A new research conducted by [**nfsupplements.co.uk**](http://nfsupplements.co.uk/) has revealed the globe’s most impactful fitness figures.
The assembling the world’s 50 most popular [fitness influencers in 2024](https://techeconomy.ng/10-most-popular-fitness-influencers-in-2024/) through a comprehensive analysis of Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, and Google search metrics, is quite interesting.
## **Key Findings:**
  * Top 10 fitness influencers amass 118.1M followers on YouTube.
  * 5 Influencers from the top 10 YouTube fitness influencers hail from the USA.
  * Female creators lead TikTok’s top ranks, holding 6 out of the top 10 spots, including the top spot.


## 10 Most Popular Fitness Influencers on YouTube
## 1. **Chloe Ting (Female, Australia):**
With an impressive 24.9 million YouTube followers, Chloe Ting has become a household name in the fitness world, known for her challenging workout routines and effective fitness challenges that resonate with audiences worldwide.
## **2. Jeff Cavaliere (ATHLEAN-X™) (Male, US):**
Jeff Cavaliere, with 13.5 million YouTube followers, is renowned for his expertise in strength training and sports conditioning, providing his audience with science-backed advice to achieve their fitness goals.

## **3. Patry Jordan (gymvirtual) (Female, Spain):**
Patry Jordan’s gymvirtual channel boasts 13.1 million followers, offering diverse workout routines tailored to different fitness levels, making fitness accessible to her global audience.
## **4. Adriene Mishler (Yoga With Adriene) (Female, US):**
With 12.3 million YouTube followers, Adriene Mishler has established herself as a leading yoga instructor, guiding viewers through yoga practices that promote physical and mental well-being.

## **5. Thibaud Delapart (Tibo InShape) (Male, France):**
Tibo InShape’s YouTube channel, with 12.1 million followers, delivers entertaining and informative content on fitness and nutrition, attracting a loyal following seeking practical advice for a healthier lifestyle.
## **6. Pamela Reif (Female, Germany):**
Pamela Reif’s 9.7 million YouTube followers appreciate her dynamic workout routines and motivational content, inspiring viewers to prioritize fitness and self-care in their daily lives.
## **7. Cassey Ho (blogilates) (Female, US):**
Cassey Ho’s blogilates channel, with 9.2 million followers, offers a diverse range of Pilates workouts and challenges that emphasize strength, flexibility, and overall wellness.
## **8. Sofie Dossi (Female, US):**
Sofie Dossi’s 9.1 million YouTube followers are drawn to her unique blend of acrobatics, flexibility, and fitness tutorials, showcasing her versatility and creativity in the realm of physical activity.
## **9. Chris Heria (THENX) (Male, US):**
With 7.7 million YouTube followers, Chris Heria shares his expertise in calisthenics and bodyweight training through his THENX channel, empowering viewers to achieve their fitness goals with minimal equipment.
## **10. Fausto Murillo (Male, Colombia):**
Fausto Murillo’s 6.5 million YouTube followers turn to his channel for dynamic workout routines and motivational content, reflecting his passion for fitness and dedication to helping others lead healthier lives.
