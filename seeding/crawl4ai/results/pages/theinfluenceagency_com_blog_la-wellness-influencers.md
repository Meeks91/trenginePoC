# 11 LA Wellness Influencers To Follow
Last Updated
March 15, 2023
Originally Published
October 26, 2021
[ Written by The Influence Agency ](https://theinfluenceagency.com/author/the-influence-agency)
No matter how busy life gets, wellness should always be a priority. After all, what’s the point of working hard if we won’t be well enough to reap what we sow?
Life is full of great things, and prioritizing wellness allows you to enjoy it all to the fullest! 
Ultimately, achieving wellness is a journey. Along the way, you’re going to want to pick up the best advice possible. [The Influence Agency](https://theinfluenceagency.com/about-us) has rounded up 11 LA [wellness influencers](https://theinfluenceagency.com/influencer-marketing/wellness) who have amazing tips and tricks you wouldn’t want to miss out on!
Read on to see who they are!
##  **Shayla Quinn |**[**@shaylaquinn**](https://www.instagram.com/shaylaquinn/)
Shayla Quinn is an actress and singer turned LA wellness influencer who’s dedicated to helping people find their flow!
As the creator of [@theyogainspiredlife](https://www.instagram.com/theyogainspiredlife/), a 12-week self-discovery program, she aims to share how yoga can positively impact literally every aspect of life. If you’re serious about achieving wellness, you _seriously_ need to give yoga a try!
##  **Allegra Rose B. |**[**@allegraroseb**](https://www.instagram.com/allegraroseb/)
Aside from the fact that she owns an _adorable_ little coastal cottage that she often features in her Instagram feed, Allegra Rose is lighting up the social media platform with incredible wellness content!
Whether you’re an eco-conscious person looking for the perfect plant-based and plastic-free shampoo and conditioner products, or simply want to know how to mix a refreshing orange lemonade tonic, Allegra has the answers!
##  **Jhánneu |**[**@jhanneu**](https://www.instagram.com/jhanneu/)
There are probably thousands of people out there who are willing to go down the road of sustainable living but don’t know exactly where to begin. If you’re one of those people, then Jhanneu is an influencer you should definitely follow!
This eco-hero is spreading the word on the sustainable and plastic-free alternatives we should all consider switching to. After all, ensuring the planet’s wellness is a vital component of personal wellness.
##  **Holly Owens |**[**@wellthybelly**](https://www.instagram.com/wellthybelly/)
Non-toxic living and holistic healing? Yes, please! Holly Owens is all about teaching everyone how to have a “wellthy belly”. From workouts to gut health and even bee venom therapy, she’s breaking down all the essentials!
Holly is currently followed by over 70K people on Instagram. Oh, and we thought it’s worth mentioning that Jennifer Aniston is one of those people. *OMG* 
##  **Amy |**[**@theluxilook**](https://www.instagram.com/theluxilook/)
Amy is the LA wellness influencer behind the incredible blog, [_theluxilook.com_](https://theluxilook.com/lifestyle/wellness/). She documents her honest opinions and adventures on everything wellness, as well as style and travel. 
Whether it’s a deep dive into the “Whole30” diet experience, or tips on how to create a perfect morning routine, Amy has got you covered with her useful and detailed content! 
##  **Amelia Edmondson |**[**@amelia.edmondson**](https://www.instagram.com/amelia.edmondson/)
Okay, but Amelia Edmondson’s Instagram feed is the epitome of a vintage dream. Her photos with that retro Volkswagen microbus are _everything!_
And aside from getting your daily dose of aesthetic posts in your feed, you’ll also find yourself double-tapping content on must-try herbal beverages and plant-based meals. She’s definitely one of the LA wellness influencers to follow!
##  **Cortney |**[**@projectsunny**](https://www.instagram.com/projectsunny/)
Looking for inspiration for fresh food and an active lifestyle? Then you’ve got to follow LA wellness influencer @projectsunny! 
Her Instagram page is actual proof that delicious food _can_ contribute to wellness at the same time. Cortney’s recipe for mushroom spinach quinoa even went viral on Pinterest! So you definitely don’t want to miss out on that one! And while you’re at it, don’t forget to try her recipe for roasted red pepper pasta and veggie bagel sandwiches as well. 
##  **Amarachi N. Ukachu |**[**@amarachiukachu**](https://www.instagram.com/amarachiukachu/)
Count on Amarachi Ukachu to provide the ultimate therapy and self-care resources! She shares all her favourite reads for healing, fitness routines, and impeccable style posts in between. Amarachi is also an in-demand partner for many of the world’s most popular fitness brands. 
After tapping that follow button, head on over to her blog, [amarachiukachu.com](http://www.amarachiukachu.com/), to find more amazing content!
##  **Jenn Im |**[**@injennim**](https://www.instagram.com/imjennim/)
Aside from eating healthy food and maintaining a fitness routine, do you know what else contributes to immense wellness? Reading. 
That’s why we love how Jenn Im has created a book club called “Curl Up Club“. It currently has over 73K followers on Instagram! 
She’s one of Instagram’s most recognized LA wellness influencers who’s also well-known for her popular [YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCgWfS_47YPVbKx5EK4FLm4A) with over 3 million subscribers. Here, she shares lifestyle content on fitness, food, and fashion!
##  **Jonelle Lewis |**[**@jonelleyoga**](https://www.instagram.com/jonelleyoga/)
Jonelle Lewis is an LA wellness influencer and yoga expert who will contribute incredible ideas to your fitness journey! With over 15 years of practicing yoga, you just know that she’s a total pro who’s worth following! You can catch her as a trainer on [Apple Fitness+](https://www.apple.com/apple-fitness-plus/). 
Learn more about her [enriching workshops and retreats](https://www.jonellelewis.com/workshops/mexico23) on her blog. 
##  **Chelsie Hill |**[**@chelsiehill**](https://www.instagram.com/chelsiehill/)
[Chelsie Hill](https://www.chelsiehill.com/about) believes that “dance is dance whether you’re walking or rolling”, and we couldn’t agree more with the inspiring vision of this trailblazer! 
As the founder behind [@rolletes_la](https://www.instagram.com/rollettes_la/), a wheelchair dance team, she continues to empower women with disabilities to live boundlessly. She’s proof that in order to achieve your dreams, all you have to do is keep moving!
