## List of the top 10 biggest TikTokers worldwide: Our methodology
Before we start, we'll give you a quick guide to the metrics we pulled from our database for each social media personality:
  * **Kolsquare Credibility Score** : This data point estimates an influencer's trustworthiness. Higher numbers indicate that most of their audience comprises real people (as opposed to fake or bought followers, for example). We consider a good Credibility Score to be anything over 70 (out of 100).
  * **Engagement rate** : To determine the engagement of influencers' audiences, the engagement rate calculates how many of their followers interact (comments, shares, likes, etc.) per post.
  * **Earned media value (**[**EMV**](https://www.kolsquare.com/en/industry-ranking/top-10-brands-in-the-uk-by-emv-on-tiktok)**)** : This estimates how much the buzz generated by an influencer's content would cost through traditional marketing channels. 


**Our methodology?** We filtered the Kolsquare database containing data about over three million KOLs to show only TikTok influencers with a Credibility Score above 70. Then we sorted by community size to discover the most popular creators.

### How to run an influencer marketing campaign with famous influencers TikTokers?
TikTok influencer marketing connects you with much of the UK population. It's also a bridge to cynical Gen Z audiences, as 76% of people aged between 18 and 24 use the app. 
In addition, while younger demographics dominate TikTok, its addictive algorithm appeals to all generations. More than [17% of users are aged over 45](https://www.statista.com/statistics/1299771/tiktok-global-user-age-distribution/#:~:text=TikTok%3A%20distribution%20of%20global%20audiences%202025%2C%20by%20age%20and%20gender&text=As%20of%20February%202025%2C%20it,percent%20of%20the%20platform's%20audience.). 
Marketers have a huge opportunity to reach large audiences where they're highly engaged and active. Below are some stats about TikTok: 
  * **High engagement rates:** [TikTok's average (4.07%) engagement rate is high](https://www.brandwatch.com/blog/good-engagement-rate-tiktok/). Collaborations with smaller influencers, such as nano or micro-influencers, yield even more engagements because their niche content feels more authentic. 
  * **Time spent on platform:** [UK users spend over 49 hours per month ](https://www.statista.com/statistics/1315572/uk-social-media-app-usage-monthly-time-spent/)on TikTok, likely due to the attention-grabbing, tailored algorithm. Don't miss out on this chance to connect! 
  * **TikTok Shop:** [Over half (56%) UK have made a purchase through social media platforms](https://www.retaileconomics.co.uk/retail-insights/thought-leadership-reports/the-power-of-social-commerce-building-brands-in-the-tiktok-era). New social commerce features make inspiring sales via social media easier than ever. 


TikTok users value genuine and relatable content, which is why influencers are authentic and relatable. 
To run a successful campaign, follow these tips:
  * **Define objectives and the target audience:** Do you want increased brand awareness, sales, or brand lift? Defining some specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and time-bound (SMART) goals keeps your campaign on track. In addition, pinpoint your target audience. This will affect which influencers you work with and what content you create.
  * **Choose the right influencers:** Choose influencers whose followers match your target audience's demographics and location. Look beyond follower counts to assess engagement rates and content quality. An influencer marketing tool like Kolsquare streamlines influencer selection with an extensive database, AI-driven search, and more. 
  * **Collaborate authentically:** Partner with influencers whose values align with your brand's. Give them creative freedom to ensure content feels genuine and engaging. Creators tend to know their audiences best, so collaborate on ideas and provide a brief, but don't go for overly scripted or high-production videos on this channel.
  * **Take advantage of TikTok's unique features:** Consider hashtag challenges, live streams, and short, snappy videos to captivate audiences. TikTok's algorithm analyses each video's engagement rate after it's posted rather than analysing follower size, which makes it possible to go viral on a budget
  * **Measure and optimise:** To increase visibility, you can boost influencer posts with TikTok's paid advertising options. In addition, track performance with KPIs like engagement rate, conversions, and EMV to refine strategies for future campaigns.
