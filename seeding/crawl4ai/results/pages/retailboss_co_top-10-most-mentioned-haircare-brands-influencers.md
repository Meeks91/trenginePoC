This RB report identifies and ranks the **Top 10 Most Mentioned Haircare Brands by Influencers in 2025** , based on a custom, dual platform ranking methodology. The analysis synthesizes data on **Earned Media Value (EMV)** from **TikTok** and **Strategic Presence** from **Instagram** , providing a comprehensive view of brand visibility and positive sentiment across the most influential social media channels.
The ranking is dominated by treatment focused, high performance brands and digitally native brands with strong aesthetic identities. [**Olaplex**](https://olaplex.com/) and [**Gisou**](https://gisou.com/) share the top rank, demonstrating the power of combining viral product efficacy with founder led, aspirational content. The results highlight that success in the haircare sector is driven by a blend of massive, product focused reach on **TikTok** and strategic, high quality engagement on **Instagram** , often centered around professional authority and celebrity influence.
## **Methodology: Custom Ranking Criteria**
To provide a comprehensive and nuanced ranking, a custom scoring system was developed, weighting the two most critical social media performance indicators (KPIs) for the haircare sector in **2025**.
**KPI** | **Weight (Max Points)** | **Data Source & Rationale**  
---|---|---  
**TikTok** Earned Media Value (EMV) Score | 70 points |  Based on the brand’s relative EMV on **TikTok**. This metric is a proxy for the total value generated by influencer mentions, capturing the sheer volume and positive financial impact of the brand’s visibility on the platform, often driven by product demonstrations and viral trends.  
**Instagram** Presence Score | 30 points |  Based on the brand’s consistent high performance and strategic engagement on **Instagram**. This measures the quality, strategic nature, and consistent positive sentiment of the mentions, often driven by aesthetic, founder led, or professional content.  
The total score for each brand is the sum of these two weighted components.

**Key Findings and Brand Analysis**
The **2025** haircare influencer market is defined by the success of brands that offer tangible results and a strong visual identity.
### **1. The Dual Platform Leaders: Olaplex and Gisou**
**Olaplex** and **Gisou** share the top rank with a Total Score of **95** , showcasing two distinct paths to dominance. **Olaplex** achieves its score through an industry leading **TikTok** EMV, driven by countless positive mentions and demonstrations of its viral product efficacy. **Gisou** , on the other hand, secures a perfect **30/30** for **Instagram Presence** , reflecting its founder led aesthetic and exceptional audience loyalty, which translates into high quality, aspirational content.
### **3. Cécred**
**Cécred** , launched by [**Beyoncé Knowles‑Carter**](https://www.beyonce.com/) , quickly entered hair Tok conversations in **2025** as one of the most buzzed about new haircare brands. **A**[**Mintoiro**](https://www.mintoiro.com/)**TikTok trend** report listed **Cécred** among the **Top 30 Haircare Brands Trending** on **TikTok** in **August 2025** , alongside long established names, reflecting a rapid rise in mentions for a new label.
### **4. Redken**
Professional brand **Redken** remains deeply woven into stylist led content and home care recommendations.**A Mintoiro TikTok** ranking of **Top 30 Haircare Brands Trending** on**TikTok** (**August 2025**) lists **Redken France** as one of the standout brands, alongside **Bleach London** , **Gisou** , **Kérastase** and **Olaplex** , highlighting the label’s strong creator campaigns and effective formulas.
Credit: Redken
### **5. K18**
**Biotech** hair brand **K18** dominated “hair repair” and “bond vs bond” conversations in **2025**. Its [**Peptide Prep Detox Shampoo**](https://www.k18hair.com/products/peptide-prep-detox-shampoo-8-5oz) and [**Molecular Repair Hair Mask**](https://www.k18hair.com/products/leave-in-molecular-repair-hair-mask-50-ml) appeared in multiple “**Top haircare launches of 2025** ” and “**best haircare of 2025** ” creator roundups, with influencers praising visible damage reversal and simplified routines.
Credit: Biotech K18
### **6. Amika**
Colorful, community driven **Amika** is one of **Gen Z’s** go to prestige hair brands at retailers like [**Sephora**](https://www.sephora.com/) and [**Ulta**](https://www.ulta.com/). A **2026** insight report on **Gen Z** haircare found that when shopping at **Ulta** and**Sephora** , **amika** is chosen by **23%** of **Gen Z** consumers, **ahead of Olaplex** (**20%**) and **Ouai (18%),** showing its strong share of mind at key beauty retailers.
Credit: Amika
### **7. Bleach London**
**UK** born **Bleach London** remains a cult favorite for **DIY color** and **pastel transformations**. The **Mintoiro August 2025 TikTok** **haircare** ranking names **Bleach London** among the**top 30** trending haircare brands, citing its vibrant color kits and strong resonance with younger audiences exploring bold shades at home.
Credit: Bleach London
### **8. Ouai**
Founded by stylist [**Jen Atkin**](https://www.instagram.com/jenatkinhair/?hl=en) , **Ouai** sits at the intersection of chic minimalism and fragrance forward haircare. In the **Beauty Contract’s Gen Z** hair oil survey, **Ouai** captured **22%** of premium hair oil users, second only to [**Moroccanoil**](https://ca.moroccanoil.com/) (**30%)** and ahead of **Olaplex, Gisou** and **Kérastase** , highlighting its strength in the hair oil category in **2025**.
### **9. Isima**
Emerging brand **Isima** gained traction as part of the new wave of haircare names surfacing in **Mintoiro’s TikTok** monitoring. While detailed public metrics remain limited compared with heritage brands, **Isima’s** inclusion in **Top 30** **Haircare Brands Trending** on **TikTok** (**August 2025**) signals rising mention volume and engagement among early adopter creators seeking fresh, niche options.
### **10. Kérastase**
Luxury salon brand **Kérastase** continues to set the benchmark for high end haircare. **Cosmetify’s Hair Care Report** ranked **Kérastase** as the number one hair care brand globally based on annual searches between **April 2023** and **March 2024** , ahead of **Gisou** and **K18** , which translated into strong visibility heading into **2025**.

### **The Rise of Treatment-Focused Brands**
The high placement of **K18** (Rank 4) and the continued strength of **Redken** (Rank 4) highlight the consumer demand for science backed, treatment focused haircare. **K18’s** success is a result of its disruptive technology and strong influencer focus on product results, while **Redken** leverages its professional authority to generate significant EMV on **TikTok**.
### **Celebrity and Aesthetic Influence**
The impact of celebrity is evident with **Cécred** (Rank 3), which achieved a high ranking shortly after its launch due to its high profile rollout and the cultural influence of its founder. Similarly, **Bleach London** (Rank 7) and**Amika** (Rank 6) succeed through their strong, recognizable visual identities and aesthetic packaging, which make them highly shareable and positively mentioned on **Instagram**.
### **Legacy Brand Resilience**
**Kérastase** (Rank 10) demonstrates the resilience of legacy brands. While its **TikTok** EMV is lower than the viral leaders, its high **Instagram Presence Score** reflects its premium positioning and sustained relevance among high value influencers and professional stylists. This indicates that for luxury and professional brands, a strategic, high quality presence can maintain a top tier ranking despite lower volume.
## **Conclusion**
The most positively mentioned haircare brands in **2025** are those that can effectively communicate product performance and brand identity across social media. The data confirms that a successful strategy requires a high volume, results driven approach on **TikTok** , coupled with a strategic, aesthetic, and authoritative presence on **Instagram**. The leaders in this category are masters of both viral product demonstration and aspirational brand storytelling.
