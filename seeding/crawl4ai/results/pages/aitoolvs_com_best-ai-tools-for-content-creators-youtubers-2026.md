## FAQ
Which AI tool do top YouTubers use most?
vidIQ and TubeBuddy for SEO optimization, Descript for editing, and Opus Clip for shorts creation are the most commonly used among full-time creators.
Can AI write entire YouTube scripts?
AI can generate solid first drafts, but the best creators use AI for structure and research, then add their personal voice and stories. Pure AI scripts tend to sound generic.
Is it ethical to use AI in content creation?
Yes, when used as a tool rather than a replacement. Most platforms allow AI-assisted content. The key is adding genuine value and being transparent with your audience about AI usage.
