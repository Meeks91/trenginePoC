## What is a Micro Influencer?
A [micro influencer](https://adparlor.com/glossary/term/micro-influencer/) is someone with a social following between 10K and 50K, and while their numbers might be smaller, their impact is anything but. These creators usually have tight-knit communities and higher engagement, since their followers are genuinely into their content and trust their recs. According to Sprout Social, micro influencers see around a 6% engagement rate on Instagram, compared to just 1.97% for mega-influencers with over a million followers. Most micro influencers focus on a specific niche, which keeps their audience super dialed in. Fashion micro influencers, for example, build loyal followings by sharing their personal style, outfit inspo, and everyday fashion finds. Because of that connection, followers often look to them for what to wear next. For brands that work with micro influencers, it’s a win – they get to tap into a trusted voice and reach a super-engaged, style-loving audience. Plus, it builds a shared sense of loyalty, as [followers connect with the brand ](https://apparelmagic.com/evolving-marketing-strategies-the-rise-of-micro-influencers-in-fashion-retail/)through a mutual love for the influencer’s style. With that, let’s get into the top fashion micro influencers making waves in 2025!
