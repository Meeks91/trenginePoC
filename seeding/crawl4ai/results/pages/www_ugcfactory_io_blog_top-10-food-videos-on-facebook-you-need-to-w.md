The platform has transformed into a central meeting place for individuals interested in food. The platform currently has over 2.23 billion active users monthly, who demonstrate that food videos are dominating Facebook. The shared interest in online videos increases daily as cooking videos on Facebook steadily climb to the top alongside other video content. All food-related content from simple recipes to cooking solutions and meal ideas exists on Facebook through its many pages.
The following article examines the most impactful Facebook cooking videos and pages. The pages showcase both high popularity and a captivating presentation that includes quick recipes and expansive cultural food content.
## **1. Tasty: The King of Food Videos**
Tasty has become a well-known entity for producing famous videos on Facebook. Tasty commands a following of more than 96 million users as they specialize in producing brief attractive cooking videos for Facebook.
Users can easily understand their recipes because text overlays provide instructions alongside visual step-by-step guides despite lacking audio.
Why it’s popular:
  * Quick, engaging recipes.
  * Diverse content, from desserts to savory dishes.
  * Fun extras like polls and quizzes.


Tasty also runs spin-off pages like Tasty Junior and Proper Tasty, catering to different audiences. If you’re new to food videos, this is the perfect place to start.
## **2. Food Network: Bringing TV Magic to Facebook**
The Food Network isn’t just a TV channel; it’s also a massive hit on Facebook. Their page features everything from quick recipes to behind-the-scenes looks at their shows.
What to expect:
  * Recipes from famous chefs.
  * Short clips of confectionery art.
  * Sneak peeks of new shows.


If you love cooking competitions or want to learn from the pros, this page is a must-follow.
## **3. Tastemade: A Global Food Adventure**
Tastemade focuses on experiencing international dishes through its platform. Their Facebook fanbase reaches 30 million subscribers who watch their blended videos that combine both user-generated and original content.
Highlights:
  * Short, 45-second recipes.
  * Focus on international dishes.
  * Engaging storytelling.


A baked coconut dessert recipe video from the page gained 20 million views among viewers. Foodies who belong to the millennial generation will find entertainment here.
## **4. Tipsy Bartender: Cocktails with a Twist**
Hosting a party? Tipsy Bartender has you covered. This page, run by cocktail expert Skyy John, features creative drink recipes that are as fun to make as they are to drink.
Why it stands out:
  * Over 21 million followers.
  * Eye-catching cocktail presentations.
  * Easy-to-follow recipes.


From colorful layered drinks to classic cocktails, Tipsy Bartender makes mixology accessible to everyone.
## **5. BuzzFeed Food: More Than Just Recipes**
BuzzFeed Food is another gem from the BuzzFeed family. With 23 million followers, this page offers longer, more detailed recipes compared to Tasty.
What you’ll find:
  * Multi-course meal ideas.
  * Creative kitchen hacks.
  * Ingredient-focused videos.


If you love experimenting in the kitchen, this page will keep you inspired for hours.
## **6. Goodful: Healthy Eating Made Fun**
Goodful is all about healthy living. Created by BuzzFeed, this page focuses on nutritious recipes that cater to various diets, like vegan and keto.
Why it’s unique:
  * Recipes for special diets.
  * No annoying ads.
  * Focus on wellness.


Whether you’re looking for low-carb dinners or protein-packed snacks, Goodful has something for everyone.
## **7. So Yummy: Spicing Up Everyday Cooking**
So Yummy is all about making everyday meals exciting. With 21.5 million followers, this page offers quick recipes, food hacks, and holiday meal ideas.
What makes it special:
  * Easy-to-follow videos.
  * Perfect for busy home cooks.
  * Fun, creative recipes.


If you’re tired of the same old meals, So Yummy will help you mix things up.
## **8. 12 Tomatoes: Simple Recipes for Everyone**
12 Tomatoes is part of the GreaterGood network and focuses on making cooking fun and accessible. With 9 million followers, this page is perfect for families.
Why it’s great:
  * Budget-friendly recipes.
  * Simple, no-fuss instructions.
  * Comfort food classics.


From Dump Cake to Pumpkin Pie, 12 Tomatoes proves that cooking doesn’t have to be complicated.
## **9. Delish: Maximum Engagement**
Delish is a powerhouse on Facebook, with an engagement rating of 35 million over three months. Their videos, often titled “How to Make,” are straightforward and highly shareable.
What to watch for:
  * High-quality recipe videos.
  * Viral hits like primavera stuffed chicken.
  * Easy-to-navigate format.


Delish is proof that great content doesn’t need to be overly complicated.
## **10. Food Insider: Exploring the World of Food**
Food Insider, the food division of INSIDER, takes a unique approach to food videos. Instead of just recipes, they explore what makes food delicious and dive into food culture.
What sets it apart:
  * Educational content.
  * Profiles of famous restaurants.
  * Insights into global cuisines.


If you’re a food nerd, this page is a treasure trove of information.
