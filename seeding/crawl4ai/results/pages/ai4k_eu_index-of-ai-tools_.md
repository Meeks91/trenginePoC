## AI Agents
An [**AI agent**](https://ai4k.eu/content/how-ai-agents-can-boost-your-online-business-and-where-to-learn-for-free/ "AI Agents") is a software that perceives its environment, learns from data, and makes decisions autonomously. Unlike **automation tools** , which follow predefined rules, AI agents can adapt, handle unstructured data, and manage complex tasks with minimal human input.
### **Key Differences:**
  * **Decision-Making:** AI agents learn and adjust, automation tools follow fixed scripts.
  * **Flexibility:** AI adapts to new situations, automation requires manual updates.
  * **Task Complexity:** AI handles reasoning-based tasks, automation works best for repetitive processes.


Example: An AI chatbot learns from interactions, while an automated bot follows a strict script.
