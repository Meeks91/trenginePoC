You get detailed reports showcasing the impact of your campaign through metrics that are relevant to your goals. Here are some of the top influencer marketing campaign metrics that you can expect to see from these agencies.
  * **Impressions –** A measure of the number of times the influencer content is displayed to TikTok users. 
  * **Reach –** A measure of the number of times people see the influencer content.
  * **Video views –** The number of unique views collected by influencer-generated videos for the campaign.
  * **Shares –** How many times the influencer videos are shared with other users.
  * **Engagements –** The total number of engagements received for all the influencer-generated videos for the campaign. 
  * **Click-through rate/clicks –** A measure of the number of times people clicked on a link shared by influencers for the campaign.
  * **Follower growth –** The number of followers you gained after launching your campaign.
  * **Number of unique content pieces –** The number of unique content pieces generated around the campaign. This metric may not only measure influencer-generated content pieces but all the fan-created videos resulting from the campaign.
  * **Cost-per-impression (CPM) –** How much you have to pay for every 1,000 impressions or views.
  * **Cost-per-engagement (CPE) –** How much you have to pay for each engagement generated by the campaign.
