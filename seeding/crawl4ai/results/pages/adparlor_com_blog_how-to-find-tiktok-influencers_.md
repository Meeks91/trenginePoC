## **What Makes TikTok Influencers So Impactful (And Why You Need to Know How to Find TikTok Influencers)**

Before you dive into how to find TikTok influencers who drive engagement, it’s important to understand why TikTok’s algorithm matters.
The algorithm—no one does it better than TikTok. Its algorithm is designed for discovery, which means creators can build massive followings faster than on any other platform, even without years of content. So don’t be quick to discredit new creators.
Sure, they can reach a massive audience, but can they actually engage? Absolutely. In fact, they drive some of the highest engagement rates across social media. When thinking about how to find TikTok influencers, authenticity should be at the top of your checklist.
Unlike other ultra-polished influencer marketing platforms (*cough* *cough* Instagram), TikTok thrives on realness. Influencers show up without filters, post from their bedrooms, and share personal moments; audiences love it.

TikTok influencers create a unique sense of intimacy and trust, which is critical when recommending products or services. Learning how to find TikTok influencers who naturally embody trust and authenticity is key to building long-term loyalty for your brand.

## **FAQs**
**How do I reach out to TikTok influencers?**
Start by checking their TikTok bio for an email or management info. If that’s a dead end, try Instagram or finding their info through an influencer marketing platform. If you’re serious about finding TikTok influencers and connect with them, a simple DM can also be an option; just keep it professional.
**How do you search for creators on TikTok?**
If you know their username, simply search for it. If not, try keywords and hashtags or track their content across Instagram or YouTube. Creators love to repurpose content, helping you backtrack to their original account, making how to find TikTok influencers easier.

Depends on who you ask. However, big names like Charli D’Amelio and Khaby Lame lead in followers, but that doesn’t mean they’re the right fit. Focus on influencers who align with your niche, tone, and campaign goals.
**How do I determine if a TikTok influencer is a good fit for my campaign?**
Check their content, consistency, engagement rate, audience demo, and how they work with brands. Would their style make sense for your brand? If the answer is yes, you might have a winner.
