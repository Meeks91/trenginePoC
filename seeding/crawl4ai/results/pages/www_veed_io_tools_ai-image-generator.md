How do I use AI to turn words into art? 
Creating effective AI images starts with writing descriptive prompts. Try this simple formula:
  * Subject + action + setting + style
  * Example: "A golden retriever surfing on ocean waves, digital art style"


More descriptive prompts produce better images. Include details about lighting, colors, perspective, and mood. Experiment with different prompts and refine based on initial results.
