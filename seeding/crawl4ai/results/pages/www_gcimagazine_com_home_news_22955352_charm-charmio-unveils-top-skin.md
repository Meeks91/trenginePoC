# Charm.IO Unveils Top Skin Care Trends on TikTok
Nov 21st, 2025
The report provides readers with valuable insights into the top brands, products and trends in skin care right now.
ckybe
Charm.IO has released a new report analyzing the latest trends in skin care, with special attention to what's trending on TikTok Shop. The report breaks down top hashtags, what's generating revenue, bestselling skin care shops and products and DTC skin care brands by success metrics. 
The report dives into TikTok Shop's data from January through August 2025, covering the latest in skin care across TikTok's $309 million in U.S. sales. From Korean skin care brands like Medicube, Dr. Melaxin and Anua to top trending searches for #glassskin, #hyperpigmentation and #glowingskin, Charm.IO has all the stats on what's leading the online beauty game.
