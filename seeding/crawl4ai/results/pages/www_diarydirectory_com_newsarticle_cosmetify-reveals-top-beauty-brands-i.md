####  Cosmetify reveals top beauty brands, influencers and products in Beauty Index 2024 
Date posted on DIARY directory: Monday 25th November 2024 
Beauty comparison platform, **Cosmetify** has released its annual Beauty Index for 2024.
**Hottest Beauty Brands** - ranked after collecting and using data centred around Instagram followers, Instagram engagement, Instagram mentions, Google searches and change in searches.
  * Sol De Janeiro
  * Charlotte Tilbury
  * Fenty Beauty
  * Drunk Elephant
  * Rhode
  * Dior Beauty
  * ELF Cosmetics
  * Glossier
  * Laneige
  * Bubble Skincare


**The Most Searched-For Brands** - by analysing the brand’s social media following and its annual Google search volume, Cosmetify has uncovered the top 5 beauty brands.
  * La Roche Posay
  * CeraVe
  * Sol de Janeiro
  * Charlotte Tilbury
  * The Ordinary


**The Most Powerful Beauty Influencers** - Cosmetify analysed statistics such as follower count on YouTube and Instagram, and potential income from sponsored posts.
  * James Charles
  * Mari Maria
  * Bretman Rock


**Rising Stars of Beauty** - beauty brands which have seen the biggest surge in interest over the past year.
  * Phlur 400% increase
  * Rhode 232% increase
  * Summer Fridays 173% increase


**The Beauty Brands Losing Popularity** - beauty brands experiencing the biggest declines in interest over the past year.
  * Nudestix - 33% decrease
  * Olaplex - 66.64% decrease
  * Pink Honey - 18% decrease


**Biggest Social Following** - the beauty brands with the biggest social media followings on Instagram and TikTok.
Most followed:
  * Huda Beauty 64.4m Combined IG & TikTok followers
  * MAC Cosmetics 25.2m Combined IG & TikTok followers
  * Fenty Beauty 16.0m Combined IG & TikTok followers


Best engagement:
  * Juliette Has A Gun 59.68%
  * e.l.f. Cosmetics 5.11%
  * Lancôme 5.04%


**Spotlight on K-beauty** - searches for ‘Korean beauty’ have increased by 53% year-on-year; ‘Korean skincare’ searches are also up 83% year-on-year.
  * Laneige
  * Beauty of Joseon
  * Cosrx
  * Anua
  * Missha


**Cosmetify’s Most Popular K-beauty Products** - top 10 most popular K-Beauty products.
  * Eqqualberry Daily Peeling Swimming Pool Toner
  * CosRx Advanced Snail 96 Mucin Power Essence 
  * Innisfree Dewy Glow Jelly Cream With Jeju Cherry Blossom
  * Laneige Lip Sleeping Mask
  * Laneige Neo Essential Blurring Finish Powder
  * CosRx Advanced Snail 92 All In One Cream
  * Mediheal Retinol Collagen Lifting Pad
  * CosRx Aloe Soothing Sun Cream SPF 50
  * ANUA Peach 70% Niacinamide Serum
  * CosRx Hydrium Green Tea Aqua Soothing Gel Cream


**Cosmetify’s Hottest Beauty Products 2024** - the most sought-after beauty products that Cosmetify users have been actively searching for throughout the course of 2024.
  * The Ordinary Niacinamide 10% + Zinc 1%
  * Olaplex No.3 Hair Perfector
  * Ecoco Eco Styler Olive Oil Styling Gel
  * Answr At-home Keratin Treatment
  * Pura Collagen Glow+ Advanced Collagen PLUS Formula
  * ELEMIS Pro-Collagen Cleansing Balm
  * Medik8 Advanced Night Restore
  * Clarins Hand & Nail Treatment Cream
  * ARIANA GRANDE Thank U Next Eau De Parfum
  * Murad Hydrating AHA/BHA Exfoliating Cleanser
