You’ve been using Google for probably your whole life… …but are you actually using it right? 🤨 Sounds familiar? 😏 Hey lovelies 💞 It’s another Tech Tip Tuesday 🥳 Last time, I shared some Google Search tricks that made a lot of you go: “Wait… WHAT?!” 😳 (in case you missed it, I'll drop the link in the comments 😌) So today, we’re continuing with Part 2 - more sneaky Google hacks that make searching faster, smarter, and way less frustrating 😌 Before I start, SAVE THIS post 📌 You’ll thank me later 😉 Okayyy… let’s gooooo 🚀 1️⃣ Fill in the blanks with * Forgot a word in a sentence or maybe an idiom? Use * and let Google help you out. Example: 👉 "burning the * at both ends" Google fills in "candle" instantly ✅ Perfect when you remember most of a phrase but not all of it. 2️⃣ Search only in page titles Want Google to focus on the main topic of a page? Example: 👉 intitle:"Top AI tools for 2025" This shows only pages where that phrase appears in the title, not buried somewhere in the content. 3️⃣ Search words inside URLs Looking for pages that are really about a topic? Example: 👉 inurl:automation Google now shows pages that actually have automation in the link itself. Less noise. Better results 😌 4️⃣ See deleted or old pages (Google’s little time machine ⏳) Ever clicked a link and it’s suddenly gone? 😩 Try this: 👉 cache: [website.com/page](https://www.linkedin.com/redir/redirect?url=http%3A%2F%2Fwebsite%2Ecom%2Fpage&urlhash=k6Z-&trk=public_post-text) Google shows a saved version of the page; like travelling back in time before it disappeared 😌 5️⃣ Reverse image search - but smarter Upload an image and Google shows similar images and where it came from. Want better results? After uploading, add a simple word to guide Google. Example: Upload a picture of a dress → type white Google shows similar white dresses 👌 Or upload a room photo → type minimalist Google shows similar room designs 😜 It’s like saying: “This image… but this style.” 6️⃣ Search within a range (price or year) Want results between certain numbers? Examples: 👉 laptop $500..$1000 👉 Olympics 2000..2012 results Perfect when you want focused results without endless scrolling 😮💨 And that’s it 😌 Start using these today and watch how much easier and faster Google becomes. No stress. No scrolling marathon. Just better answers 😜 Happy Tuesday, my lovelies 💞 Hugs and kisses 🤗😘 I’m [Stephanie](https://ng.linkedin.com/in/stephaniechisom?trk=public_post-text), the tech princess 👸 and your LinkedIn Sweetie ✨ PS: Which one of these surprised you the most? 🤔 Be a darling and repost ♻️ Let's spread the knowledge 😉
