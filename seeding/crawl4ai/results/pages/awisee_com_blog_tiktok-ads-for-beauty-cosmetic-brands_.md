High-performing hooks for **TikTok ads for beauty cosmetic brands** include:
  * A fast transformation clip
  * A creator saying, “I tested this for 7 days…”

  * A texture close-up

Beauty shoppers care about detail. Testing different lighting, skin types, or application styles helps your **cosmetics TikTok ads strategy** stay strong.
