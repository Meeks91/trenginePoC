## **Features to Consider in A Free AI Content Creator Tool**
Let us highlight some of the major features of AI Content creator tools below.
  *     * **Ease of Use** : It is important that AI content creators provide a simple and user friendly interface so that even beginners can generate content using the tool.
    * **Content Quality:** The AI content creators must produce quality and relevant contents suitable for users or anyone grasping the content online. 
    * **Customization Options** : These AI content creators must provide users with options to change the format, tone or style of the content created. It must be flexible enough to adapt to different use cases such as social media, blogs, email campaigns, and more.
    * **Templates** : These tools must have pre built templates based on different types of content as this will save time and provide a starting inspiration if someone is not sure where to start.
    * **Language options** : These AI tools must be able to offer multilingual support which makes it useful for global users. Also, there must be options to switch tones to align content or make it suitable for different audience.
    * **SEO Optimization Support** : AI Content creators tool must offer some level of SEO optimization including meta descriptions, keyword integrations, and more. 
    * **Plagiarism Free:** Content generated by these AI tools must be free from plagiarism with no duplication issues. Also, grammar correction is a plus. 
    * **Collaboration Support:** These free AI content creators tool must offer team sharing options where teams can work together on content.

## **Limitations of Free AI Content Creators**
There are many limitations offered by these Free AI content creators tool mentioned below. 
  * Free AI Content creators tool offers many limitations such as word limits, restricted features, limited customization and more. 
  * AI Content creators generally remix and rephrase already available information to create content which might lack originality, creativity, and deep insights. 
  * Sometimes AI misses the depth of the context, tone sarcasm or the cultural references leading to awkward or inaccurate output.
  * AI tools might hallicunate and create inaccurate content especially when there are stats involved. Hence, it become important to cross check for facts before using.
  * AI still fails to capture the unique brand voice or convey deep emotion in writing even after many level of fine tuning. 
  * There are always risks of copyright issues, plagiarism, misleading content if not carefully monitored.
