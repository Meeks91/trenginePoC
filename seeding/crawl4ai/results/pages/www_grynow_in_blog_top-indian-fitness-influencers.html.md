##  Who are Fitness Influencers?
Fitness influencers are the key opinion leaders of the fitness niche, where, through their personal experience and regimes, they guide their followers to achieve physical fitness. Through their content, they spread awareness about fitness routines, exercises, diets, supplements, nutrition, workout equipment, recovery methods, etc.
To add to that, as awareness around fitness continues to grow, these fitness influencers in India have taken charge. They target fitness to increase physical exercise awareness and impact global physical health, which holds a lot of promise for a sustainable world. They motivate their followers to guide them about activities like running, going to the gym, cardio, exercising, etc., and having a healthy routine, good physique, and sane mental health.
## Types of Fitness Instagrammers in India
### 1. Personal Trainer
Personal trainers help individuals reach their target fitness objectives. Through their video content and personal lessons, they support the involved individual.
### 2. Fitness Equipment Trainers / Gym Trainers
Fitness equipment is one of the basic tenets of realizing goals in the fitness arena. The creators help people with the equipment with its correct use and to get muscles toned/gain strength.
### 3. Creators Guiding Weight Loss
A higher body mass index is one of the major deteriorating factors of global health. To control it and get fit, the influencers, either through personal experience or a textbook approach, try to do it.
### 4. Yoga Trainers
Yoga is considered for its physical and mental benefits. It helps in fighting a lot of ill elements like deteriorating mental health, poor physical health, improving consciousness, enhancing focus, etc.
