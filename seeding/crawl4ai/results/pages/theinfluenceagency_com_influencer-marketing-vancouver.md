## Have questions?We have answers
### What does an influencer agency in Vancouver do for a local brand?
Our influencer agency plans the strategy, writes the brief, finds/vets BC based or Canada focused creators, manages approvals, launches posts, run paid amplification if needed, and reports on results with clear next steps.
### How much does influencer marketing cost in Vancouver?
Costs vary by niche, deliverables, audience size, and planned usage. Our influencer marketing services begin at 10k; 75% of that budget is dedicated to influencer fees and paid amplification; 25% management fees dedicated to sourcing, management, negotiation and contracting, execution, and reporting. Contact us for a quote that estimates reach, content volume, and media support.
### Which is better for us, a Vancouver full service agency or specialist influencer shop?
We recommend choosing full service if you want creators, paid social, and creative in one plan. We recommend choosing a specialist if you only need influencer strategy and execution with faster turnarounds
### How do I find real Vancouver creators not fake followers?
We recommend checking audience location in creator insights, review engagement quality not just rate, look at past brand posts, and ask for screenshot proof before booking. With a roster of over 2,500,000 social media influencers, we’re confident we can find the perfect fit for your next marketing campaign.
### What KPIs should I track for Vancouver campaigns?
We’ll track the success of your influencer marketing campaign across a variety of metrics that are relevant to you. Maybe you're looking to generate leads, or perhaps just increase your social media engagement,we'll keep tabs for you.
### How can micro-influencers be effective for my brand in Vancouver?
Micro-influencers often have highly-engaged fanbases. They're great options if you're looking to market a more niche product or service to a group of likely-to-be-interested people. They're often better fits than superstar influencers!
### Does Canada require ad disclosure for sponsored posts in Vancouver?
Yes, disclose any material connection clearly and near the content using simple labels like ad or sponsored and follow platform tools.
