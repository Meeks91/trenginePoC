# 2023-2024 Top Beauty Influencers & Brands
Jan 25th, 2024

A trio of recent reports outlines beauty's top influencers and brands across categories and social media platforms, each leveraging a different set of metrics to uncover insights for marketing and social teams.
## Top Influencers & Brands on TikTok
[Cosmetify's recent report ](https://www.cosmetify.com/us/tiktok-beauty-index/)measured brands and influencers against the number of TIkTok followers, videos and hashtag views they generated in 2023.
The top three color cosmetic brands in this sector were:
  1. Huda Beauty 
  2. Rare Beauty 
  3. Florence By Mills


Each brand is fronted by a notable celebrity, it should be noted.
By the same metrics, the top TikTok skin care brands were:
  1. The Ordinary 
  2. CeraVe 
  3. Nivea 


TikTok's top influencers were:
  1. Kylie Jenner 
  2. James Charles 
  3. Abby Roberts 


## Fastest-growing Beauty Influencers
In assessing the fastest-growing beauty influencers, [Dash Hudson's (admittedly non-exhaustive) ranking](https://www.dashhudson.com/blog/top-beauty-influencers) focused on those with the most rabid new follower growth rates between January 1, 2023 and September 1, 2023 (see report for methodology).
These influencers span categories including color cosmetics, skin care, hair care, fragrance and nail care.

## Top Category Influencers by VIT
[Traackr's latest list of top influencers by category](https://www.traackr.com/blog/top-beauty-influencers-2023) leveraged a "VIT" measurement that reportedly enables brands to measure influencer content performance across platforms in an apples-to-apples manner (visit the report for more information on methodology, etc.).
Accoring to the firm, the top color cosmetic influencers are:
  1. Kylie Jenner 
  2. Selena Gomez 
  3. Jennifer Lopez
  4. Ariana Grande 
  5. Sol Pena 


Top skin care influencers include:
  1. Hailey Rhode Baldwin Bieber 
  2. Kim Kardashian 
  3. Dr. Muneeb Shah 
  4. Dr. Dustin Portela 
  5. Dr. Angelo Landriscina 


Notably, many on the top performers list are dermatologists.
The top hair care influencers are:
  1. Zeph Sanders 
  2. Anthony Claxton 
  3. Daryl Ann Denner 
  4. Chris Olsen 
  5. Destiny Lashae 


Finally, the top fragrance influencers are:
  1. Billie Eilish 
  2. Lina Noory 
  3. Emma Watson 
  4. Paul Fino 
  5. Sofia Richie Grainge
