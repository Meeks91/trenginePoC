## What Happening
Like everybody else, I was getting into **ChatGPT,** and all the buzz came around it, I was immersed there for a while and raised my head to look around to find that **ChatGPT** is old news, there was something even cooler: **open-source LLMs**. You can download and play with these endless digital playgrounds on your computer. You can hop from one model to another, exploring all sorts of stuff they can do. They’re super diverse, designed for all kinds of tasks, and have huge potential to solve just about any problem you can think of.
## Possible Limitations
While diving into open-source LLMs is thrilling, it’s not without its hitches. One major speed bump I hit was the lack of proper hardware. These models are power-hungry beasts, and without the right setup, you’re not getting the full show. This snag nudged me to scout for alternative playgrounds. In addition to the hardware bump, The complexities and technicalities of setting up and utilizing these models can pose significant challenges. Understanding their intricate configurations, managing dependencies, and optimizing performance requires a steep learning curve.
Okay, I needed a way to play around with these LLMs without my old computer and newbie brain holding me back. So, I went on a hunt for awesome places online where I can access platforms, labs, and playgrounds for free.
Guess what? I struck gold! let me show you what I’ve found.

## CHAT.LMSYS.ORG
[LMSYS Org](https://chat.lmsys.org/) is a project that aims to democratize the field of large models through open research. to encourage community involvement and collaboration, and to be part of the AI evolving process.
one round of battle on chat.lmysys.org
## Projects
LMSYS Org is involved in several groundbreaking projects that push the boundaries of what is possible with large models. These projects are categorized into evaluation tools, systems, datasets, and models.
here is a non-exhausted list of what they have:
### Evaluation Tools
offers three evaluation tools: **Chatbot Arena** , a platform for LLM battles with Elo ratings; **Arena Hard** , an automated pipeline for generating tough benchmarks; and **MT-Bench** , a set of challenging questions for chat assistant evaluation.
### Systems
They have several systems for fine-tuning, runtime interface, etc, the most notable one is **FastChat** : Which is an open and scalable platform for training, fine-tuning, serving, and evaluating LLM-based chatbots.
### Datasets
They offer three datasets: **LMSYS-Chat-1M** with 1M real-world LLM conversations, **Chatbot Arena Conversations** with 33K human-preferred conversations, and **ToxicChat** with 10K entries for content moderation.
### Models
With three chatbots: **Vicuna** , a Llama-based model with 90% of ChatGPT’s quality; **LongChat** , also Llama-based but designed for long context conversations; and **FastChat-T5** , a compact yet powerful commercial-friendly option.
from all their project what I’m interested in as a beginner is the following:
### **⚔️ Arena (battle):**
it’s like a fight club for chatbots! where different language models go head-to-head, and you get to decide who wins. You chat with two anonymous AIs, then vote on who did a better job. This helps rank the chatbots, so we can see which ones are the most helpful and fun to talk to. Think of it like a popularity contest for AIs, with you as the judge!
### **⚔️ Arena (side-by-side):**
in this section, You pick two chatbots from tens available, ask them the same question, and watch their answers pop up right next to each other. It’s like a fun quiz show but with super-smart AIs!, you can easily see which one gives the best answer. It’s the perfect tool if you’re curious about how different AIs think or just want to have some fun comparing them head-to-head.
### **💬 Direct Chat:**
here you will have a one-to-one conversation with a single chatbot of your choice. Unlike the ⚔️ Arena (side-by-side) where two chatbots are compared, Direct Chat allows users to focus on interacting with and evaluating the performance of one specific model at a time. It’s a great way to get a feel for the chatbot’s personality, knowledge base, and conversational abilities in a more focused setting.
### 👀 Vision Direct Chat:
LMSYS just dropped a super cool feature called Vision Direct Chat! You can chat with AI about any picture you throw at it. Just upload a pic and ask away — the AI will tell you what it sees, answer your questions, or even make up a funny story about it.
### 🏆 Leaderboard:
The LMSYS Leaderboard is like the scoreboard of the AI world, showing which chatbot is the reigning champ. It’s got two parts:
  * **Arena Leaderboard:** This one’s all about popularity. It’s based on over a million votes from people who’ve battled different chatbots in the Arena. It even shows you who’s the best at specific skills, like coding or answering long questions.
  * **Full Leaderboard:** This one’s a bit more serious. It combines scores from three tests: the Arena battles, a tough question challenge (MT-Bench), and a general knowledge test (MMLU) test. It’s like the ultimate report card for chatbots.


> So, whether you’re just curious about which AI is the most popular or you want to know who’s really got the brains, the LMSYS Leaderboard has got you covered.
## **Together AI Playground**
Have you ever wished for an easy way to experiment with cutting-edge AI models?
### **What’s Together AI All About?**
from their documentation:
> Together AI makes it easy to run or fine-tune leading open source models with only a few lines of code.
In a nutshell, Together AI makes using AI super accessible. You can:
  * Run and test Various Open-Source AI Models.
  * Fine-tune and customize Models with just a few simple commands.
  * Need Serious Power?: If you’re into heavy-duty AI projects, they’ve got you covered with private GPU clusters.


### **The Playground: Our Interested part**
The [Together AI Playground](https://api.together.ai/) is where the real fun begins! It’s a web interface where you can interact with different types of models:
  * **Chat Playground:** Have conversations with AI chatbots and even tweak their personalities.
  * **Language Playground:** Write text together with AI language models, great for creative writing or getting help with drafts.
  * **Image Playground:** Generate images from text prompts, and unleash your inner artist!
  * **Code Playground:** Work with AI to write code, a handy tool for programmers.


Ready to jump in? Just log in to [api.together.xyz](https://api.together.xyz/playground/), you need to register first and it’s free, then choose your playground, pick a model, and start experimenting!

## Meta.ai: Chatting with Llama 3 Models
Meta has this cool new thing called Meta.ai where you can chat with their super smart Llama 3 AI models.
### What’s the Buzz about Llama 3?
Llama 3 is Meta’s latest brainy AI model, coming in two sizes: 8B and 70B parameters. The 70B one is the real powerhouse. It’s like the superhero of AI, beating a lot of other models in tasks like coding, reasoning, and following instructions.

### Chatting with Meta AI
Meta’s integrated Llama 3 into their [Meta AI assistant](https://www.meta.ai/), which you can now chat with on Meta.ai, It even can pull in real-time search results, and it can construct new images from text descriptions and even make them move! The image game is strong with this one.

## Cohere AI
Cohere is a company that’s using cutting-edge AI technology to help businesses of all sizes. They’ve got a bunch of cool AI models that can understand and respond to complex business data, like financial reports and stock prices. It’s like having a super smart virtual assistant that never gets tired!

### Command R+: Cohere’s Latest Model
Cohere’s latest model, Command R+, is a big deal in the world of AI. It’s designed to handle NLP tasks like summarization and text generation. What’s cool is that it’s ranking high among both open-source and closed-source models, thanks to its advanced capabilities.
### Cohere Playground
Cohere has a cool feature called the “[**Playground**](https://dashboard.cohere.com/playground/chat)” where you can test and play with their AI models. It’s like a sandbox where you can experiment with the models and see how they can be used for different business needs. So, if you’re a business looking to harness the power of AI, definitely check out Cohere. They’re making some serious waves in the world of AI-for-enterprise!

## Groq.com
### Blazing-Fast Inference
Groq is a company that offers **inference speed** as a service, Their secret weapon is their LPU Inference Engine.
## [Groq builds the world's fastest AI inference technology The LPU™ Inference Engine by Groq is a hardware and software platform that delivers exceptional compute speed, quality… groq.com ](https://groq.com/?source=post_page-----8b57bea84ade---------------------------------------)
The heart of Groq’s technology is the **Language Processing Unit (LPU)** Inference Engine. This specialized processing system is a speed demon, designed to handle the immense computational demands of large language models (LLMs) like the ones powering chatbots and virtual assistants.
## **Interesting Parts for Us**
### Making AI Accessible: Open-Source Models
[Groq](https://groq.com/) isn’t just about proprietary solutions; they also believe in open access to AI technology. They offer various **open-source models** that anyone can use, including LLaMA3 8b, LLaMA3 70b, Mixtral 8x7b, and Gemma 7b.
### Playground for Developers: Test Drive Groq’s Tech
Groq doesn’t leave developers out in the cold. They offer a [**playground platform**](https://console.groq.com/playground) where you can experiment with their technology and see how it can supercharge your AI projects, and it’s free for now including their API.

## Conclusion
Open-source LLMs have made AI way more accessible, giving us a ton of cool playgrounds to experiment and have fun with. Platforms like Poe.com, LMSYS, and others mentioned above, let you play around with different AI models for free. These platforms are great for everyone, from newbies just starting to seasoned pros who want to get their hands dirty. You can do everything from chatting with AI bots to customizing models to fit your needs. While AI is still a growing field, these open-source playgrounds are a fantastic opportunity to see what AI can do and be part of the future. So go ahead, explore, and have some fun!
