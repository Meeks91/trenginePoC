### Research skills include the ability to find, evaluate, analyse and synthesize information from various sources.
These skills are essential for higher education, as they enable you to conduct independent research, write in an appropriate academic style, and actively participate in scholarly discussions.
Research skills also help you develop critical thinking, problem-solving, and creativity, all of which are invaluable for your future career prospects and personal development.
