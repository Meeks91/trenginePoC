# The Future of B2B Marketing Is on TikTok: 10 AI Creators to Watch
### by | Jun 17, 2025
If we say “TikTok,” what pops into your head? Probably dance trends, GRWM vids, and someone shouting “run, don’t walk!” to the newest brunch spot in NYC.
And sure — TikTok _did_ start as the home of lip-syncs and lifestyle content. But here’s what most B2B brands are still missing: TikTok has grown up. Fast.
If you’ve avoided the platform thinking it’s “not for your audience,” you’re not alone. But here’s a little déjà vu — remember how B2B brands once shrugged off Instagram? TikTok is following the same curve. And in 2025, it’s not just a place to scroll — [it’s a full-blown ecosystem for B2B creators](https://martech.org/b2b-marketing-on-tiktok-what-you-need-to-know/).
Especially in AI.
As the world gets increasingly obsessed with artificial intelligence, TikTok has become a go-to space for creators breaking down complex AI topics in digestible, relatable ways. These aren’t just hobbyists — they’re developers, researchers, consultants, and startup founders building real communities and trust with niche, tech-savvy audiences.
**So, why should B2B marketers care?**
  * **AI is everywhere** — and these creators help you stay relevant without drowning in jargon.


  * **They’re building credibility** in real time with engaged followers (including your future customers).


  * **The content moves fast** — just like the industry.


  * **And no, it’s not just Gen Z anymore.** Decision-makers are watching, too.


More than anything, these creators are open to brand partnerships — especially with companies that align with their values and audience interests.
So, still think TikTok isn’t for B2B? Check out these 10 AI creators making waves — and see what your brand could learn (or partner with) next.
## 10 AI-Focused TikTok Influencers on the Rise
### **Allie K. Miller**
[@alliekmiller](https://www.tiktok.com/@alliekmiller)
Allie shares snappy, short-form videos instructing businesses on how they can most effectively utilize AI in their marketing workflows, as well as industry updates on new AI tools and products. Her videos often explore the intersection of human creativity and AI-powered ventures, allowing businesses a thoughtful perspective on how they might use AI to enhance their creative output without losing their brand’s human touch. 
### **Catherine “Cat” Goetze**
There’s no denying the fact that audiences are, in general, [skeptical of AI](https://www.typeform.com/getreal). In this vein, Cat offers a nuanced, well-rounded perspective on AI use in the business world, and through her candid, yet personable, videos, she emphasizes that businesses need to implement AI the _right_ way. Cat is the perfect creator to follow for B2B marketers looking for an approachable entry into the world of AI, and she often does partnerships with AI and tech brands, so she’s a great person to consider for future collaboration.
### **Nat Choprasert**
If you’re overwhelmed by the influx of AI and unsure where to even _start_ implementing it into your brand, you’re not alone – and Nat is here to help. She aims to demystify AI technology, leveraging her background as a digital strategist to help businesses utilize AI in their brand-building efforts. Nat covers a broad range of topics in which companies can effectively use AI, such as targeted advertising, consumer data analysis, personalized marketing strategies and more, providing quick tips that businesses can implement immediately to improve their marketing efforts.
