## Frequently asked questions about influencer rankings
##### What is a fitness influencer?
A fitness influencer is a creator who shares health, training, or wellness content online to educate, inspire, or motivate their audience.
##### Why are fitness influencers important?
They build trusted communities, drive health trends, and make fitness more accessible through online content and coaching.
##### Why is storytelling important for fitness creators?
Personal journeys, progress, and behind-the-scenes training build credibility and deepen the connection with audiences.
##### How has social media changed the fitness industry in South Africa?
It has made quality fitness content and coaching more accessible, democratizing wellness beyond gyms and traditional programs.
##### Are all fitness influencers certified trainers?
No. Some are certified professionals, others share personal experiences and routines that resonate with their audience.
