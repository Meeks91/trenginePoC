## What Makes a Top Content Marketing Agency?
You might be thinking, what criteria have we considered when choosing each agency to add to this list?
As an agency ourselves, we understand what makes other agencies work well - so these are the most important areas you should look for in an agency when considering to work with them.
### Case Studies
The proof is in the pudding.
Look at their case studies to see what industries they’ve worked with, their campaign goals, and their results for other brands. This will be a good example of what they could achieve for your business.
### Expertise
Sometimes, it’s not about how long an agency has been operating for but how much experience the people in it have.
Have a look at their ‘about us’ pages and have a read at what they do and their backgrounds. This will prove their expertise and knowledge as an agency.
### Communication
Efficient communication is key, from proposal and intro call stages to regular status calls to know how your campaigns are doing and always stay updated.
Also, transparency is another thing to keep an eye on, as you want to ensure that you’re aware of how your budget is being spent and the results.
### Culture
Making sure that an agency’s culture and values align with your own is essential, as it will ensure the same vision and way of working.
If there’s chemistry between brand and agency, chances are that the outcome will be better.
