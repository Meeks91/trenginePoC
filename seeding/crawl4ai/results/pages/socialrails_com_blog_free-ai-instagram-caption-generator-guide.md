  1. **Hook:** Attention-grabbing opening line
  2. **Story:** Personal anecdote or valuable information
  3. **CTA:** Clear call-to-action for engagement


**Example:**
> I almost gave up on my business in 2019. [HOOK]
> After 6 months of barely breaking even, I was ready to throw in the towel. Then I discovered one strategy that changed everything: [story continues with specific details and lessons learned] [STORY]
> What's one lesson you've learned from a challenging time? Share below! [CTA]
