## What is an AI Influencer?
An AI influencer is a virtual avatar created using artificial intelligence and advanced technology. Unlike traditional influencers, these digital personalities are entirely computer-generated, designed to mimic human behavior, appearance, and even emotions. They can interact with audiences, create content, and participate in trends—just like their human counterparts. 
## 7 Top AI Influencers on TikTok for 2025
### 1. FN Meka
[FN Meka](https://www.tiktok.com/@fnmeka?lang=en) is a fictiona AI rapper/avatar created by Brandon Le in 2019 as part of the company Factory New. Anthony Martini joined the project in 2020. Marketed as a "virtual rapper," FN Meka rose to fame, becoming the most prominent virtual rapper with 10 million followers and over 100 million views on TikTok.The character’s music, initially described as AI-generated, was created using data points from video games and social media. FN Meka's music and persona also involved virtual items and NFTs, with collaborations like a "Lamborghini porta-potty" NFT sold for $6,432. FN Meka's avatar is a Black male cyborg.
### 2. Lil Miquela
[Lil Miquela](https://www.tiktok.com/@lilmiquela?lang=en) is a 19-year-old virtual music artist from Los Angeles, created by Cain Intelligence and managed by Brud. With 3.4 million TikTok followers, she has become a leading figure in AI influencer culture. Lil Miquela is celebrated for her unique blend of music and fashion, collaborating with top brands like Dior, Calvin Klein, Prada, and Givenchy.
Beyond her fashion influence, Lil Miquela is known for her music, with hits like Wasted and Not Mine. She’s appeared alongside celebrities such as Bella Hadid and Millie Bobby Brown. In 2018, Time Magazine named her one of the 25 Most Influential People on the Internet, cementing her cultural significance.
### 3. Lechat 
[Lechat](https://www.tiktok.com/@lechat.vv?lang=en) is an AI influencer known for her quirky and playful persona, representing creativity, artistry, and a love for all things whimsical. With 3.1 million TikTok followers, she has made a mark in the art and creativity space.
Lechat's collaborations with art supplies brands, creative workshops, DIY kits, and colorful products highlight her influence. She inspires her audience to embrace their artistic side and engage with brands that promote self-expression and creativity. Through her vibrant presence, Lechat continues to champion the colorful world of art.
### 4. Kizuna AI
[Kizuna AI](https://www.tiktok.com/@kizunaai0630?lang=en) is a pioneering Japanese Virtual YouTuber who debuted in November 2016, coining the term "Virtual YouTuber" and becoming a global sensation. She is known for her bubbly personality, signature appearance with long brown hair and a sailor suit, and her passion for connecting with people through technology.
Over the years, she has reached milestones like 3 million TikTok followers, hosted TV shows, released music, and even had a farewell concert with over 1,000 VTuber cameos before her hiatus in 2022. Her legacy includes influencing the VTuber community, creating an anime, and venturing into new projects like the #KZN Singeroid.
### 5. Imma
[Imma](https://www.tiktok.com/@imma.tokyo?lang=en) is an AI influencer from Japan who debuted in 2018, quickly making a name for herself in the digital world. She is known for her signature pink bob and chic fashion sense. Imma has around 388,000 followers on Instagram and 466k followers on TikTok.
Imma has collaborated with global brands like IKEA, Lenovo, and Porsche Japan. Her presence at major events such as the 2020 Paralympics closing ceremony and her appearance in Masayuki Ino’s Doublet collection at Paris Fashion Week solidified her influence across both virtual and real-world platforms.
### 6. Noonoouri 
[Noonoouri](https://www.tiktok.com/@realnoonoouri?lang=en) is a Parisian AI virtual influencer known for her distinctive cartoonish look and strong presence in fashion and sustainability. Created by designer Joerg Zuber, Noonoouri has amassed over 490,000 followers on Instagram and 80k followers on TikTok.
With collaborations with high-end brands like Dior, Versace, and Marc Jacobs, Noonoouri uses her platform to promote ethical fashion and sustainability. Her influence goes beyond just style—she's an advocate for social consciousness, championing sustainable fashion as a key cause.
### 7. Bermuda 
[Bermuda](https://www.tiktok.com/@bermudaisbae?lang=en) is an AI influencer based in LA with over 200K followers on Instagram and 44K followers on TikTok. Debuting in 2016, she identifies as a robot woman dedicated to inspiring young entrepreneurs, particularly women, to pursue careers in robotics.
Alongside her tech ambitions, Bermuda is building a music career, having released a cover of "Under the Bridge" by the Red Hot Chili Peppers on Spotify. Though her brand collaborations are not publicly disclosed, Bermuda continues to trailblaze in robotics and music, motivating others to follow their passions.
