# Top Science-Based Fitness Influencers to Follow
Last Updated
July 20, 2023
Originally Published
July 21, 2023
[ Written by John Bastawrous Account Director ](https://theinfluenceagency.com/author/john-bastawrous)
With all of the [fitness influencers](https://theinfluenceagency.com/influencer-marketing/fitness) giving advice, I’m sure you think to yourself, which ones can I trust? There is a lot of false information, especially in the fitness industry. Nowadays, with social media, you never know who has done their proper research and who hasn’t.
Now, fitness is not as black and white as one may seem; there are multiple ways to reach the same goal. However, these influencers have proved themselves time and time again to be reliable sources of fitness tips!
##  **1. Derek |**[**@MorePlatesMoreDates**](https://www.youtube.com/@MorePlatesMoreDates)
Derek from More Plates More Dates is an ex-bodybuilder who uses his experience and research to discuss different health and fitness industry topics. Derek is a Canadian-based influencer who uses his platform to discuss topics such as steroids, supplements, and fitness in general.
On his YouTube channel, "More Plates More Dates," Derek became a trusted source of information and advice on fitness, muscle growth, hormone optimization, and the safe and effective use of performance-enhancing substances. The way he presents complex scientific concepts is accessible, so even beginners can understand them.
As a result of Derek's commitment to integrity and transparency, he has developed a loyal following. An evidence-based approach is important to him, examining scientific literature extensively and drawing conclusions based on facts. In addition to providing in-depth analyses and detailed explanations, he is dedicated to helping his audience achieve their health and fitness goals safely and intelligently.
You can also find Derek on Instagram as [@moreplatesmoredates](https://www.instagram.com/moreplatesmoredates/?hl=en)!
##  **2. Sabrina McMullen |**[**@beefcake_brina**](https://www.tiktok.com/@beefcake_brina)
After suffering from two eating disorders and being bullied throughout grade school, Sabrina became a CPT. Her passion for fitness led her to bodybuilding, then figure competition, and now she's on her way to becoming WNBF/INBF Pro.
She's passionate about connecting scientific research and everyday people trying to improve their health as a science-based fitness influencer. As a trusted source in the fitness industry, she provides evidence-based information and debunks myths. Sabrina empowers her audience to make smart decisions and take on transformative journeys.
Don’t forget to follow Sabrina on TikTok - you can find her on the platform as [@beefcake_brina](https://www.tiktok.com/@beefcake_brina).
##  **3. Jeff Nippard |**[**@JeffNippard**](https://www.youtube.com/c/JeffNippard)
Jeff Nippard is an expert in bodybuilding, strength training, and evidence-based practices in the fitness industry. Growing up in Canada, Jeff has been passionate about fitness and bodybuilding since a young age. Having a degree in biochemistry, Jeff has built a reputation for using evidence-based training methods.
Using his YouTube channel and social media platforms, Jeff shares his expertise with fitness enthusiasts, effectively bridging the gap between scientific research and real life. He breaks down complicated concepts into understandable, actionable advice in his content.
He stands out as a fitness influencer because he's honest and transparent. As an expert on studies and peer-reviewed research, he can critically evaluate the industry's latest trends, fads, and misconceptions. Jeff helps his audience navigate the overwhelming amount of fitness information and make informed training and nutrition decisions by debunking myths and providing evidence-based insights.
If you want to follow Jeff more closely, check out his Instagram [@jeffnippard](https://www.instagram.com/JeffNippard/)!
##  **4. JPG Coaching |**[**@jpgcoaching**](https://www.tiktok.com/@jpgcoaching)

Science-based coaching is what JPG Coaching is all about. He incorporates evidence-based methods into his coaching strategies, staying at the forefront of industry research. As a fitness expert with a vision for transforming lives, JPG Coaching continues to innovate, grow, and empower people through evidence-based fitness advice and a commitment to people's success to make a lasting impact on the fitness industry.
Follow JPG Coaching’s TikTok account, [@jpgcoaching](https://www.tiktok.com/@jpgcoaching?lang=en), where he posts all about fitness!
##  **5. Ryan Jewers |**[**@ryjewers**](https://www.instagram.com/ryjewers/?hl=en)
Physical fitness has been a passion of Ryan's since he was a kid. Through his own experience, he saw firsthand how fitness can improve one's mental and physical health. As a result of his own transformation, Ryan took control of his health and set out to inspire and empower others to do the same.
