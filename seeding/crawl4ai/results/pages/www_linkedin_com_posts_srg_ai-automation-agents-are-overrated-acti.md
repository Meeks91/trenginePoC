AI Automation & Agents are Overrated!!! And that hurts most professionals today… Although you can now use natural language and no-code platforms to set up automations and agents with AI you still need: - Specialization; - Maintenance; - Invest Time. The reality: Over 95% of professionals I talk to daily haven’t even created a custom GPT. One didn’t even know what ChatGPT was! So there’s a HUGE gap between what AI can do and what professionals actually use it for daily. That’s why all the hype about “AI agents” and “full automation” is like a future dream, because for most, it still is. The truth? Talking about AI agents today is like selling a spaceship to someone who doesn’t yet drive a car. The smarter approach is simpler: start with small, practical wins. Everyday use cases that build confidence and prepare professionals for more sophisticated AI solutions later. Here’s how that transition looks in practice: 1️⃣ Start with daily tasks: – Outline reports – Summarize emails – Draft LinkedIn posts 2️⃣ Move to structured tools: – Market analysis – Guided itineraries – Forecasting trends 3️⃣ Then experiment with agents: – Automate workflows – Scale with confidence – Integrate across systems The future of AI in tourism isn’t skipping steps. It’s walking professionals from familiarization → integration → automation. 👉 Want practical AI wins you can actually use today?
