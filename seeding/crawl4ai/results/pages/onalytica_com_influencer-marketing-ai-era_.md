# Influencer Marketing Is Essential for the AI Era
_“75% of B2B enterprises will increase budgets for influencer relations. Marketers must invest in influencer engagement that builds trust when AI-powered search and AI agents bolster discovery .”_**Forrester 2026 Predictions: B2B Marketing, Sales & Product**
AI-powered search and AI agents are changing how buyers discover, evaluate, and shortlist vendors. They are not just scanning websites for keywords. They are synthesizing answers from sources they consider credible. That shift changes what visibility means. In the AI era, visibility is earned through trust. And trust is built through people.
### Why SEO Content Isn’t Enough For GenAI Discovery
Traditional SEO still matters, but it is no longer the whole game. GenAI tools do not reward content simply because it is optimized around keywords and topic clusters. They look for signals that ideas are reliable, supported, and connected to real expertise. Those signals often come from credible individuals: practitioners, analysts, subject matter experts, and respected creators. If your content strategy does not include credible human voices, you will be less discoverable in the moments buyers are asking AI for recommendations.
### Influence Is The Trust Layer For AI-Driven Discovery
Influencer marketing is no longer only about reach. In the AI era, it becomes a trust and authority engine. The right experts help you build credibility faster in high-skepticism categories, reinforce your point of view with real-world experience, create content that travels beyond your owned channels, and increase visibility across AI-driven and human-driven discovery. When credible voices consistently validate your narrative, your brand becomes easier to recommend, easier to believe, and easier to choose.
### Influencer Co-Creation Makes Your Content More Discoverable
Co-creation is what turns influencer marketing into a durable advantage. When influencers help shape the narrative, not just appear in it, you get stronger perspectives that stand out from generic AI-generated content, more original insights that AI systems and buyers tend to value, better engagement because content is attached to a real person with proven credibility, and more distribution points as creators share across their channels and communities. This is how content becomes visible no matter how your buyers are searching: Google, LinkedIn, communities, AI assistants, or internal research tools.
### What Enterprise Teams Should Do Now
To stay discoverable in the AI era, the goal is not more content. It is more credible content. A strong enterprise approach typically includes defining the trust narrative and proof points you want associated with your brand, activating credible voices across awareness, consideration, and decision moments, co-creating content built to be referenced, shared, and reused, and measuring what matters, including message pull-through, content performance, and business impact.
