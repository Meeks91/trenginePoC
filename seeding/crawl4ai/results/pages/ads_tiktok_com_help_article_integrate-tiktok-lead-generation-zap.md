# How to integrate TikTok Lead Generation with Zapier
Last updated: November 2025 
Zapier is a Customer Relationship Management (CRM) integration available for TikTok lead generation.
Learn more [about available CRM integrations for TikTok lead generation with Instant Form](https://ads.tiktok.com/help/article/available-crm-integrations-tiktok-lead-generation).
## Before you begin 
Before you integrate TikTok lead generation with Zapier, make sure you:
  * Go to <https://zapier.com/apps> and check if Zapier supports your CRM.
  * Created at least one [Instant Form](https://ads.tiktok.com/help/article/build-instant-form?lang=en) on TikTok Ads Manager.
  * Ensure you have a [Zapier](https://zapier.com/) account.
  * Locate your CRM's login credentials.
  * Make sure you have a TikTok Ads Manager account and admin access to that account.


Learn [about asset level permissions](https://ads.tiktok.com/help/article/about-assets-and-asset-level-permissions?aadvid=72391499277&lang=en).
## How to integrate TikTok lead generation with Zapier
To integrate TikTok lead generation with Zapier:
  1. Log in to [TikTok Ads Manager](https://ads.tiktok.com/i18n/login?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Fads.tiktok.com%2Fi18n%2Fhome%3Fredirect%3Dhttps%253A%252F%252Fads.tiktok.com%252Fi18n%252Fhome%253Frefer%253Dfrom_help_create).
  2. There are 3 options to access the Zapier integration in TikTok Ads Manager:
     * Select the **Tools** tab, select **Leads Center** , and click **Connect CRM**.
     * Select the **Tools** tab, choose **Instant Pag** e under the Creative section, select **Instant Form** , and click on **Download Lead Data** under the form.
     * Select the **Campaigns** tab, go to the Ad section, and then click on **Download Lead Data** from the Download Leads column within the table.
  3. Search Zapier or find it in the list of CRM options.
  4. Select **TikTok direct integration**.
  5. Sign in to your Zapier account when prompted.
  6. Give Zapier authorization to access your ad account, agree to the Platform Service Agreement, and click **Confirm**. Once you've confirmed, the Zapier editor will open within your TikTok Ads Manager interface.
  7. You'll need to create a Zap, which is an automated workflow that will connect TikTok Lead Generation to your CRM. To do this:
     * Search for and select TikTok Lead Generation as your trigger app.
     * Connect your TikTok Ads Manager account to Zapier. If you've connected Zapier through TikTok, you'll see your Ads Manager account available to select in the dropdown menu.
     * Select the Instant Form you'd like to use.
     * Test your trigger.
     * Set up your action steps, which are the things you'd like your Zap to do for you. In this step, you will map the fields of your instant form with the fields of your CRMs.
     * Test your Zap and turn it on.


Learn [how to edit your CRM integration settings](https://ads.tiktok.com/help/article/how-to-edit-your-crm-integration-settings?aadvid=72391499277).
