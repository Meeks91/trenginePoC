## **Powerful Data Science Leaders in the World**
The following are the powerful **data science leaders** in the world: 
### **Andrew Ng**
Dr Andrew Ng is the most famous data scientist and an inspiration to many data scientists who want to achieve big in this area. He is one of the **Data science leaders** who has a bountiful knowledge of Machine learning and artificial intelligence. 
Apart from this, he is the one who is the founder of deeplearning.ai and the CEO of landing. AI. He also gives lectures in the Department of Computer Science at Stanford University. 
To do extensive research in the area of data science, he founded the deep learning artificial intelligence research team along with Google. This is named Google Brain. He also worked as a Chief scientist at Baidu. 
There are around 1300 students who were mentored by this great data scientist in this AI group. He also developed the best AI strategy for the company. He also led the course development team at Stanford University and founded Coursera. 
On this website, they are offering Machine learning courses for students. Many thousands of students have benefitted from this site. Being an expert in ML, he also holds a degree from the reputed Carnegie Mellon University and the University of California. 
Many research papers are co-authored by this great data scientist in the field of [robotics](https://pwskills.com/blog/artificial-intelligence-in-robotics/), ML and different fields and have been featured as the most **famous data science leaders**. 
### **Andrej Karpathy**
He is a Slovak- Canadian who holds a PhD degree from the reputed Stanford University. He is one of the **famous data science leaders** who has built JARVIS in open AI. It has become quite popular. Not just this, he also worked in the capacity of the director of artificial intelligence and autopilot vision at Tesla. 
Andrej is very much interested to do research and learn more about deep neural nets. He has completed double majors in Physics and computer science and later went to Columbia to continue his studies. 
He worked with Michael van de Panne to learn about controllers. He also got an opportunity to work with Fei-Fei-Li while doing his PhD at Stanford Vision Lab. In this company, he got to work on key concepts of data science like convolutional neural networks and recurrent neural network architectures. 
There is also research done on how these concepts are used in natural language processing and computer vision. He also designed an instructor for convolutional neural networks for visual recognition. 
He also blogs by sharing his thoughts about new concepts in data science and developing various libraries related to deep learning. He is certainly the most intelligent and great data scientist that the world has. There are some really good **data science leaders podcast** available. 
### **Amena Anadkumar**
She is from Mysore and is working as a professor at Caltech and one among many **famous data scientists in india**. She is also working as a director of AI research in one of the famous companies, i.e. NVIDIA. With her passion for data science and sharing a lot of input with youngsters, she grabbed the attention of many and has over 159417 followers. 
The research carried out by her is in the areas of machine learning, high-dimensional stats and non-convex optimization. She has a degree from IIT Madras and later completed her master’s from Cornell University. 
She has worked with Amazon web services as a principal scientist and is a member of IEEE, ACM and Alfred P. Sloan Foundation. She has developed novel artificial intelligence that is used in prediction weather, scientific simulations and making drugs. She has got prestigious awards of which the key one is the ACM Gordon Bell special prize for her research conducted on HPC during COVID-19 time.

### **Fei-Fei-Li**
She is another famous data scientist and a leader who is excelling in this field. She is currently the co-director at the Stanford Institute for the area of Human-centered artificial intelligence (AI) as well as the vision & learning lab. She also gives guest lectures at Stanford university for computer science students. 
Before this, she served as a vice president at Google and worked as a Chief Scientist at Google Cloud. With rich experience in the field of data science, she worked on cognitively inspired AI, computer vision, and machine learning and used AI in the healthcare industry.
Around 200+ scientific articles were published by her and many journals in different fields. The revolutionary project that is headed by her is ImageNet. She is also bringing revolution in STEM and AI at a national level. 
For her meritorious work, she has received many prestigious awards and is featured in ELLE Magazine for being one of the Tech women in 2017. She also received a Global Thinker Award by Foreign Policy in 2015 and Great Immigrants: The Pride of America in 2016. 
### **Yann LeCun**
With a lot of experience in technical consulting, research, and scientific research, he is currently the Chief scientist at Facebook. He has done many commendable works such as computer vision, machine learning and computational neuroscience. He founded convolutional nets and worked on computer vision projects and stood as one of the **Data Science Leaders.**
He is the creator of DjVu and won the Turing Award in 2018. He has major contributions to machine learning that are applied to recognize handwriting. It is also used to recognize bank checks. Companies like NCR have used this technology in their platforms. 
### **Ian Goodfellow**
He is the most influential data scientist and is based out of America. He has done extensive research on machine learning and worked as a director of the Machine learning department at Apple. He has a B.S. and M.S. degree in computer science and these degrees were earned from Stanford University. 
He earned a PhD degree from Universite de Montreal. He has ample experience in deep learning and worked as a research scientist at Google Brain. He wrote a book on deep learning and invented generative adversarial networks.

He is a known name who has made a lot of contributions to the artificial intelligence world. He has done a lot of research on deep learning and got a prestigious award called 2018 A.M. ### **Jeremy Howard**
He is an Australian and one of the best **Data Science Leaders** , educator, and entrepreneur. He worked as a management consultant with McKinsey & Co. as well as AT Kearney. He worked there for over 8 years before starting his firm. 
He contributed to many open-source projects and developed the PERL language. He played a critical role in the evolution of PERL. 
He has started companies like FastMail, which was later acquired by Opera software and Optimal Decisions Group. He worked as the founding CEO of Enlitic and was the past president of Kaggle. He is a research scientist at the University of San Francisco.
