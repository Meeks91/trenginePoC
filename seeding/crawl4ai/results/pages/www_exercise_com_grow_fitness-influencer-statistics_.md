Forget total followers. Track these five **operating stats** instead—across Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube—so you can forecast revenue:
  1. **Activation Rate (AR):** % of viewers who take a _free_ step (save, DM keyword, join waitlist).

  4. **Day-30 Paid Retention (R30):** paid members who are still active 30 days later.
  5. **Average Revenue Per Paying User (ARPPU):** total paid revenue ÷ active payers.
