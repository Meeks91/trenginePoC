## **Wrapping Things Up**
The shape of influencer marketing has changed a lot in the past few years. Brand planning to incorporate influencer campaigns in their future marketing strategies must evolve to adopt these changes. 
Some of them include: 
  * **Working with micro-influencers.** Celebrities and mega-influencers don’t do it for every business. If you need someone with an engaged audience in your particular niche, a micro or nano-influencer is your best bet. 
  * **Working with multiple influencers.** Don’t settle with one or two influencers. Collaborate with a few to ensure you’re reaching most, if not all, sub-segments of your audience. 
  * **Analyzing results.** Influencer marketing can often take time to take off. Don't give up too quickly. Keep track of your campaigns and analyze the results regularly. 
  * **Measuring different metrics.** Not all brands [use ROI as the main metric](https://influencermarketinghub.com/influencer-marketing-roi/). Some opt for impressions, while others focus on brand mentions. Go through our report to learn more about this trend.
