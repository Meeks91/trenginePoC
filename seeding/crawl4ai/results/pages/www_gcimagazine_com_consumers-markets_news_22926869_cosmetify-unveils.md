# Cosmetify Unveils Most-searched Brands and Top Influencers of 2024
Nov 19th, 2024
Cosmetify's 2024 Index reveals some of the top brands in beauty from the last year based on search data from Instagram and Google.
AdobeStock by stnazkul
Cosmetify's 2024 index provides a fascinating snapshot of the beauty industry's digital landscape. While traditional powerhouses like L'Oreal and Estée Lauder continue to dominate, the industry is experiencing a rise of new brands and trends.
[Cosmetify’s 2024 index](https://www.cosmetify.com/us/the-cosmetify-index/) was compiled using data centered around each [brand’s ](https://www.gcimagazine.com/brands-products/hair-care/news/22915411/2024s-top-hair-care-brands-products-concerns)Instagram follower count, Instagram engagement rate, Instagram mentions, Google searches and change in searches.

  1. La Roche Posay
  2. CeraVe
  3. Sol de Janeiro
  4. Charlotte Tilbury
  5. The Ordinary
  6. L’Occitane
  7. Estee Lauder
  8. L’Oreal
  9. Olaplex
  10. Cetaphil


## Top 5 Social Media Influencers
  1. James Charles
  2. Mari Maria
  3. Bretman Rock
  4. Nikkie Tutorials 
  5. Jeffree Star


## 3 Brands Losing Popularity
  * Nudestix: -33% decrease
  * Olaplex: -66.4% decrease
  * Pink Honey UK: -18% decrease


## Rising Stars
  1. Phlur: 400% increase 
  2. Rhode: 232% increase
  3. Summer Fridays: 173% increase


Cosmetify’s keyword search tool reflected that online searches for “Korean beauty” have increased by 53% year-on-year, and searches for the term “Korean skin care” are up 83% year-on-year.
