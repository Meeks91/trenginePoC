## Latest Updates 
### 26 February 2026 
  * The [DigitalOcean Cloud Security Posture Management (CSPM) dashboard](https://cloud.digitalocean.com) is now in public preview, allowing you to scan your DigitalOcean resources for cloud misconfigurations and security liabilities. The dashboard lists issues by severity and provides mitigation steps on how to resolve them. For more details such as pricing, see our [CSPM product page](https://docs.digitalocean.com/products/cspm/).


### 11 February 2026 
  * As of 1 February 2026, DigitalOcean charges Retail Sales Tax (RST) for customers with a tax location in Manitoba. This complies with [Information Bulletin – RST 033](https://www.gov.mb.ca/finance/taxation/pubs/bulletins/033.pdf) issued by the Manitoba Government.
Services provided by DigitalOcean to customers located in Manitoba are subject to 7% RST in addition to 5% GST for a total tax rate of 12%. [Learn more about taxes in Canada](https://docs.digitalocean.com/platform/billing/taxes/can/).


### 20 January 2026 
  * We have enabled silent re-authentication for [single sign-on (SSO)](https://docs.digitalocean.com/platform/teams/how-to/configure-sso/) when using the DigitalOcean Control Panel. DigitalOcean automatically refreshes the identity provider (IdP) access tokens, so you are no longer unexpectedly logged out when the tokens expire. This improves security and minimizes interruptions to your workflow.


For more information, see [the full release notes](https://docs.digitalocean.com/release-notes/).
