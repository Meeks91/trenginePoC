## Who is the Most Streamed Artist on Spotify? 
Quick answer: **Taylor Swift holds the throne of the most-streamed artist on Spotify** with a total of whopping 117 billion streams. She’s right above Bad Bunny and Drake. From Shake It Off to the Fate of Ophelia, her popularity continues to grow, and it doesn’t seem like she will be giving up her well-earned throne anytime soon.

## Most-Streamed Artists on Spotify Compared
The artists on this list might not have the most-streamed song in 2026, but the numbers are calculated based on total streams, including older tracks and albums. The **data for this list comes from**[ChartMasters](https://chartmasters.org/most-streamed-artists-ever-on-spotify/). With that out of the way, let’s check out the list itself.
### 1. Taylor Swift 
Taylor Swift is the most-streamed artist on Spotify of all time, and it isn’t even surprising anymore. Her past 6 albums have all peaked on the Billboard 200, and her latest track, _The Fate of Ophelia_ is dominating streaming charts, garnering over 500 million streams on Spotify alone.
Image Credit: Universal Music Publishing Group (via YouTube/ Taylor Swift)
Whether you’re a long-time Swiftie or just casually tuned in, her numbers this year just prove that she is arguably the biggest music icon of this generation, and that’s why she **consistently appears** in many people’s [Spotify Wrapped](https://beebom.com/spotify-wrapped-2024-now-out/) every year, including mine.
**Lead Streams:** 117,170,820,898
### 2. Bad Bunny 
Puerto Rican rapper and singer Bad Bunny took over the world in 2021 with his viral hits. His latest hit _DtMF_ (_DeBÍ TiRAR MáS FOToS_), became **an uncontested party anthem** of the year. It came out in January and continues to blast through clubs, cars, and every “for you” playlist you could imagine.
Image Credit: Rimas Entertainment (via YouTube/ Bad Bunny)
Meanwhile, his album, _Debi Tirar Mas Fotos_ , which features numerous collaborators representing a range of iconic Puerto Rican styles, continues to dominate Spotify streams. His style of blending reggaeton (Latin American music genre), trap, and emotional storytelling makes his music hit just as hard at 2 PM as it does at 2 AM.
**Lead Streams:** 99,453,500,623
### 3. Drake 
Drake is 3rd on this list, and it’s simply because his tracks still hit as hard as they did a decade ago. His 2025 single, ‘ _Nokia_ ,’ might have been the butt of a lot of memes, reels, and TikTok edits. But it still managed to **reach the ‘Biggest Hits’ playlist consistently** , surpassing 500 million streams worldwide.
Image Credit: Republic Records (via YouTube/ Drake)
Drake has had a record of silencing criticism with his songs, and _Nokia_ was a much-needed hit Drake needed after he feuded with Kendrick Lamar last year. It just goes to show that Drake knows his audience and what catches listeners instantly. So say what you want about him, but Drizzy isn’t going anywhere soon, and his ability to produce replay-worthy hits remains undefeated.
**Lead Streams:** 95,854,121,208
### 4. The Weeknd
Starboy and Blinding Lights singer, The Weeknd, did it again in 2026 with his gut-wrenching track, _Cry For Me_. It brings his signature **haunting vocals with a cinematic touch** that only he could pull off, and clearly, listeners were all in for it.

Image Credit: UMG Recordings, Inc. (via YouTube/ The Weeknd)
His album _Hurry Up Tomorrow_ features more such tracks like _Drive_ , _Red Terror_ , and _Take Me Back to LA_ , with a mix of synth-pop, R&B, and dark storytelling that really plays in his favor, and the listeners are in for it. Not to forget his previous astronomical hits like _Save Your Tears_ , _Timeless_ , and _Heartless_ are still amassing millions of streams daily.
**Lead Streams:** 75,638,489,345
### 5. Ariana Grande 
_Wicked_ star Ariana Grande continues to be a streaming juggernaut in 2026, and it’s easy to understand why. Her tracks, from the album ‘ _Dangerous Woman_ ‘, still feel refreshing today, and her evolution from writing pop and R&B tracks to more somber tracks in ‘ _Eternal Sunshine_ ‘ shows her range and singing talent.
Image Credit: Republic Records (via YouTube/ Ariana Grande)
Ariana’s latest song, ‘ _Twilight Zone,_ ‘ has become a fan favorite instantly, getting over 200 million streams. Her strength lies in how her songs **resonate with audiences of all age groups** , and this is why she secures the fifth spot in this list of the most-streamed artists on Spotify.
**Lead Streams:** 59,177,743,485
### 6. Ed Sheeran
Ed Sheeran continues his streak of being one of those artists whose streaming numbers never really dip. When people ask who is the most-streamed artist on Spotify, Ed Sheeran’s name almost always comes up, and for good reason. His older tracks from _Divide_ still rack up millions of plays every day, with songs like _Shape of You_ and _Perfect_ aging far better than most hits from that era.
Image Credit: Republic Records (via YouTube/ Ed Sheeran)
Even his newer releases like _Sapphire_ have that Ed Sheeran charm, which keeps people hooked, and the reason behind this is that his music is simple, **emotional, and easy to revisit**. His music works for every age group, country, and all sorts of people, making it clear why he is one of the top 10 most streamed artist on Spotify.
**Lead Streams:** 57,209,583,667

### 7. Justin Bieber
From the merry old days to _Baby_ , to the hip hits like _Sorry,_ Justin Bieber has evolved a lot as an artist, and so has his audience. People who listened to him for the first time in his debut track still follow him to this very day. His catalog is stacked with songs that pull massive daily numbers, from _Purpose_ era hits like _Love Yourself_ to the more mature sound of _Justice_. So it becomes easy to understand why he has a spot as the most-streamed artist of all time.
Image Credit: via YouTube/ Justin Bieber
His music connects with both longtime fans and newer listeners, and even people like me who thought that we had grown out of the _Bieber era_ have come around to his music again. Even years after their release, Bieber’s songs remain the **default choice across many pop and chill playlists**.
**Lead Streams:** 54,765,199,893
### 8. Eminem
Whether you know him by Marshal, Slim Shady, or Eminem, there is no denying that he is the most talented rapper, and even veteran artists can vouch for that. Even in 2026, Eminem continues to pull massive numbers on Spotify, which is why his name often comes up when people ask who the most-streamed artist on Spotify is. His classic albums like _The Marshall Mathers LP_ and _The Eminem Show_ still feel as relevant as they did decades prior. 
Image Credit: Shady Records, Aftermath Entertainment, and Interscope Records (via YouTube/ EminemMusic)
Even the newer tracks, like  _Houdini, Killshot, and Fall,_ generated a ton of buzz around the music scene. His bars, rhymes, hooks, and, of course, charm are second to none. This is why he has amassed such a massive global audience, who actively listen to his tracks, whether it’s for workouts, nostalgia, or **pure lyrical appreciation**.
**Lead Streams:** 54,159,463,498
### 9. Billie Eilish
Billie Eilish has also come a long way from her humble beginnings to a full-fledged streaming star. Her soulful voice, melodic vocals, and deep lyrics easily connect with the audience, which makes it clear why she is one of the most streamed artist on Spotify. Her songs like _Bad Guy_ , _Happier Than Ever,_ and _Lunch_ pull huge numbers, thanks to their **distinct sound and emotional honesty**.
Image Credit: Darkroom/Interscope Records (via YouTube/ Billie Eilish)
And her music videos often feature a very minimal production quality, so as not take anything away from her indulging voice. She also often delves into darker themes, making her songs a hit among younger listeners and easy to revisit. And some would even go as far as to say that Billie Eilish’s tracks have become **part of how a generation listens** to music on Spotify.
**Lead Streams:** 53,985,148,430
### 10. Kanye West
Despite his numerous controversies, which we will not get into in this read, Kanye West remains one of the most unpredictable yet consistently most streamed artist on Spotify, even in 2026. His albums like _Graduation_ and My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy still pull in massive daily streams every day.
Image Credit: YZY (via YouTube/ Kanye West)
Kanye has made it to this list of the most-streamed artists on Spotify **due to his legacy**. His music spans multiple eras, moods, and styles, giving listeners a reason to keep coming listening. His discography has aged remarkably well on streaming platforms, and that **long-term replay value** is exactly why Kanye West continues to rack up numbers year after year.
**Lead Streams:** 51,085,634,420
