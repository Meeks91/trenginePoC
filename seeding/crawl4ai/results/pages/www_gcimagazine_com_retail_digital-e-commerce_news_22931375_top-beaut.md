## What is VIT, and Why Does It Matter? 
The Visibility + Impact × Trust (VIT) score, developed by Traackr’s Data Science team, measures an influencer’s performance. It factors in visibility (reach and impressions), the impact of the content (engagement and creativity), and the trust they build with their audience. With VIT scores, brands and followers alike can identify which influencers truly make a mark in the beauty industry.
