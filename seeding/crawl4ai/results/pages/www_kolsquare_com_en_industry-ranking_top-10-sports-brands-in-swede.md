### Local champions vs global giants: the uniqueness of sports influence in Sweden
In Sweden, the sporting influence on Instagram is driven by a clever mix of local brands and big international names. Swedish players such as RevolutionRace, Nordic Wellness or Vasaloppet dominate the national rankings thanks to remarkable EMV and engagement rates, often higher than those of global giants. While brands like Prozis or Premier League clubs remain very present, local loyalty and authentic partnerships with creators give Swedish brands a clear advantage — proof that community strategies are making a difference in 2025.
