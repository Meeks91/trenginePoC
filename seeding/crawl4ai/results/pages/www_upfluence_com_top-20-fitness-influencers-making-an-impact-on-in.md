## **20 of the Most Popular Fitness Influencers**
##  **Michelle Lewin**** _(15.2M Followers)_**
Where Michelle once used to work in a clinic in Venezuela, today, she is one of the biggest fitness stars on Instagram. This model/bodybuilder has her own range of supplements and workout equipment and has also created two apps: _Fitplan_ and _Mealplan_. Her motivational fitness videos and her down-to-earth nature are two of the reasons why her followers are so loyal to her. 
##  **Jen Selter**** _(13.6M Followers)_**
Based in New York, Jen inspires her followers with nutrition, fitness, and lifestyle posts on a daily basis. This influencer began her fitness journey in high school when she worked at a local gym. From there, she quickly gained millions of followers when her booty-based workouts started going [viral](https://www.upfluence.com/glossary/virality-unleashing-the-power-of-content-viral-spread?utm_source=organic&utm_campaign=direct_blog&utm_medium=organic "viral"). She has also collaborated with several health and beauty brands such as The Perfect Sculpt and Rebels Refinery.
##  **Kayla Itsines**** _(15.4M Followers)_**
This Australian fitness star has been posting up a storm on Instagram and won’t be slowing down any time soon. Kayla founded The Bikini Body Training Company and is the author of Bikini Body Guide. Kayla encourages her followers to share their before and after pics as a way to create a tight-knit community that inspires and motivates each other. In 2016, Kayla made it onto TIME Magazine’s list of the 30 Most Influential People on the Internet.
##  **Simeon Panda**** _(8.2_ _M Followers)_**
This fitness entrepreneur has graced the covers of several top magazines and is one of Instagram’s most popular influencers in the health and fitness space. It wasn’t until 2013 that this British trainer started having an impact online but today, Simeon has his own sportswear line and range of fitness accessories. His workout programs continue to be very popular with his followers and fans.
##  [**Dannella Munoz** ](https://www.tiktok.com/@dannellamunoz?utm_source=organic&utm_campaign=direct_blog&utm_medium=organic)** _(129.2K Followers)_**
Dani is a fitness influencer and mother who shares videos showing how she keeps fit postpartum. She also has her own line of gym clothes. She has quickly built up a very engaged following with an average [engagement rate](https://www.upfluence.com/glossary/engagement-rate?utm_source=organic&utm_campaign=direct_blog&utm_medium=organic "engagement rate") of 20%.
## **Lauren Kagan _(4M Followers)_**
In 2013, Lauren made the decision to take her health and fitness a little more seriously, which lead to her becoming a WBFF Bodybuilding Pro. Not only is Lauren a registered nurse but she’s also a personal trainer and fitness model with a passion for adventure. Lauren uses her Instagram account to share workout and nutrition [tips](https://www.upfluence.com/glossary/tips?utm_source=organic&utm_campaign=direct_blog&utm_medium=organic "tips") and advice, with a strong focus on having a positive mindset.
## **Massy Arias _(2.7M Followers)_**
This health coach and fitness entrepreneur is a rising Instagram star that has been featured by several American, European, and Latin-American media outlets. Massy’s unique take on working out is one of the reasons why she is so popular. Not only does she combine cardio, strength training, and yoga routines, but she is known for always finding a way to workout wherever she is. She connects with her followers by encouraging them to post their before and after photos and by sharing sweet photos of her daughter.
## **Emily Skye _(2.5M Followers)_**
Emily is an Australian fitness influencer that is very popular amongst moms who want to stay fit and healthy during their pregnancies and get their pre-pregnancy bodies back. She’s all about setting realistic goals and making family a priority. Her daily workout posts and videos encourage body positivity and healthy lifestyle choices. Emily is a global ambassador for Reebok and regularly blogs about fitness, beauty, and nutrition.
## **[jchristofitness](https://www.tiktok.com/@jchristofitness?utm_source=organic&utm_campaign=direct_blog&utm_medium=organic)  _(345.2K Followers)_**
@jchristofitness is a diabetic fitness coach who has amassed a following of 344K on his [TikTok](https://www.upfluence.com/glossary/tiktok?utm_source=organic&utm_campaign=direct_blog&utm_medium=organic "TikTok") account. He shares tips for how people with diabetes can workout safely as well as bodybuilding tutorials. Within Upfluence’s [software](https://www.upfluence.com/?utm_source=organic&utm_campaign=direct_blog&utm_medium=organic "software") he scores A+ for engagement, views, likes and comments.
## **Joe Wicks _(4.2M Followers)_**
Also known as The Body Coach, Joe aims to transform people’s bodies and lives one healthy meal at a time. Joe’s _Lean in 15_ cookbook series is what first put him on the map as a health and fitness influencer. He has since also developed a 90-day program that incorporates meal plans and workouts that will help his followers reach their goals. Publications such as Grazia, Cosmopolitan, and The Daily Mail, regularly mention Joe, which has boosted his following even further.
## **Alexia Clark (2 _M Followers)_**
This certified nutrition specialist and personal trainer love to switch things up so that she’s always catering to different fitness levels. Alexia, who left her marketing job to pursue her passion for fitness, loves to use a wide variety of equipment to create her workouts so that her followers always have something new to try. She has also created the [_Fit For A Reason_](https://www.alexia-clark.com/?utm_source=organic&utm_campaign=direct_blog&utm_medium=organic) program, which you can sign up for on her website.
## **Lauren Fisher _(1.1M Followers)_**
Lauren is another fitness star that is well known for her performances in six CrossFit Games. Along with her regular training-related posts, Lauren connects with her followers by sharing photos from her life and her travels. While Lauren has made a name for herself in the world of CrossFit, she is also an accomplished weightlifter who has participated in several professional competitions. Grown Strong is a brand that encourages women to tap into their inner strength and is one of Lauren’s main initiatives at the moment.
## **[GrowWithJo](https://www.tiktok.com/@growwithjo?utm_source=organic&utm_campaign=direct_blog&utm_medium=organic)  _(2.4M Followers)_**
Jo shares a mix of fitness and lifestyle content on her TikTok account. She shares vlogs from her everyday life and workout videos. She uses different musical styles in her workouts including Afrobeats and Reggaeton.
## **Shaun T _(1.2M Followers)_**
This fitness star and dad to twins made his mark when he created the Insanity workouts and wrote the book _T is for Transformation_. Shaun’s Instagram account features regular workout tips as well as before and after shots of his clients that he uses to inspire his many followers. Shaun also has created his [own range](https://shauntapparel.com/?utm_source=organic&utm_campaign=direct_blog&utm_medium=organic) of tees, hoodies, tanks, and pants, and even has a podcast that’s available on iTunes and Android.
## **Dylan Werner _(802K Followers)_**
This LA-based yoga teacher is making waves all across the globe. His strength, flexibility, and mobility make for amazing Instagram posts and he regularly runs [workshops, retreats, and events](https://www.dylanwerneryoga.com/?utm_source=organic&utm_campaign=direct_blog&utm_medium=organic) for both yoga students and teachers. Along with having a sizeable following on Instagram, Dylan also has over 10,000 subscribers on YouTube. Dylan regularly partners with brands and sponsors, with Alo Yoga being his most long-standing brand relationship.
## **Sophie van Oostenbrugge _(2.1M Followers)_**
Sophie is a fitness enthusiast based in Amsterdam who has created a website and subscription to fitness plans as well as her [social media content](https://www.upfluence.com/glossary/social-media-content?utm_source=organic&utm_campaign=direct_blog&utm_medium=organic "social media content"). She’s built up a huge audience of over 2M followers on Instagram, and her audience is largely female (63%).
## **Jeanette Jenkins _( 906K Followers)_**
Even though Jeanette works with a number of professional athletes from the NFL and NBA, she still posts workouts that anyone can follow. When she’s not working out in a studio, she can be found streaming from her home-based gym. Jeanette runs a website called [The Hollywood Trainer](https://www.thehollywoodtrainer.com/?utm_source=organic&utm_campaign=direct_blog&utm_medium=organic) that offers a wide selection of programs and tools that can be purchased online.
## Melissa Alcantara _(1M Followers)_
Melissa rose to fame as a celebrity fitness trainer, helping the likes of Kim Kardashian get into shape. She helps people to find the right work out program for them with her own fitness app and dedicated YouTube videos. She specializes in weight training and cardio exercises, as well as nutrition content.
## **[William Li](https://www.tiktok.com/@wiilliamli?utm_source=organic&utm_campaign=direct_blog&utm_medium=organic)  _(3.9M Followers)_**
William is a bodybuilder based in Canada who regularly collaborates with brands to promote protein supplements and nutrition for weight training. He shares workout videos and entertaining videos like his ‘bicep party trick’ and the ‘impossible egg challenge’.
##  **Lisa Migliorini****_(1.4M Followers)_**
Lisa’s workout of choice is going for a run. She uses her [social media platforms](https://www.upfluence.com/glossary/social-media-platforms?utm_source=organic&utm_campaign=direct_blog&utm_medium=organic "social media platforms") to document her runs and encourage others to take up running too. She takes on running challenges like running on snow and in cities around the world including Paris and Barcelona.
