Frequent Questions
Do you have more questions?
What is the most popular plan on the platform?
The Performance Plan is the most popular plan on the platform, primarily because it offers more collaborations, a generous invitation count, and multiple campaigns.
What is a micro-influencer?
The definition of a micro-influencer often differs from various sources. However, at Social Cat, we define micro-influencers as social media users who fall within the 10,000 - 50,000 followers/subscribers bracket.
What types of influencers can be found on Social Cat?
On Social Cat, you can predominantly work with micro-influencers, but we also have many macro-influencers available for both gifted and paid campaigns.
What is a gifted collaboration?
A gifted or barter collaboration is a type of collaboration on social media where influencers receive products for free, and in exchange for that, they post on their social media channels. Gifted collaborations are a great way to start learning, but we recommend transitioning to paid campaigns for more campaign options and deliverables.
How much do influencers charge on Social Cat?
For paid collaborations, influencers on our platform can charge anything between £25 and £500. It depends on the brand, deliverables, and other details.
Can I create gifted and paid campaigns separately?
Yes, gifted, affiliate, or paid campaigns can be created separately, and influencers will apply according to the type of payment. You can also send invitations to influencers for gifted, affiliate, or paid campaigns. However, the multiple campaigns option will be available only on the Growth and the PRO Plans.
