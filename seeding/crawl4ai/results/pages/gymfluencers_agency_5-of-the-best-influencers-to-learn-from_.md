Getting and staying in shape is a constant struggle. It requires dedication and motivation because you can be sure you’ll get discouraged along the way. Therefore, if you’re looking to kickstart your fitness journey or build your online following, it’s essential you have a source of motivation to keep you going. Hence, this list of the best fitness influencers to learn from.
Social media is replete with several [fitness influencers](https://gymfluencers.agency/5-fitness-youtubers-you-need-to-follow/) that post helpful content on their pages. These influencers have made names for themselves in the fitness world by developing great content and inspiring thousands of people to reach their health and fitness goals.
From workout tips and nutrition advice to lifestyle motivation, these five influencers have it all. Here are the five biggest fitness influencers to learn from!
## Top 5 of the Best Influencers to Learn From
### Michelle Lewin (on [Instagram](https://instagram.com/michelle_lewin?igshid=YmMyMTA2M2Y=) and [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@MichelleLewin))
Known for her incredible physique and energetic presence, Michelle Lewin is one of the biggest fitness influencers in the world today. The Venezuelan-born model began her fitness journey after being inspired by the Miami fitness scene and has gone on to compete in several bikini fitness competitions. 
Her Instagram page has amassed over 15 million followers, who consistently look forward to the [video content she creates and shares](https://gymfluencers.agency/top-5-tools-for-creating-and-editing-social-media-videos/) there.
Her positive attitude has inspired many to make better decisions when it comes to their health and wellness. On her page, Michelle showcases her intense workout routines, including weight training and cardio. She also focuses on what it takes to achieve a toned and healthy body by providing advice on meal prepping and nutritional tips.
Michelle’s passion for health and fitness is evident in everything she does and has made her one of the most followed fitness influencers in the world. She’s the owner of two apps: Fitplan and Mealplan.
### Massy Arias (on [Instagram](https://instagram.com/massy.arias?igshid=YmMyMTA2M2Y=) and [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@Mankofit))
Formerly known as Mankofit, Massy Arias is a fitness guru and social media influencer with over 2.5 million followers. She’s known for her incredible body transformations, inspiring others to lead healthy lifestyles, and advocating for self-love. Interestingly, Arias has a fitness app known as Empowered by Massy Arias, a Fabletics collection and a supplement line.
Arias has been in the fitness industry for over seven years, saying fitness “saved her life” when she was battling depression. She often shares photos of her workouts on Instagram, motivating her audience to stay active and strong. She also encourages her followers to post their before and after pictures to inspire others. 
Arias works with individuals from all walks of life, helping them to create and maintain healthy habits. Arias shares meal plans, exercise advice, and motivational words to inspire her audience. Celebrities like Chris Hemsworth and SZA follow her.
### Jen Selter (on [Instagram](https://instagram.com/jenselter?igshid=YmMyMTA2M2Y=) and [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@JenSelter))
Jen Selter is one of the most popular fitness influencers on the internet today. With a reach of over 13 million followers on Instagram, she has become a powerhouse in the health and wellness industry. Jen began her fitness journey while in high school, working at a local gym, but quickly grew an online presence when she started posting her workout photos.
The success of Jen’s career can be attributed to her passion for fitness and commitment to motivating others. She has created a variety of programs that range from beginner-level workouts to more advanced ones. Jen also provides tips and tricks to help people stay motivated and reach their fitness goals.
If you are interested in learning more about fitness and developing healthy habits, Jen Selter is a fantastic resource. With her Instagram posts, website content, and online programs, she provides everything you need to kickstart your fitness journey. Aside from her Instagram posts, Jen also has a website where she shares weekly meal plans, fitness videos, and other helpful information. Jen also has a line of fitness apparel and activewear so that you can work out in style.
### Kayla Itsines (on [Instagram](https://instagram.com/kayla_itsines?igshid=YmMyMTA2M2Y=) and [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@kaylaitsines/))
Co-creator of High Impact with Kayla, formerly known as Bikini Body Guide, Kayla Itsines is a top fitness influencer that has helped thousands improve their health and fitness. The Australian fitness expert who recently welcomed her first child with fiance Jae Woodroffe got certified as a personal trainer for women in 2008. Kayla is also the author of Bikini Body Guides, which she released in 2013.
Kayla’s passion to see people healthy and fit made her start her own fitness app, Sweat, with her then-fiance, Tobi Pearce. After the app’s launch, it brought in a lot of money, surpassing MyFitnessPal and Nike+. Afterwards, the pair then went on to sell the app to IFit, a U.S. fitness company, for $400 million in the middle of 2021. Also, in 2016, Kayla made it to Time Magazine’s list as one of the 30 Most Influential People on the internet, with her fitness app amassing more revenue than other fitness apps. Kayla has over 15 million followers on IG, where she often shares workout routines, meal plans, and health tips.
### Simeon Panda (on [Instagram](https://instagram.com/simeonpanda?igshid=YmMyMTA2M2Y=) and [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@SimeonPanda))
The only male influencer on this list, Simeon Panda, is one of the [top earning fitness influencers](https://gymfluencers.agency/the-biggest-earning-british-influencers-you-need-to-follow/) in the world today. Simeon is easily one of Instagram’s highest-earning influencers and has over eight million followers on Instagram. 
The former Musclemania Pro champion owns fitness brands Just Lift, which focuses on comfy gym apparel and Elimin8, in addition to being a fitness influencer. Simeon also owns a supplement brand known as Innosupps.
A fitness entrepreneur, Simeon was once featured on Forbes as one of the top fitness influencers in the world. His popularity isn’t just a result of his good looks and physique but how well he represents the sport and fitness industry. Simeon’s content is centered on weightlifting and bodybuilding for both newbies and advanced lifters.
Simeon Panda is also adept at repurposing content, and you’ll easily find him on Instagram, YouTube, and even TikTok.
