# How The Influence Room Works For Brands
# Step One
Request a demo of the platform with our team. We’ll work with you to understand your business needs and find a package that suits you.
# Step Two
Create your campaign with opportunities for influencers to bid on. Then review the bids and choose the ones you’d like to work with.
# Step Three
Communicate with your chosen influencers directly on the platform, and get sent the content to be used across your own channels.
# Step Four
Measure the efficacy of your campaigns with the metrics that matter most to you - whether it’s ROI, engagement rate or overall reach and impressions.
# Work With Top Influencers
Discover the best influencers for your brand by browsing bids from advocates who already love you. Whether you’re looking to work with the best creators on TikTok or niche micro-influencers to boost your visibility on Instagram - they’re all here on The Influence Room and waiting to work with you.
