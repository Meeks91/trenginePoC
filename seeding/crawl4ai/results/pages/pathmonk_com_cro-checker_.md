What we find
## Most websites have the sameconversion killers. Unfixed.
Our audits consistently surface the same high-impact issues across hundreds of websites. Here are the critical findings that come up most often, and the ones costing the most revenue.
### No trust signals above the fold
Zero testimonials, review counts, or trust badges are visible without scrolling. In most categories, trust is a purchase prerequisite, and you're making visitors work to find it.
### Navigation built for the owner, not the buyer
Too many top-level categories with no intent-based entry points. First-time visitors don't know where to start, so most of them don't.
### Cart abandonment with no recovery
No abandoned cart emails, exit-intent offers, or retargeting pixels. Industry benchmarks put abandonment at 72-78%. Without recovery, every one of those sessions is permanently lost.
### Zero presence in AI search results
Across every AI search query we test, most brands aren't mentioned once. As buyers shift to conversational search, invisible brands lose discovery that never shows up in traditional SEO rankings.
These issues are found on **the majority of websites we audit** , across B2B, SaaS, and e-commerce. Most teams don't know the exact cost until it's quantified. The CRO Checker puts a number on it.
