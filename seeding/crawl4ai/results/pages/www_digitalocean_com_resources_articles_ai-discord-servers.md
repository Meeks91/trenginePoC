# 10 Best AI Discord Servers to Join in 2025
By [Jess Lulka](https://www.digitalocean.com/community/users/jlulka)
Content Marketing Manager
  * Published: April 29, 2025
  * 8 min read


Learning about technology—especially AI—doesn’t happen in a vacuum. That’s why, as AI adoption grows, more professionals and hobbyists want to find like-minded people to deepen their [machine learning](https://www.digitalocean.com/community/tutorials/an-introduction-to-machine-learning) knowledge and investigate [AI trends](https://www.digitalocean.com/resources/articles/ai-trends). Historically, this might be through in-person meetups or live lectures. Over the past several years, thanks to the internet, more people have found these [AI communities](https://www.digitalocean.com/resources/articles/ai-communities-for-learning) online through applications like Discord.
Discord is an application that allows communication through voice calls, video calls, text messages, and media. Each community is called a server and often centers around a specific topic or project, like AI or machine learning.
To get you started, we’ve rounded up a list of 10 AI Discord servers you can join to expand your overall AI knowledge.
Key takeaways:
  * AI Discord servers are online communities where people interested in artificial intelligence can discuss topics, share resources, and learn from each other in real time via chat, voice, and collaborative channels.
  * The best servers attract AI enthusiasts, students, and professionals alike, often featuring dedicated channels for machine learning help, news on the latest AI research, project showcases, and even mentorship or study groups for learning together.
  * Joining a top AI Discord server in 2025 provides quick access to collective knowledge and support—members can get questions answered faster, find collaborators for projects, and stay motivated by engaging with a community of like-minded individuals passionate about AI.


##  [AI Discord servers to join](https://www.digitalocean.com/resources/articles/ai-discord-servers#ai-discord-servers-to-join)
Each active AI Discord server focuses on AI types, products, learning resources, [AI courses](https://www.digitalocean.com/resources/articles/ai-courses), events, and engagement opportunities. You can customize your experience based on your interest level (amateur, hobbyist, professional) or what information you want to receive regularly.
Anyone can start an AI Discord server. However, there are two main types of servers: ones created by AI companies and ones made by AI communities. Here’s a look at some of the best AI Discord servers available today—whether you want to learn about Google DeepMind’s Gemini 2.0 architecture or dive into fine-tuning techniques for Claude models:
###  [1. DigitalOcean](https://www.digitalocean.com/resources/articles/ai-discord-servers#1-digitalocean)
An extension of our Community site, the [DigitalOcean Discord](https://discord.com/invite/digitalocean) is a place for you to explore, collaborate on, and exhibit all your DigitalOcean AI projects. You can access the most recent information on the GenAI platform, GPU Droplets, and Bare Metal GPUs, and see what questions the community is asking in real time. It’s also an excellent spot for live video content, including coding livestreams, weekly Cloud Chats, [AI podcasts](https://www.digitalocean.com/resources/articles/ai-podcasts), and AI/ML office hours with David vonThenen.
**Discord server highlights**
  * Weekly coding livestreams and DigitalOcean office hours to expand your knowledge about DigitalOcean products and capabilities.
  * Dedicated discussion channels where you can discuss your AI projects, such as GenAI agents and AI-powered websites.
  * Daily access to new how-to guides, tutorials, [and AI blogs](https://www.digitalocean.com/resources/articles/ai-blogs).


###  [2. OpenAI](https://www.digitalocean.com/resources/articles/ai-discord-servers#2-openai)
The official [OpenAI Discord server](https://discord.gg/openai) is a community where you can connect, engage, and explore the latest offerings of ChatGPT, Sora, and OpenAI models. You can customize the information you want regarding OpenAI announcements and product updates. Individual channels are available to discuss GPTs, Sora, AI-generated images, prompt engineering tricks, developer projects, and OpenAI feedback.
**Discord server highlights**
  * Server-specific events include Sora Office Hours, Canvas feedback, and developer livestreams.
  * Themed Sora challenges and a #hall-of-fame channel for top-voted Sora and DALL-E generations.
  * Developers’ Corner, a set of channels where you can discuss using the OpenAI API, get help, and share projects.


###  [3. Anthropic](https://www.digitalocean.com/resources/articles/ai-discord-servers#3-anthropic)
[Anthropic’s Discord server](https://discord.com/invite/zkrBaqytPW) is designed for [Claude.ai](http://Claude.ai) users. It’s a community where AI enthusiasts and developers can collaborate and build applications with Anthropic’s API. Several channels are dedicated to company, product, and Discord server announcements. With community channels, you can ask general AI questions, get feedback on prompts, report suspected model changes, and share projects. As a developer, you can access discussions on Anthropic’s API, secure deployments, and [Model Context Protocol](https://www.digitalocean.com/community/tutorials/model-context-protocol).
**Discord server highlights**
  * Dedicated #question-of-the-week thread where you can learn more about the community’s users and engage in stimulating discussions.
  * Featured Projects, where you can see the community-voted Project of the Month.
  * A channel to report suspected model changes and regressions.


###  [4. Hugging Face](https://www.digitalocean.com/resources/articles/ai-discord-servers#4-hugging-face)
[Hugging Face’s Discord server](https://discord.gg/hugging-face-879548962464493619) is a community for machine learning users. You can find channels to discuss the ethics and politics of machine learning, robots with open source technology, technical help, and get feedback on your projects. Hugging Face also provides a space to discuss open-source machine learning, with dedicated channels for [computer vision](https://www.digitalocean.com/resources/articles/computer-vision), [natural language processing](https://www.digitalocean.com/resources/articles/natural-language-processing), and dataset sharing. This server requires a Hugging Face account, which you can get for free.
**Discord server highlights**
  * Product-specific channels to learn and discuss FastRTC, Core Diffusers, and the AI Agent Course.
  * Robotics channels where you can chat about open-source robotics and Pollen Robotics.
  * A regular reading group that hosts events to discuss AI and machine learning related literature.


###  [5. Mistral AI](https://www.digitalocean.com/resources/articles/ai-discord-servers#5-mistral-ai)
The [Mistral AI Discord](https://discord.gg/J6AdSuKM) is a discussion space for Mistral AI users and enthusiasts. It has channels to discuss your applications and prompts and get help with Plateforme and Le Chat. You can also provide feedback through dedicated failed prompts, bug reporting, and feature request channels. Mistral AI employees host dedicated virtual office hours, post announcements, and highlight new features.
**Discord server highlights:**
  * Miscord chatbot, where you can ask questions about documentation and services.
  * Show-and-tell channels to share the applications you’ve made with Mistral and successful prompts.
  * A dedicated thread for sharing AI resources, facts, and related resources of interest.


###  [6. Midjourney](https://www.digitalocean.com/resources/articles/ai-discord-servers#6-midjourney)
Midjourney AI lets you create images based on text prompts. You can use the [Midjourney Discord](https://discord.gg/midjourney) to learn how Midjourney works, craft image prompts, and have general product discussions. There’s also space to publicize your Midjourney projects that have made it into the real world (including jewelry creation, 3D printed objects, and tarot cards). To join this server, you must have a Midjourney account and have a paid subscription for full server access.
**Discord server highlights**
  * Channels for newcomers to learn more about Midjourney and how to generate images.
  * Threads to get help with prompt creation and feedback.
  * Regularly scheduled events to discuss digital art and character generation. You can also attend weekly Midjourney office hours.


###  [7. Learn AI Together](https://www.digitalocean.com/resources/articles/ai-discord-servers#7-learn-ai-together)
Developed by Louis-Francois Bouchard, the [Learn AI Together Discord server](https://discord.com/invite/learnaitogether) is designed to make AI more accessible regardless of your expertise. The community has lots to explore, including general discussions around AI, Bouchard’s collection of “What’s AI” content, job postings, and AI news. It promotes community through resource sharing, project collaboration channels, events, seminars, a weekly reading group, and product review threads.
**Discord server highlights**
  * A #for-hire channel, where you can share your professional profile and experience with other community members and companies.
  * Specific threads dedicated to helping you find study groups, work project groups, and promoting collaboration.
  * Partnership with Learn Prompting to increase [prompt engineering](https://www.digitalocean.com/resources/articles/prompt-engineering-best-practices) knowledge and company updates.


###  [8. Learn Prompting](https://www.digitalocean.com/resources/articles/ai-discord-servers#8-learn-prompting)
Sander Schulhoff and Fady Yanni created the [Learn Prompting community](https://discord.com/invite/learn-prompting-1046228027434086460) to help ChatGPT users maximize their outputs and build better AI prompt documentation. This server is designed for prompt engineering enthusiasts and professionals. Its main goal is to help you learn more about prompt engineering, find valuable resources, and discuss the latest AI news. It also includes dedicated channels for community updates, support questions, job postings, and self-promotion.
**Discord server highlights**
  * Regular open office hours to discuss Learn Prompting, GenAI, or prompt engineering.
  * A HackaBot AI assistant that helps you learn prompt injections and red-teaming.
  * Dedicated #prompt-hacking channel to chat about jailbreaks and [HackAPrompt 2.0](https://www.hackaprompt.com/).


###  [9. ](https://www.digitalocean.com/resources/articles/ai-discord-servers#9-fetch-ai)[Fetch.ai](http://Fetch.ai)
[Fetch.ai](http://Fetch.ai)’s offerings let you develop and deploy [multi-agent systems](https://www.digitalocean.com/resources/articles/multiagent-system) via an open [AI agents](https://www.digitalocean.com/resources/articles/ai-agents) marketplace. The official [Fetch.ai Discord server](https://discord.com/invite/sEjfFYbBSR) has channels for company news, product updates, and Agentverse and DeltaV support. It also has specific discussions for testing and interacting with new applications, such as [asi1.ai](http://asi1.ai), the first agentic [large language model](https://www.digitalocean.com/resources/articles/large-language-models) (LLM). You’ll also see threads dedicated to specific in-person events, [Fetch.ai](http://Fetch.ai) agent support, and its microagent framework.
**Discord server highlights**
  * Specific channels for CosmPy developer support and [Allify.io](http://Allify.io) agent hacks.
  * Semi-regular hackathon challenges and discussions related to in-person AI events and innovation summits.
  * Live workshops that cover [Fetch.ai](http://Fetch.ai) features, products, and specific agents.


###  [10. ElevenLabs](https://www.digitalocean.com/resources/articles/ai-discord-servers#10-elevenlabs)
Focused on AI-generated text-to-speech, [ElevenLabs’ Discord server](https://discord.com/invite/elevenlabs) allows you to access documentation, discuss the latest product news, and present work. It has several custom bots that provide answers to common questions or test out ElevenLabs’ services. You’ll also find dedicated channels to discuss developments and features for [conversational AI](https://www.digitalocean.com/resources/articles/conversational-ai-platforms) LLMs, voice design, and the Reader App.
**Discord server highlights**
  * Channels for important product updates, special opportunities, and ongoing incremental changes.
  * Opportunities for you to share your ElevenLabs-generated audiobooks, API projects, audios, and videos.
  * Direct support channels for ElevenLabs products and related technology.


##  [AI Discord servers FAQ](https://www.digitalocean.com/resources/articles/ai-discord-servers#ai-discord-servers-faq)
**What types of AI Discord servers are available and how do they differ?**
There are two main types of AI Discord servers: those created by AI companies (like OpenAI, Anthropic, and Midjourney) and community-driven servers (like Learn AI Together). Company servers focus on specific products and updates, while community servers emphasize learning, collaboration, and general AI discussions across different platforms and expertise levels.
**Which AI Discord servers are best for beginners learning about AI?**
Learn AI Together, Learn Prompting, and OpenAI Discord servers are excellent for beginners as they provide accessible learning resources regardless of expertise level. These servers offer dedicated channels for newcomers, study groups, resource sharing, and project collaboration to help beginners build their AI knowledge foundation.
**What can you expect to find in company-specific AI Discord servers?**
Company servers like OpenAI, Anthropic, and Midjourney offer product announcements, developer support channels, API discussions, and direct access to company representatives. They also feature specialized events like office hours, product feedback sessions, themed challenges, and showcases where users can share projects built with company tools.
**How can AI Discord servers help with professional development and networking?**
AI Discord servers provide opportunities for professional networking through dedicated job posting channels, for-hire sections, and collaboration spaces for finding project partners. They also offer regular events like reading groups, hackathons, workshops, and live streams that help members stay updated on industry trends and expand their professional connections.
**How do I find AI Discord servers?**
You can find AI Discord servers through official vendor websites or by searching Google for “AI Discord servers” or “AI communities on Discord.”
