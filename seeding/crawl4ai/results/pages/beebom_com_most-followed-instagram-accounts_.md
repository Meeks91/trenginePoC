## Most Followed Accounts on Instagram 
### 1. Instagram
  * **Follower Count:** 698 Million


Call it irony, or fate, but the **top-most followed Instagram account** is none other than Instagram’s official page itself. With over 698 million total followers at the time of writing, it is far ahead of any other account on this list. The handle actively uploads posts and videos from across the globe, belonging to various other content creators on Instagram.
These posts range from educational and inspirational to downright wholesome, allowing them to be at the top of this list with such a huge follower base. Besides, nothing screams _king more_ than Instagram dominating its very own platform, right?
Follow @instagram
### 2. Cristiano Ronaldo
  * **Follower Count:** 670 Million


It should come as no surprise that Cristiano Ronaldo is the most-followed personal Instagram account in the world. The 41-year-old professional footballer has amassed a whopping 670 million followers on Instagram, to be specific, at the time of writing this. I’m pretty sure by the time you read this, the numbers will have gone up significantly.
As per a [report from CNN](https://edition.cnn.com/videos/business/2023/08/11/exp-ronaldo-instagram-earnings-081104aseg2-cnni-business.cnn#:~:text=Cristiano%20Ronaldo%20earns%20a%20whopping,on%20the%20social%20media%20site.), he is also the most expensive Instagrammer, as he charges approximately **$3.23 Million per sponsored post**!
Follow @cristiano
### 3. Lionel Messi
  * **Follower Count:** 511 Million


Now, I don’t want Messi and Ronaldo fans fighting in the comments section. Or, maybe I do. This is not about who’s the best footballer in the world, but has more to do with actual data. Right after Ronaldo, we have Lionel Messi coming in hot with over 511 million followers on the social media platform, making him the **third most-followed** Instagram account. You can see a mixture of Instagram Posts, Reels, and sponsored posts on Messi’s Instagram handle.
Follow @leomessi

## 4. Selena Gomez 
  * **Follower Count:** 415 Million


Next up, we have the Come & Get It singer, who has raked in a mind-boggling 415 million followers on Instagram over the years. This is a little lower than it was last year. But still, Salena Gomez manages to be the 4th most followed account on Instagram which is no easy feat. Many of you _may_ be wondering why the 33-year-old singer and performer has so many followers on Instagram.
Well, Selena is known for _not faking it_ and pouring her heart out to her followers on Instagram. The Wizards of Waverly Place star also has a special place in the hearts of many kids across the globe. If you take a look at her Instagram handle, you will see that most of her Instagram **posts are from and about the _real_ world**. Maybe this is exactly why she was once the most followed Instagram account in the world, back in 2017, before Ronaldo surpassed her.
Follow @selenagomez
