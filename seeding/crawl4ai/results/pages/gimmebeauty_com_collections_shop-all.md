###  Which detangling brush should I pick? 
Whether you have thick, medium, or fine hair, we've got you covered! The bristles on each brush are optimized with a combo of length and flexibility that are proven to glide through your unique hair type with a softer pull to reduce damage.
**Thick Hair Brush** has the longest and most rigid bristles — designed to tackle tough tangles on thick or dense hair, giving you a smoother finish.
**Medium Hair Brush** is a great in-between option — its mid-length, medium-flex bristles work well with most hair types.
**Fine Hair Brush** has the shortest and most flexible bristles — perfect for gently detangling fine and delicate hair types, with ease.
