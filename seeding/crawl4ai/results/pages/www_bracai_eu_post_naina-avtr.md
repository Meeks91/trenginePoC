## Quick facts: Naina Avtr
  * Is Naina Avtr real? No. She’s a digitally crafted persona
  * Platform: YouTube
  * Created by: Avtr Meta Labs (India)
  * Created in: 2022
  * Content themes: fitness and fashion, plus trending dances and travel-style posts


## **The “body double” model**
Unlike other AI influencers, Naina is not presented as fully CGI animation.
Her production has been described as using “body double technology”. A human performer provides the movement, then AI post-production applies the character identity consistently.
Why that matters: you get real-world motion without the cost and stiffness of pure CGI.

## What you can learn from **Naina Avtr**
  * Start with a content system, not a character render
    * Decide formats first
  * Build one visual hook that repeats forever
    * Recognition beats lore
  * Treat it like IP, not a creator relationship
  * Don’t overclaim results
    * If you can’t measure it, don’t promise it.
  * Make disclosure boring and clear
    * The safest AI influencer campaigns aren’t mysterious. They’re transparent
