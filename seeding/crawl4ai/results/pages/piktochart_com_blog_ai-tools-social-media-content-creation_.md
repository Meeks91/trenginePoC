## How We Picked The 10 Best AI Tools For Social Media Content Creation

## 9. Loom AI 
With InVideo, you use AI to create a video from a text prompt. With [Loom](https://www.loom.com/)’s AI tools you create a text-based document from a video.
Social media content creators will appreciate the way Loom allows them to demonstrate use of software, correct an online problem, or present screen-based information available on demand. 
To test Loom’s capabilities, I created a short screencast demonstrating how I analyze LinkedIn job descriptions to understand what keywords to use in a resume targeting that position. 
I used the Loom app on my phone to record the video, then switched to the desktop app to finish the process. 
Reviewing the video, I found a lot of filler words like “um” and “ahh.” Using Loom’s filler word reducer (powered by AI), the tool removed 11 filler words and shortened the video by about 3 seconds.
With the video I wanted, I created a step-by-step guide using the tool. The guide can be used to create another social media asset (such as an Instagram carousel), extending the usefulness of the original content. 
Loom’s recording, rendering and output process were fast. I calculated I could do an ongoing series of posts using Loom to post videos for True North Resumes on LinkedIn, Facebook or Instagram, taking less than an hour a week to go from idea to finished product.
My only regrets with the Loom platform are that it lacks advanced editing tools, and there are so few types of text-based output that can be derived from the video.
