**Key Features & Benefits** | 
  * **Predefined styles:** Skip long prompts and apply stunning styles instantly.
  * **Sketch & Layer Tools:** Gain control over image layout and composition.
  * **AI Photo Filter:** Turn photos into artistic cartoons.
  * **Generative Fill:** Add/edit objects with text prompts.
  * **Background Removal & HD Export:** Easily remove backgrounds and export in up to 6144×6144px resolution.
  * **User-Friendly UI:** Built for both beginners and pros.
