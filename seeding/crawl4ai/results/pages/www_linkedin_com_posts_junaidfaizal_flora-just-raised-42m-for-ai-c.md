#  Flora Raises $42M for AI Creative Tools, Revolutionizing Filmmaking
This title was summarized by AI from the post below.
Flora just raised $42M for AI creative tools. As someone making videos with AI daily, here's what I'm seeing: the tools are finally good enough. The bottleneck isn't the tech anymore. It's knowing how to direct it. Filmmakers who learn to prompt will eat. Those who dismiss AI as "not real filmmaking" are going to get left behind.
FLORA has raised a $42M Series A, led by [Redpoint](https://www.linkedin.com/company/redpointventures?trk=public_post_reshare-text), to build a unified creative environment for the generative era. As a team of creatives, we started [FLORA](https://www.linkedin.com/company/floraai?trk=public_post_reshare-text) to solve our own frustrations: existing AI platforms felt like toys designed for consumers, not professionals. And accessing the best models meant fragmenting your workflow across multiple platforms. FLORA unifies the best creative AI models across text, image and video into one workflow to accelerate your creative process. With team workspaces, real-time collaboration, and unlimited seats, FLORA is designed for the creative team of the future. Today, millions of creatives are using FLORA across brand & marketing, product visualization, film/VFX, and more – compressing weeks of iterative work into hours. We’re grateful to [Alex Bard](https://www.linkedin.com/in/alexbard?trk=public_post_reshare-text), [Jordan Segall](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jordan-segall-7b938632?trk=public_post_reshare-text), and the team at Redpoint for leading this round, with participation from the founders of [Vercel](https://www.linkedin.com/company/vercel?trk=public_post_reshare-text) ([Guillermo Rauch](https://www.linkedin.com/in/rauchg?trk=public_post_reshare-text)), [Frame.io](https://www.linkedin.com/company/frame-io?trk=public_post_reshare-text) ([Emery Wells](https://www.linkedin.com/in/emery-wells-5624161?trk=public_post_reshare-text)), and [fal](https://www.linkedin.com/company/features-and-labels?trk=public_post_reshare-text) ([Burkay Gur](https://www.linkedin.com/in/burkaygur?trk=public_post_reshare-text), [Gorkem Yurtseven](https://www.linkedin.com/in/gorkemy?trk=public_post_reshare-text), and [Batuhan T.](https://www.linkedin.com/in/batuhan-t-7803b61a0?trk=public_post_reshare-text)). This brings our total capital raised to $52M, alongside existing investors including [Mike Volpi](https://www.linkedin.com/in/mavolpi?trk=public_post_reshare-text) at [Hanabi Capital](https://www.linkedin.com/company/hanabi-capital?trk=public_post_reshare-text), [Amy Wu Martin](https://www.linkedin.com/in/amytongwu?trk=public_post_reshare-text) at [Menlo Ventures](https://www.linkedin.com/company/menlo-ventures?trk=public_post_reshare-text), [a16z speedrun](https://www.linkedin.com/school/a16zspeedrun/?trk=public_post_reshare-text), [Long Journey](https://www.linkedin.com/company/longjourney?trk=public_post_reshare-text), [Company Ventures](https://www.linkedin.com/company/company-ventures?trk=public_post_reshare-text), [Alumni Ventures](https://www.linkedin.com/company/alumniventures?trk=public_post_reshare-text), [Justin Kan](https://www.linkedin.com/in/justinkan?trk=public_post_reshare-text) (founder of [Twitch](https://www.linkedin.com/company/twitch-tv?trk=public_post_reshare-text)), [Cyan Banister](https://www.linkedin.com/in/cyanb?trk=public_post_reshare-text), [Matthew Hartman](https://www.linkedin.com/in/matthewforresthartman?trk=public_post_reshare-text) at [Factorial Capital](https://www.linkedin.com/company/factorialcap?trk=public_post_reshare-text), Gabe Whaley (founder of MSCHF), and more.
