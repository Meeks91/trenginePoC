It's streamlining the monitoring and analysis of vast data sets to detect anomalies, thereby enhancing cybersecurity measures. LogRhythm has partnered with D3 Security to enhance threat management and incident response in its cloud-native LogRhythm Axon SIEM system. This collaboration aims to improve operations for security teams by allowing them to better detect threats and prioritize responses with refined security intelligence.
The partnership combines LogRhythm's expertise in transforming scattered data into actionable insights with D3 Security's proficiency in security orchestration, automation, and response (SOAR). The integration of LogRhythm Axon SIEM with D3’s Smart SOAR solutions automates incident enrichment and response, empowering security professionals to focus on critical security challenges more effectively.
Andrew Hollister, CISO at LogRhythm, stressed the growing need for advanced tools to combat escalating cyber threats, stating that this collaboration equips security teams with the tools necessary to protect their organizations efficiently. The benefits of this integration include:
Rapid Response: LogRhythm Axon SIEM’s alerts trigger D3 Smart SOAR’s automated actions for quicker incident resolutions.
Automated Workflows: Streamlines processes by automating data enrichment and correlation, reducing manual tasks.
Centralized Management: Enables MSSPs to manage multiple deployments through a single D3 Smart SOAR interface, simplifying operations.
The partnership also facilitates content management across multiple LogRhythm Axon SIEM tenants and enhances the overall health monitoring and maintenance of security systems.
Amardeep Dhingra, Director of Strategic Alliances at D3 Security, emphasized that this integration boosts visibility and operational efficiency, helping security teams manage complex information and tool arrays more effectively, thereby strengthening defenses against modern cybersecurity threats.
