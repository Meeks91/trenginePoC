## FAQ
### What brands have you worked with?
I’ve had the pleasure of working with a variety of amazing brands, including Shamrock Farms, Panera, Chop Shop, Jollibee, Mr. Tortilla, Frok, Mila and Nordic Catch. Each collaboration allowed me to create engaging content that resonated with my foodie audience, from showcasing new menu items to highlighting unique products. 
### Who is your audience?
My audience is made up of food lovers, adventurous eaters, and home cooks who love trying new things. They’re mostly millennials and Gen Z, who follow me for my honest food reviews, restaurant recommendations, product unboxings, and easy-to-follow recipe videos. They’re always looking for the next must-try dish or gadget and trust me to show them what’s worth their time and taste buds!
