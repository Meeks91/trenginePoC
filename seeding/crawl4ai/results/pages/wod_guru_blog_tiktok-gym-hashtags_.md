### What’s the Best Hashtag Placement Strategy?
The _ultimate guide_ to placing hashtags on TikTok in 2025 comes down to balance and precision. The most effective method? Use your _personal trainer hashtags_ and other _related hashtags_ in the video _caption_ rather than just as on-screen text. This ensures the algorithm can read and index them properly. 
Good to know!Aim for **3–5 high-performing hashtags** plus **2–3 branded tags** —enough to increase discoverability without overcrowding the 2,200-character _caption character limit_.
However, featuring one or two hashtags visually in the video can still enhance brand recognition or humor. The trending formula is to combine viral hashtags (e.g., #GymTok), niche-specific tags (e.g., #KettlebellStrength), and branded ones (e.g., #TrainWithJess).
### Banned Hashtags - what’s that? 
Not all hashtags are safe to use. Some terms get flagged or shadowbanned by TikTok for violating community guidelines—even if they seem harmless. These banned hashtags can drastically reduce your video’s reach or make it invisible altogether. 
Often, creators unknowingly use the same hashtags repeatedly or include outdated slang that’s been blacklisted. To protect your content, always check whether a hashtag is still active and safe to use, especially before uploading a new post. 
A strong TikTok strategy involves regularly auditing your hashtag list to avoid penalties and stay ahead of the algorithm.

## Best Practices for Maximizing TikTok Gym Hashtag Performance
  * **Mix and Match:** The optimal strategy involves a blend of broad, niche, trending, and location-specific tags.
  * **Relevance is King:** Only use hashtags that genuinely relate to your video content to ensure you attract the right audience.
  * **Test and Learn:** Don’t just set it and forget it. Experiment with different hashtag combinations and monitor your analytics to see what drives the most views and engagement for _your_ specific content and audience.
  * **Caption vs. Comments:** While TikTok mainly considers hashtags in the caption, testing additional, highly relevant tags in the first comment can sometimes provide a slight boost.
  * **Keep it Concise:** TikTok recommends 3-8 hashtags, but avoids stuffing. Focus on quality over quantity.
  * **Stay Updated:** TikTok trends change rapidly. Regularly research new **best hashtags for tiktok** gym content and trending sounds.
