## **Cutting-Edge Tools Behind TikTok’s AI Influencers**
AI TikTok influencers did not just come about out of nowhere. AI tools were needed to generate and use them for multiple purposes. 
This section will explore the technologies that allowed AI Influencers to rise on the TikTok platform. It will focus on AI-generated content and TikTok’s avatar tools.
### **How does AI generate lifelike digital personas for TikTok?**
AI, machine learning (ML), and computer graphics work together for a digital persona to be generated. The process is as follows:
  1. **Data Collection -** Large datasets of human images, videos, and behaviors are collected to teach AI models. Once this is done, [AI models can predict or act on data](https://www.hpe.com/cz/en/what-is/ai-models.html) that was not observed before.
  2. **AI and Machine Learning -** ML algorithms are trained on collected data, [especially deep learning models.](https://www.coursera.org/articles/deep-learning-models) These models are taught to recognize patterns in facial expressions, body movements, voice tones, and other human features. 
  3. **3D Modelling and Animation -** 3D avatars are then designed and animated through rigging and motion capture to replicate and imitate real-life actions.
  4. **Voice Synthesis -**[ Text-to-speech systems](https://influencermarketinghub.com/text-to-speech-on-tiktok/) (like the one available on TikTok) and voice cloning applications online give the digital persona a natural-sounding voice. The content of the persona’s conversations can be based on scripts written by humans or scripts that are written using [content creation software tools.](https://influencermarketinghub.com/ai-content-creation-software/)
  5. **Behavioral Algorithms -** AI-driven behavioral algorithms are applied to simulate human personality traits. [Natural language processing (NLP)](https://aws.amazon.com/what-is/nlp/#:~:text=Natural%20language%20processing%20\(NLP\)%20combines,Computational%20linguistics) is used to allow the persona to engage in real-life conversations.
  6. **TikTok Integration -** The digital persona is posted on TikTok to interact with users.
  7. **Update AI Models -** New data is always added to AI models, enhancing the persona’s accuracy and engagement over time.


### **What is the role of automation and deep learning in the consistency of your content?**
Automation handles your routine tasks and [ensures a steady production schedule.](https://influencermarketinghub.com/ai-social-media/) This allows a consistent and reliable flow of content.
On the other hand, deep learning keeps your piece aligned with your desired style and tone. It also adapts your content to what your audience wants without losing consistency.
Together, they create content that is both cohesive and responsive to the needs of your audience.
## **TikTok’s Avatar Tools**
### **TikTok Symphony Avatars**
_Source:[newsroom.tiktok.com](https://newsroom.tiktok.com/en-us/express-yourself-through-tiktok-avatars)_
On June 27, 2024, TikTok launched [Symphony Digital Avatars.](https://newsroom.tiktok.com/en-us/announcing-symphony-avatars) Together with AI Dubbing and Symphony Collective, they form part of the company’s generative AI (Gen AI) ad solutions, [Symphony.](https://ads.tiktok.com/business/creativecenter/tools/tiktok-symphony/pc/en?rid=9lxjkyzu30d)
[With 58% of TikTok users](https://ads.tiktok.com/business/creativecenter/insight/creativeinsight/mobile/en) who are probably going to trust brands they learn about from creators, Symphony Digital Avatars give brands and creators a chance to reach global audiences and share impactful messages in an immersive and authentic manner.
Symphony Digital Avatars offers two ways for brands and creators to showcase their creativity and individuality to TikTok users. These are:
  1. **Stock Avatars -** These are pre-made avatars created using paid actors and licensed for commercial purposes. Stock Avatars allow companies to add a personal touch to their content with quick and easy access to global creators from different backgrounds and nationalities who speak more than 30 languages.
  2. **Custom Avatars -** These are designed to portray a creator or brand spokesperson who can speak multiple languages. They give creators and marketers the ability to improve their brand presence and recognition. Creators can use these avatars to show their images and create multilingual versions to widen their global reach and brand partnerships. Meanwhile, brands use these avatars to incorporate their brand identity, spokesperson, or partnered creator to facilitate easy collaboration with global creators and localize their international campaigns.


Through follows and likes, TikTok users can interact directly with these Digital Avatars. Similarly, the avatar can respond to user comments, driving up further engagement.

## **Navigating the Challenges of AI Influencers**
### **Balancing Authenticity and Innovation**
According to a [2023 survey conducted by KPMG Australia,](https://kpmg.com/xx/en/home/insights/2023/09/trust-in-artificial-intelligence.html) 61% of consumers were cautious about trusting AI systems, and a [2024 YouGov survey](https://today.yougov.com/technology/articles/49099-americans-2024-poll-ai-top-feeling-caution) found that 54% of Americans felt the same. Such skepticism is normal for new technologies like AI, especially when its outputs can seem generic and disengaging.
To address these concerns, businesses should disclose that AI influencers are computer-generated and not real people. Moreover, AI should be managed by humans to avoid sharing confusing information with customers. These measures can help businesses build trust and credibility with their customers.
### **Ethical Considerations**
AI Influencers can be biased as they only follow what their owners have programmed them to do. Because of this, customers—especially the younger ones—could be manipulated into thinking that the company’s offerings are the best even if they’re not. 
In addition, AI Influencers could gather customer data through their comments or any of their online activity. This can pose a threat to the customer’s privacy. These ethical challenges of AI must be addressed by businesses through transparency with their customers.
