In today’s competitive world, all businesses are looking for a way to introduce their products to others. Many of them try to acquire [social media accounts](https://socialtradia.com/sell-instagram-account/) from existing influencers and creators who have thousands of followers.
But one of the ways that brands and businesses use recently to present their products is to hold online advertising campaigns and collaborate with famous people on social media platforms.

In this article, we will explain all the information you need to know about how a TikTok Creator Marketplace works.
## What is TikTok Creator Marketplace?
TikTok Creator Marketplace was introduced and launched in 2019. It is where TikTok influencers and creators are active and present their products, collaborate with other brands, promote their products, and earn money through advertising.
With the advancement of technology, businesses have distanced themselves from traditional and old advertisements and are increasingly trying to use virtual media such as TikTok creator marketplace for advertising their products because, in this way, their advertising videos have millions of views and will increase their sales.
Businesses are looking for the best TikTok content creators to get the best results from their ads.
This marketplace is available for creators with at least 10,000 followers, giving them the necessary audience to introduce products to them.
In order to work on this platform, all TikTok creators must have some qualifications that we will examine later.
But how can brands and businesses join TikTok Creator Marketplace and cooperate with creators? We will explain this in the next part.
## How can brands join the TikTok creator marketplace?
As said, brands and businesses can cooperate with the creator who has their target audience and introduce their products to different people.
But to do this, they have to join this creator marketplace platform, and we will explain how to do so.
To join TikTok Creator Marketplace, TikTok creators must follow the steps below:
_Step 1)_ create an account and enter all the required information, such as your name and surname, contact number, and work email
_Step 2)_ After you create your account, you need to customize it; for this, you can add your company logo as a profile picture or write a short captain about your company and describe it.
_Step 3)_ After completing the mentioned steps, you should find the influencer you want to cooperate with and send them a message, register your request for collaboration and wait for a response.
In addition to this, if you already have a TikTok Ads Manager account, you can use it to enter TikTok Creator Marketplace as a brand and start using its benefits.
## How to join the TikTok creator marketplace as an influencer?
TikTok creator marketplace is one of the best features that allows influencers and content creators on TikTok to earn money through product promotion.
The important thing is that this feature is not available for all creators, and for influencers to be able to use this feature, they must have qualifications such as:
  * Have at least 100,000 followers on TikTok
  * Their TikTok content has received at least 100,000 likes in the last 28 days
  * Have posted an average of 5 posts in the last 28 days
  * Be at least 18 years old


If you have these conditions, you can use this marketplace to collaborate with different brands and make money through this promotion.
In addition, remember that you can promote your own products in this TikTok’s creator marketplace, which leads to getting more attention and more customers.
## How to find the best creator to collaborate with
By now, you have realized how you can help your business if cooperate with active creators in TikTok’s Creator Marketplace.
But the most important thing is choosing the best influencer to promote your products and brand.
To do this, you need to follow the points that we will examine below:
  * Find the topic you want to promote by influencer marketing using the creator filter option.
  * Try to use the creator filter to find a creator who lives in your country and region.
  * Before sending a collaboration request to an influencer, check their biography and try to understand what other brands which have collaborated with them have to say about them.
  * Pay attention to the number of likes, views, and comments on their posts.
  * Try to find out the age of the influencer’s target audience using the audience filter.


By following these tips, you can find and collaborate with the best creators and get the best results.

## Advantages and disadvantages of TikTok creator marketplace
Using this marketplace to promote products is a win-win game for creators and businesses.
However, this feature has advantages and disadvantages, which include:
### Advantages
  * It helps the creators to have the opportunity to cooperate with different brands, which increases their income
  * It helps brands and businesses find more customers and make more sales
  * It allows businesses to choose the influencer they want according to their budget, so it’s budget-friendly
  * You can access the complete information of the creator you intend to cooperate with
  * It brings brand awareness to businesses


### Disadvantages
  * Business owners have to negotiate contracts with each creator, which may not be unpleasant for some people.
  * It can be challenging to find the best influencer that suits your needs
  * You can’t talk to the creators directly; you can only send them a link inviting them to cooperate. This can be frustrating for some people.
