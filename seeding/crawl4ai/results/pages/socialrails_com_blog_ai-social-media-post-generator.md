##  How AI Generators Work (Simple Version)
**Step 1:** You enter a prompt _Example: "Write an Instagram post about productivity tips for remote workers"_
**Step 2:** AI analyzes your request
  * Identifies the topic (productivity)
  * Notes the platform (Instagram)
  * Considers the audience (remote workers)


**Step 3:** AI generates options
  * Usually 2-5 variations
  * Different angles and hooks
  * Platform-appropriate length


**Step 4:** You edit and personalize
  * Add your examples
  * Adjust the voice
  * Fix any awkward phrasing

##  Platform-Specific Tips
###  Instagram
  * Ask for carousel slide breakdowns (or use an [AI carousel maker](https://socialrails.com/blog/ai-carousel-maker))
  * Request hook-first structure
  * Include [hashtag generation](https://socialrails.com/blog/best-instagram-hashtags)
  * Specify if for feed, stories, or reels


###  LinkedIn
  * Request professional but conversational tone
  * Ask for personal insights angle (great for [consultants](https://socialrails.com/blog/social-media-for-consultants) and [coaches](https://socialrails.com/blog/social-media-marketing-for-coaches))
  * Keep [hashtags to 3-5](https://socialrails.com/blog/best-hashtags-for-linkedin)
  * Include engagement question at end


###  TikTok
  * Ask for script format with hook
  * Keep it conversational
  * Request trend-aware language
  * Specify video length (check [best times to post on TikTok](https://socialrails.com/blog/best-time-to-post-on-tiktok))


###  Twitter/X
  * Request thread format for longer content
  * Keep individual tweets punchy
  * Ask for variations to test

##  The Batch Creation Workflow
Here's a simple weekly system:
**Monday (60-90 minutes):**
  1. List 5-7 topics for the week
  2. Generate 2-3 drafts per topic using AI
  3. Pick the best version of each
  4. Edit for voice and accuracy


**Tuesday (30 minutes):**
  1. Add visuals to each post
  2. [Schedule for the week](https://socialrails.com/blog/social-media-scheduler-complete-guide)
  3. Prepare hashtag sets


**Rest of Week (15 min/day):**
  1. Engage with comments
  2. Note what performs well
  3. Adjust future topics


##  Measuring What Works
Track these for AI-assisted posts:
  * **Engagement rate:** Compare to your non-AI posts
  * **Time saved:** Track actual hours saved weekly
  * **Edit ratio:** How much do you change each draft?
  * **Performance:** Are AI posts performing similarly?


If AI posts consistently underperform, you're probably not editing enough.
##  Quick Answers
**Will AI content get flagged by platforms?** No. Platforms don't detect or penalize AI-assisted content. They care about quality, not origin.
**How much should I edit AI drafts?** Plan to change at least 20-30%. Add your examples, fix awkward phrasing, and adjust for your voice.
**Can AI replace my creativity?** No. AI generates drafts. Your creativity shapes them into something worth reading.
**What about content ideas, not just writing?** AI excels at brainstorming. Ask for "10 content ideas about [topic] for [audience]" and you'll rarely draw a blank again.
