## FAQs
**How can I find TikTok influencers for my brand?**
You can discover TikTok influencers by using tools like TikTok’s Creator Marketplace or using specific platforms like Influencer Hero with advanced filters to easily identify what creators align best with your brand.
**What is the best tool to search for TikTok influencers?**
The best two platforms to find TikTok influencers are TikTok’s Creator Marketplace, that is free and provided by the platform itself or for those looking for more advanced features, Influencer hero provides a comprehensive database and filtering options.
**How do I evaluate the authenticity of a TikTok influencer's audience**
The best way to evaluate how authentic a TikTok influencer is, is through specific metrics like engagement rate, follower growth pattern and also by evaluating their profile, checking their comment section and posts.
**Are there free tools to find TikTok influencers?**
Yes, there are free tools available. TikTok's Creator Marketplace is a free platform that connects brands with TikTok creators. Additionally, some influencer marketing platforms like Creator Hero offer free tools (like fake follower analysis or engagement rate calculator).
**What factors should I consider when choosing a TikTok influencer for a campaign?**
When selecting a TikTok influencer, consider factors such as their content relevance to your brand, engagement rate, audience demographics, and previous collaboration history.
