## What Is a TikTok Business Account?
[TikTok Business Account](https://ads.tiktok.com/business/en/solutions/business-account) is a public profile designed specifically for brands and businesses to promote their products, services, and content on the platform. 
_How does it differ from a personal account?_
  * **In-depth analytics:** See who’s watching your videos, track engagement, and learn what content works best. For example, a food brand can track which recipe videos get the most views and engagement.
  * **Ad management tools:** Run and optimize ad campaigns directly on platform to boost visibility and conversions.
  * **Access to a commercial music library:** Access licensed music to add professional soundtracks to your posts without copyright concerns.
  * **Advanced targeting options:** Reach your ideal audience based on location, interests, or behavior, ensuring your ads connect with the right people.


So,_Creator Accounts_ focus on personal branding and community engagement, while _Business Accounts_ prioritize marketing products and reaching broader audiences.

Think of it as your first stop to understanding general trends in audience engagement. 


  * Next is the **Content tab** , where you go deeper into video performance; track total views, likes, and shares, but also uncover average watch times, traffic sources (l _ike whether viewers found you through the For You page_), and even audience territories. 


  * Over in the [Tiktok Friends tab](https://support.tiktok.com/en/using-tiktok/exploring-videos/friends-tab), you’ll find insights on where your followers are from, when they’re most active, and how fast your audience is growing. _Wondering when to post for maximum engagement?_ This tab has your back, so no more guessing if 5 PM is better than 8 PM.


  * Finally, for creators with LIVE access, the [TikTok LIVE](https://support.tiktok.com/en/live-gifts-wallet/tiktok-live/what-is-tiktok-live) tab shows real-time stats that matter—like total views, new followers, and even virtual gifts.
