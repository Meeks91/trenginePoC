### **#1. Instagram Insights**
We had to mention this because, at times, business and creator Instagram Accounts tend to forget that Instagram Insights is free.
With access to the Instagram business tools, users can get detailed analytics insights into their account engagement, Instagram followers demographics, and target audience.
Also, they can obtain and create video and photo posts data, find out the most active times, popular post types, and separate post views and engagements, all for free.
Though specific data disappear within 7 to 14 days, some data may be a general statistic and won’t give you detailed reporting. Henceforth you may require to use some of these other third-party Apps for Instagram.
### **#2. HootSuite**
If you have a Business or Influencer account on Instagram and are applying Instagram Marketing and Social Media Management for a while, you would have heard about[ Hootsuite](https://www.hootsuite.com/).
Hootsuite is a comprehensive app like no other and one of the best Instagram third-party applications. It’s an approved third-party app that supports Instagram and 35 different social networking platforms like Facebook, Twitter, Pinterest, LinkedIn, and more.
Hootsuite allows users to manage all their social networking platforms from one interface by optimizing their images and videos and applying filters to photo and video content.
The Marketers can also access and analyze the proper Instagram hashtag to utilize in your content to simplify their journey on Instagram.
Hootsuite is quite comprehensive as a tool, so you wouldn’t need any other platform once you choose them. However, HootSuite may be a bit pricey if you’re a new startup business, even though it does provide a free 30-day trial.
### **#3. Sprout Social**
Another well-known third-party Instagram App and Social Media Management Solution tool perfect for Instagram users and social media business platforms is[ Sprout Social](https://sproutsocial.com/).
With great features, it lets users apply content management, analyze Instagram posts, publish content through post scheduling, analyze the Instagram account’s followers and engagements, monitoring hashtags, and boost your social presence through the Sprout Social website.
Sprout Social allows businesses to monitor their social networking platforms from desktop web browsers or mobile app versions.
One of Social Sprout’s most significant advantages is being able to tag posts for categorization purposes. It’s mentionable that Sprout Social does have free trials. However, the packages are pricey, just like Hootsuite, and so expensive for the average new startup jumping on the social media marketing wagon.
### **#4. Buffer**
Another third-party Instagram app worth mentioning is[ Buffer](https://buffer.com/), mainly because it’s affordable and easy to use for the general Instagram user. Buffer is quite comparable to the other two introduced in helping you manage all your social media platforms, including Instagram.
The official approved third-party app for Instagram, Facebook, Pinterest, and LinkedIn. The application can help marketers analyze, publish, engage, and celebrate by using Buffer as their social networking tool. The app allows the user to:
  1. Utilize the social networking analytics tool to measure their performance.
  2. Plan your Instagram Marketing Campaign and schedule posts to enhance followers’ focus.
  3. Respond to followers through comment monitoring and management tools.
  4. View data analytics for your platform based on audience reach, engagements made, followers increase, and more.


This app is recommended if you’re a startup brand, e-commerce just setting up, or an influencer. Mainly because they too were a small business, and so they value their clients and their progress.
### **#5. Aischedul**
[AiSchedul](https://aischedul.com/) is one of those Instagram apps made particularly for Instagram. This tool helps you grow your Instagram by making each Instagram Post Count through:
  1. Intelligent scheduling posts tool
  2. Content Management
  3. Account Monitoring
  4. Marketing Campaign Management Tools
  5. Audit Tools,


AiSchedul’s biggest perk is that it allows the marketers to get creative by giving them a variety of features. With access to desktop-enabled editors, uploading capabilities, the ability to post multiple images, add emojis, filters, and effects.
Or even share the content with other team members in a preview without sharing the Instagram Password or accessing the existing Instagram account. The best part of AiSchedul is that you can manage multiple accounts and use the app for free forever for limited features, or use the featured tools for really priceless amounts of 7 or 15 US Dollars.
