Professor Glitch is a faceless AI & automation education channel that creates short-form content explaining how AI, automation, and systems actually work in the real world. The content breaks down AI agents, workflow automation, APIs, backend logic, and system design using voice-over, on-screen text, diagrams, and screen recordings. The audience includes founders, solopreneurs, no-code/low-code builders, developers, and tech-curious professionals who want practical, no-fluff explanations and real use cases for AI tools and automation software. Content is educational, opinionated, and designed to help viewers move from using tools to building scalable AI-powered systems.
