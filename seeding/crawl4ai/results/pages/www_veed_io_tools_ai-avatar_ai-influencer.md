FAQ
What is an AI influencer? 
An AI influencer is a character that mimics a real-life influencer on social media. They engage with followers and promote brands in a customizable, cost-effective way. Navigating ethics on transparency and representation is key to building audience trust.
How do I create an AI influencer?
Use VEED’s AI influencer generator. Select an avatar from our library or create a digital clone of yourself. Type your script and generate a lifelike avatar for your social media and marketing videos.
What should brands keep in mind when using AI influencers? 
AI influencers can engage audiences in innovative ways. But transparency is essential to build strong, lasting connections with customers.
To use AI influencers responsibly, brands should:
  * Clearly disclose the influencer's AI identity
  * Focus on entertaining, informative content versus emulating "friendship"
  * Integrate AI alongside human influencers for a balanced, authentic approach


How can brands ensure transparency when working with AI influencers?
It’s best to disclose the virtual nature of AI influencers in their bios, post captions, video descriptions, labels, or hashtags. By being transparent, brands can harness the potential of AI while maintaining a credible brand image.
