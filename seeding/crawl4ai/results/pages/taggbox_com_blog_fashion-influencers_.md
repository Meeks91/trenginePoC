Chiara Ferragni is an Italian Top fashion influencer, entrepreneur, and designer with 29.5 million followers. She gained fame through her blog “[The Blonde Salad](https://www.theblondesalad.com/en/)” and is known for her impeccable style. 
Chiara has collaborated with [major fashion brands](https://refermate.com/categories/fashion-coupons) and even launched her own successful fashion brand, becoming a prominent figure in the [fashion industry](https://taggbox.com/blog/ugc-fashion/). 
As a businesswoman and philanthropist, she uses her platform to raise awareness on important issues.
