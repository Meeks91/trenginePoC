### How do I find up-and-coming influencers?
There are two main ways you can use GRIN to find up-and-coming influencers. First is Creator Search. With this multi-channel recruitment tool, you can set a range for your creator’s ideal follower count and filter for influencers who have a respectable audience size but haven’t quite blown up. You can also set a minimum engagement rate to get an idea of how well their content resonates with their followers.Want to be extra sure the influencer you find is legit? Plug them into our [fake influencer tool](https://grin.co/influencer-marketing-tools/fake-influencer-tool/) to check for bot activity and fake followers. You can leverage our**Curated Lists service** if you want to let our expert team find and evaluate up-and-coming influencers for you. Simply tell us about your ideal partner, and we’ll deliver hand-picked lists of brand-aligned creators directly to your inbox.
### What filters are available in Creator Search?
There are two filter sets available in the Creator Search tool. The first filters by **creator performance metrics** , helping you identify creators that meet specific criteria such as follower count, minimum engagement rate, and more. The second filters by **audience information** , allowing you to hone in on creators whose followers align with your target customer’s age, gender, and other key demographics.
### Does the GRIN Web Extension work without a GRIN account?
Yes. Anyone can download and use the [GRIN Web Extension](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/grin-creator-discovery-we/ifijiokgalnlacohanefljklpbfoeipl). Once installed, you’ll gain access to instant creator metrics while natively browsing Instagram, YouTube, TikTok, and Pinterest.Those metrics include*:
  * Follower count
  * Average comments
  * Engagement rate
  * Average views
  * Average likes 
  * Average likes per comment
