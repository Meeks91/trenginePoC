# 20 TikTok Yoga Influencers to Follow
Last Updated
December 13, 2023
Originally Published
December 13, 2023
[ Written by Sari Klaczkowski Client Success Manager ](https://theinfluenceagency.com/author/sari-klaczkowski)
Breathe in… breathe out. 
Now, open your eyes. What do you see? Well, if you’re reading this article, it’s a list of 20 [TikTok yoga influencers](https://theinfluenceagency.com/services/full-service-influencer-marketing) injecting some mindfulness into our mindless scrolling—sounds like something worth checking out, doesn't it? Well, get ready to _feed_ your mind via your [TikTok feeds](https://theinfluenceagency.com/services/tiktok-marketing). Join us as we get inspired to step into our wellness era and tackle the Yoga TikTok challenge! 
## **1. Victoria Hutchins | @thedailyvictorian**
If you are interested in colouring your feed in yoga, poetry, feelings, and the occasional full moon and retrograde talk, then [@thedailyvictorian](https://www.tiktok.com/@thedailyvictorian) is your girl!
This yoga powerhouse is famous for her motivational nuggets of wisdom, which she shares while seamlessly switching from [downward facing dog](https://www.tummee.com/yoga-poses/downward-facing-dog-pose/history) to the [triangle pose](https://www.artofliving.org/yoga/yoga-poses/triangle-pose-trikonasana). We dare you to follow her and try not to smile as you binge her Yoga TikTok content!
## **2. Jessamyn Stanley | @mynameisjessamyn**
[Jessamyn Stanley](https://www.tiktok.com/@mynameisjessamyn) is a huge game changer in the world of wellness. 
Aside from encouraging her followers to live the yoga life, this TikTok yoga star likes to share snippets from her 9-5s and 5-9s. Whether it's words of affirmation or a new yoga pose that enables you to touch your toes, you are bound to find value in her videos. 
(Reminder: touch your toes at least once a day!) 
## **3. Kino MacGregor | @kino_yoga**
[@kino_yoga](https://www.tiktok.com/@kino_yoga) has a knack for creating TikTok yoga content that's simple and easy to follow. So, if you're a yoga newbie, you know where to find her follow button!
The best part is that you can find her meditating anywhere from the comfort of her home to the studio to a scenic seaside, and even majestic hills of India. Kino is proving to us one TikTok at a time that any place can be a yoga haven. 
## **4. Cathy Madeo | (@cathymadeoyoga)**
For effective tutorials on yoga poses and handstands, [Cathy Madeo](https://www.tiktok.com/@cathymadeoyoga?_t=8cFeAk0QBex&_r=1) has got you covered. 
Always clad in stylish activewear, this TikTok yoga buff is out to conquer fitness _and_ TikTok. She might even throw some humor into the mix by striking a yoga pose with her shopping cart at the supermarket. To prove that working out is the perfect bonding activity, her videos also feature her friends taking part in the yoga experience.
## **5. Brett Larkin | (@upliftedyoga)**
Yoga was made to lift you up, and you'll probably find that your energy is definitely looking up when you follow [Brett Larkin on TikTok](https://www.tiktok.com/@upliftedyoga?_t=8cFcy0GCIwJ&_r=1)!
When she isn't busy giving simple yet powerful yoga tips, you can find her discussing the roots of yoga and the undeniable health benefits of transforming chaos into calmness. She’s certainly adding some knowledge into the yoga TikTok challenge!
## **6. Faith Hunter | (@spirituallyfly)**
[Faith Hunter](https://www.tiktok.com/@spirituallyfly?_t=8cFcmFu4laK&_r=1) is on the hunt for self care, healing, and wellness. 
Aside from her mean lateral stretch, this ray of sunshine loves to talk about full moon affirmations and unlock secrets to manifestation. Tap the follow button and just trust the yoga TikTok process—you won’t be disappointed. 
##  **7. Anna Wilson |**[(@annaxoxowilson)](https://www.tiktok.com/@spirituallyfly?_t=8cFcmFu4laK&_r=1)
Yoga can mean a variety of things to different people, but first and foremost it’s about caring for your overall well being and existence. 
Your body works hard 24/7. Even in our sleep, your body makes sure that your system runs smoothly. Give it some well-deserved TLC by following [Anna Wilson](https://www.tiktok.com/@annaxoxowilson)’s TikTok yoga content. It seems yoga can also be spelled s-e-l-f-c-a-r-e. 
##  **8. Koya Webb |**[**@koyawebb**](https://www.tiktok.com/@koyawebb?_t=8cFcRiEnb3Y&_r=1)
Is it just us or are millennials and gen z people just collectively battling lower back pain? We see you nodding in agreement. 
Well, the good news is that Koya Webb is harnessing the power of yoga to help us relieve lower back pain and get in touch with our inner peace. If that isn't one of the main purposes of yoga, then we don't know what is. 
## **9. Dylan Werner | @dylanwerneryoga**
If regular yoga isn't your thing, how about yoga underwater? Yes, some people are _really_ taking the Yoga TikTok challenge to another level! 
You’ll find this and more on [Dylan Werner](https://www.tiktok.com/@dylanwerneryoga?_t=8cFcE6fUaL2&_r=1)'s yoga TikTok page. This fitness master can perform yoga poses nearly anywhere. Whether it's next to the pool or in the pool, he’s determined to prove yoga is _always_ a good idea. 
## **10. Payton Hagen | @paytonhagenholistics**
When you follow [Payton Hagen](https://www.tiktok.com/@paytonhagenholistics), you are signing up for tips on personal development, diet, lifestyle, philosophy, and of course, yoga. His holistic approach to these life-changing elements will be a welcome addition to your Sunday morning TikTok adventures.
## **11. Allee and Hendry | @ahthespace**
[Allee and Hendry](https://www.tiktok.com/@ahthespace) are the duo we never knew we needed. Their TikTok yoga page is a wellness paradise bedazzled in all things vegan and yoga. Also, if you're looking for a post-yoga snack, they have a ceviche recipe that you will love on a spiritual level. 
## **12. Juliana | (@julianabruno28)**
[Juliana](https://www.tiktok.com/@julianabruno28)'s TikTok bio says it all: Yoga inspiration. Namaste.
Well, if you’re looking to learn how to do a variety of poses—whether it’s inside or outside—then this is a page you need to follow. Inspiration indeed. 
## **13. Amalia May | (@maymoves)**
The yoga lifestyle takes a turn for the modern with [Amalia May](https://www.tiktok.com/@maymoves). This girl is always on the go in the direction of Mother Nature’s finest sights and sounds, bringing her bag of yoga tricks (and backpack) with her wherever she goes.
## **14. Tiffany | (@tiffanycroww)**
[Tiffany Croww](https://www.tiktok.com/@tiffanycroww?lang=en) wants to heal your back—smash the follow button! 
This wellness queen promotes yoga for all body types, creating an inclusive environment for this profound form of exercise. Currently, there are over 711k people with achy backs following and benefitting from Tiffany's pain-alleviating TikTok yoga advice.
## **15. Keels Montana |(@keelsmontana)**
[Keels Montana](https://www.tiktok.com/@keelsmontana) is a yoga TikTok teacher and energy healer with an endearing sense of humour.
Come for the yoga inspiration, stay for the hilarious filter content. Talk about blending the best of both worlds on one profile. The yoga TikTok challenge has got everyone talking, and we’re all ears for whatever funny stuff Keels Montana has to say. 
## **16. Sasha | (@sasha.yogasha)**
[Sasha](https://www.tiktok.com/@sasha.yogasha?lang=en) is fierce and charming, and it's evident in her yoga lifestyle. 
Looking at life through her looking glass is all yoga with a dash of dance. Her content gives rise to a colourful and playful way to getting fit.
## **17. Elena Megan | (@elenamegan5)**
[Elena Megan](https://www.tiktok.com/@elenamegan5?lang=en) is redefining yoga by working out with her best friend… who also happens to be a little grey cat. Who would've guessed that yoga and pets make such a wonderful combination?Follow her to discover who among the two besties has more yoga prowess!
## **18. Yoga With Max | @yogawithmax**
Did you know that yoga can actually make you look taller?
The TikTok yoga movement isn't just ideal for releasing stress, upgrading your wellness, and alleviating backache. It's also an amazing hack for improving posture, thus making you stand taller. Practice yoga with [Max](https://www.tiktok.com/@yogawithmax?lang=en) to get straightened out! 
## **19. Alaaabasey | @Alaaabasey**
Yoga isn't just for your body, as it can also benefit your face. Yup, there is an actual yoga form designed just for your face! [@alaaabasey](https://www.tiktok.com/@alaaabasey?lang=en) is the perfect TikTok influencer to follow for a facial refresh—and for some great content, too. 
## **20. Naya Rappaport | (@nayitavp)**
[Naya Rappaport](https://www.tiktok.com/@nayitavp) is the epitome of tranquility and vibrance. Her upbeat take on yoga, positivity, and the power of dance will light up your experience. The yoga TikTok challenge couldn’t ever be complete with these good vibes!
