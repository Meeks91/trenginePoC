## **The Heavy Hitters: Fitness Influencers Who Dominate the Fitness Scene**
Now, are you looking for the ultimate inspo? Start with these big names in US health and fitness. 
### **Jen Selter**
First, Jen Selter is the OG Instagram fitness influencer. Known for her killer curves—which made news headlines in 2014. She has turned online attention into a health and fitness empire. 
But Jen is more than just a pretty face. With a background in dance, her passion for fitness is clear in every post. She uses social media to regularly share motivational and lifestyle content, including healthy recipes, outfits, travel, and more! 
She also talks about the importance of mental health, self-care, and personal growth. We love a multi-faceted queen. 
### **Kai Green**
Next, Kai Greene is a force of nature in the bodybuilding world. He found solace in the gym after growing up in foster care in Brooklyn. Eventually, his incredible physique and artistic posing style earned him the nickname “The Predator.” 
While he’s consistently placed high in major competitions like Mr. Olympia, he’s more than just muscle! Kai is a creative spirit—branching out into [acting](https://www.imdb.com/name/nm3471034/), painting, and even comic books.
He uses social media to connect with his fans, sharing workout routines, motivational messages, and glimpses into his personal life. 
### **Lauren Drain Kagan**
Next, Lauren Drain Kagan’s [story](https://www.laurendrain.com/about/) is one of incredible transformation. Once a member of the infamous Westboro Baptist Church, she broke from its toxic influence to begin a journey of self-discovery and empowerment. 
Today, Lauren is a successful fitness model and personal trainer. Her social media platforms are filled with inspiring workout routines, healthy recipes, and motivational messages. 
Not one for boxes, Lauren is also a registered nurse, former WBFF Bikini Pro model, podcaster, and [New York Times bestselling author](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B00BJ5VUW2?ref_=cm_sw_r_cp_ud_dp_0WD0KEDSJJ34Z7C38EVN)! We love to see it. 
### **Simeon Panda**
And, this list wouldn’t be complete with Simeon Panda. While technically from the UK, Simeon has made Los Angeles his home base, so we’ve included him here. 
While his shredded physique is undeniable, Simeon’s character and high standards representing the fitness industry make him truly influential. He takes a highly positive and motivational approach to everything he does. 
[Forbes](https://www.forbes.com/profile/simeon-panda/?list=top-influencers-fitness) named him as one of the world’s top ten fitness influencers. Plus, he’s appeared in major fitness publications like MuscleMag, Train Mag, Muscle & Performance to name only a few!

## **TikTok vs. Instagram: Choosing the Right Platform to Shine**
Frist, as a fitness pro looking to [build a personal brand](https://www.trainerize.com/blog/personal-training-business/), deciding which social platforms to focus on is crucial. TikTok and Instagram are currently two of the biggest players, but they cater to different styles of content.
TikTok’s short, snappy video format lends itself well to **workout clips, exercise tips, or entertaining**[**fitness challenges**](https://www.trainerize.com/blog/the-ultimate-guide-to-running-a-fitness-challenge/). The algorithm favors creative, trendy content that gives viewers a laugh or teaches them something new in an engaging way. 
Consistency is key on TikTok—posting frequently (even daily) helps build momentum.
**_Check Out_**[ _The Ultimate Guide to TikTok for Fitness Professionals_](https://www.trainerize.com/blog/the-ultimate-guide-to-tiktok-for-fitness-professionals/)
Instagram still reigns supreme for quality photos and longer videos that allow you to go more in-depth. It’s a great platform for sharing **complete workout routines,**[**healthy recipes**](https://www.trainerize.com/blog/trainerize-update-nutrition-new-recipes/)**, behind-the-scenes looks at your training sessions, and inspiring personal stories/transformations**. 
Building a cohesive aesthetic through edited photos/videos is also important on IG.

## Instagram vs TikTok Pros and Cons
Both platforms allow you to showcase your expertise while adding a personal touch.
**When choosing a platform, consider your strengths** —do you vibe more with IG’s refined, curated vibe or TikTok’s rawer, trendier energy? Ideally, having an active presence on both can maximize your reach. But if you’re just starting out, pick one platform to focus your efforts initially. Staying consistent with quality content is more important than spreading yourself thin.
