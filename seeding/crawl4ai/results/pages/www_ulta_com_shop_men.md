## **Men’s personal grooming products**
Now more than ever, men are joining in on the personal care movement. Men’s grooming products continue to expand and work to address skin and hair care concerns, but there are some key essentials that should always be in a personal care collection. Keep reading to learn more about must-have men’s grooming essentials and beauty care products.
### **Men’s skin and body care**
Proper [skin care](https://www.ulta.com/shop/men/skin-care) is essential for improving the appearance and overall health of your skin. At Ulta Beauty, we have curated a collection of men’s skin care and grooming products to address various concerns and different skin types.
### **Men’s hair and shave care**
Keep your hair looking and feeling its best with a healthy [hair care](https://www.ulta.com/shop/men/hair) routine. Whether you’re going for a slicked back ‘do or looking to grow a fuller head of hair, consider these hair care essentials:
  * **Nourishing shampoo and conditioner,** to hydrate and strengthen your hair.
  * **Pomade and styling gel,** for maximum hold and a natural finish.
  * **Scalp treatments,** to relieve dandruff, prevent hair thinning and to promote overall scalp health.


Don’t forget about facial hair! Just like the hair on the top of your head, it’s important to keep your facial hair looking and feeling its best with a few must-haves:
  * **Beard oil,** to prevent dry, itchy skin and ensure your beard is conditioned and healthy.
  * **Beard wash,** to cleanse, condition, remove build up and soften facial hair.
  * **Shaving conditioner,** to prep the hair and ensure a smooth, pain-free shave.


But [shaving](https://www.ulta.com/shop/men/shaving) doesn’t stop there – Ulta Beauty offers a variety of beard and hair trimmers, razors, after shave lotions, grooming kits and more to ensure the closest and cleanest shave possible.
### **Men’s cologne**
Ulta Beauty offers a wide range of [men’s fragrances and colognes](https://www.ulta.com/shop/men/cologne) to help you find your signature scent. Fragrance is personal and smells different for everyone, so it’s important to find a scent that works for you. If you are looking for your next cologne, check out some of our favorites in men’s fragrance:
  * **[Dior Sauvage Eau de Toilette](https://www.ulta.com/p/sauvage-eau-de-toilette-pimprod2022740):** A fresh and woody scent with notes of bergamot, pepper and amber wood.
  * **[ARMANI Acqua di Giò Eau de Toilette](https://www.ulta.com/p/acqua-di-gio-eau-de-toilette-fs356346):** A refreshing and clean scent with marine notes, bergamot and cedarwood.
  * **[BLEU DE CHANEL Eau de Parfum Spray](https://www.ulta.com/p/bleu-de-chanel-eau-de-parfum-spray-pimprod2015836):** A fresh citrus scent followed by ambery Cedar notes.
  * **[TOM FORD Ombre Leather Eau de Parum](https://www.ulta.com/p/ombre-leather-eau-de-parfum-pimprod2007694):** A warm and spicy scent with notes of black leather, violet and jasmine flower.


**Application tip:** To better project the scent of your cologne, spray the fragrance on your pulse points, as they’re naturally warmer areas of the body.
