AI educators (like Sanidhya Tulsinandan) break down complex concepts for learners, while content creators often showcase tools and applications. Both play a role, but educators focus on clarity and depth, while creators drive awareness and experimentation. 
### How do I choose which AI influencers to follow in 2025?
