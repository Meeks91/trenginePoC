# Top 14 Toronto Vegan Influencers to Follow on Instagram
Last Updated

_Toronto vegan influencers come in all forms, from vegan educators to professional chefs to hyper-talented food photographers. We’ve picked the top 14 from the great T.O. who prove that living the vegan lifestyle is tasty and fun._
The “6ix” is currently home to one of the most vibrant, diverse, and visually stunning vegan communities on the planet. We’re seeing vegan creators combat inflation with sophisticated budget hacks and chefs elevate the food scene to Michelin-star standards. 
We’ve curated a list of **14 Toronto vegan influencers** who pack a punch with plant-based recipes, content, and lifestyles.
##  **1. Candice Hutchings |**[**@edgyveg**](https://www.instagram.com/edgyveg/)
Candice has made waves across social media with her [bold, energetic vegan content](https://theinfluenceagency.com/services/content-marketing-agency-toronto/). She’s authored two cookbooks and built a powerhouse brand by aggressively “veganizing” comfort food with a side of sharp wit. She’s got a rebellious, high-energy personality that cuts through the saturated wellness noise!
##  **2. Jessny Maureaye |**[**@makeitbrunch**](https://www.instagram.com/makeitbrunch)
If you’re skeptical about getting the right nutrition as a vegan, look no further. Jessny is a dietician who specializes in high-protein vegan and veg recipes! Her food is healthy, vibrant, and colorful without sacrificing flavor. Her guidance is proof you really can have it all as a vegan. Give her a follow to take your brunches to the next level!
##  **3. Serena Yau |**[**@friendlyveg_**](https://www.instagram.com/friendlyveg_/?hl=en)
Serena’s been a vegan for over 13 years, and she’s an expert at putting together quick and easy plant-based meals. Her style involves minimal ingredients and minimal prep, but maximum flavor. Follow her for her famous rice-cooker recipes!
##  **4. John and Vanessa |**[**@itsjohnandvan**](https://www.instagram.com/itsjohnandvan/)
What makes John and Van special is the “we’re in this together” energy they bring to the Toronto vegan scene. Their content focuses on the practical aspects of living the vegan lifestyle as a couple, from weeknight dinners to grocery hauls. You can also find a ton of vegan hotspots on their profile, both in Toronto and abroad!
##  **5. Lisa Le |**[**@thevietvegan**](https://www.instagram.com/thevietvegan/)
Lisa expertly blends her Vietnamese heritage with a no-gatekeeping approach to veganism. Her Instagram page has a host of relatable content, from **parenting to cooking to travel** , making her feed refreshingly honest and trustworthy. She’s the definitive voice for anyone seeking bold flavor without the polished pretense.
##  **6. Sam Turnbull |**[**@itdoesnttastelikechicken**](https://www.instagram.com/itdoesnttastelikechicken/)
If you’ve ever searched for a vegan version of a childhood staple, you’ve likely landed on Sam’s blog. Her “fuss-free” philosophy has made her a household name in the vegan community. 
This vegan chef has made it her mission to **make plant-based cooking easy and accessible**. 
Today, she’s a three-time best-selling cookbook author who uses her [Instagram account to promote her brand](https://theinfluenceagency.com/services/instagram-advertising-toronto/). Her recipes, approved by carnivores and vegans alike, prove that you don’t need fancy ingredients to make a world-class meal.
##  **7. Jaclyn Forbes |**[**@jaclynforbes**](https://www.instagram.com/jaclynforbes)
For something a little more well-rounded, you can’t go wrong with Jaclyn Forbes’s content. She’s not just a **Toronto vegan influencer and a lifestyle content creator**. Her Instagram profile is filled with makeup and hairstyling tips, highlights of her media career, and travel content, too!
##  **8. Lauren Toyota |**[**@laurentoyota**](https://www.instagram.com/laurentoyota/?hl=en)
This **passionate vegan chef** finally moved back to Toronto after living in Los Angeles for five years. After a decade as a national TV host, she launched _Hot for Food_ , an empire of plant-based comfort classics. 
She’s a one-woman powerhouse that hosts supper clubs and private chef events, serving up delicious vegan dishes in person and online!
##  **9. Wil Yeung |**[**@yeungmancooking**](https://www.instagram.com/yeungmancooking/?hl=en)
While not entirely vegan, Wil primarily eats a whole-foods, plant-based diet, and his recipes are _divine_. His vibrant, Asian-inspired vegan recipes set him apart while keeping his roots in the Toronto vegan scene. Not to mention, his experience as a food photographer makes his content a visual feast too (pun absolutely intended)!
##  **10. Katherine and Devin |**[**@twomarketgirls**](https://www.instagram.com/twomarketgirls/)
With Katherine behind the recipes and Devin behind the camera, these two are the ultimate dream team. Their content across their website and their socials is chock-full of personality and the **most drool-worthy plant-based comfort food**. Give them a follow for top-notch eats (and photos)!
##  **11. Laura Wright |**[**@thefirstmess**](https://www.instagram.com/thefirstmess)
Laura is the definitive voice for **“slow living” in the plant-based world**. Her recipes are sophisticated yet grounded, with a special focus on seasonal ingredients and pantry staples. She’s got the rare ability to make a simple bowl of soup look like a delicious work of art.
##  **12. Lauren McNeill |**[**@tastingtothrive_rd**](https://www.instagram.com/tastingtothrive_rd)
Switching to a vegan diet can sound pretty daunting, especially in an era of wellness “hacks”. Luckily, Lauren is here to provide a refreshing voice of reason. 
She’s a **Registered Dietitian** who brings all the clinical expertise and none of the fluff, without losing the lifestyle appeal. She’s the go-to person to follow if you want to feel good and back it up with facts.
##  **13. Fraser Fitzgerald |**[**@frasercooks**](https://www.instagram.com/frasercooks/?hl=en)
Fraser is a plant-based chef (and new dad!) living and cooking in Hamilton, Ontario. After years in the trenches of Toronto’s culinary scene, he’s shifted his focus online. If you’re looking for **wholesome, family-focused meals** that don’t skimp on flavor, Fraser’s your guy.
##  **14. Veggie in the 6ix |**[**@veggieinthe6ix**](https://www.instagram.com/veggieinthe6ix/?hl=en)
This account is the go-to for what’s happening in Toronto’s vegan food scene, from restaurants to products to events. If you’re looking for the best vegan hidden gems in the city, this is _the_ account to follow.

## **FAQs**
### Is it easy to be vegan in Toronto?
−
**Yes, it is! Vegan food is accessible in Toronto,** and if you’re not at a dedicated vegan restaurant, most places will have vegan or veggie-friendly options.
### What’s the most vegan city in Canada?
+
**Research suggests that the title belongs to Victoria, BC!** If you’re a vegan traveling in Canada, you’ll have the most luck in major metropolises like Toronto, Ottawa, or Vancouver.
### Is veganism actually healthy?
+
**It can be, but it all depends on what you eat.** Sure, broccoli and tofu are vegan, but so are Oreos. Ultimately, do what works best for your own health goals!
