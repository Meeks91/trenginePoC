Today, lifestyle influencers dictate the trends and give inspiration to millions of people throughout the world. Thus, walking into a brand new year of 2025 there are talents that will be highlighted for the distinguished content and genuine interaction. Take a look at the top 15 lifestyle influencers you should follow this year!
## **1. Julie Sariñana**
Julie Sariñana, an Instagram celebrity who is better known by the username [@sincerelyjules](https://www.instagram.com/sincerelyjules/?hl=en), has more than 6 million fans. She started the blog in 2009 and focused it on the fashion and style perspective. Being relatable and real has made Julie one of the most popular lifestyle influencers out there. 
## **2. Lauren Conrad**
Greetings to one of the most famous television personalities, a fashion designer, and an author with a great number of fans on Instagram: over 5 million. [@Lauren Conrad](https://www.instagram.com/laurenconrad/?hl=en) emerged into fame from the television show Laguna Beach and has worked to build a lifestyle company mainly targeted at fashion and home.
## **3. Caitlin Covington**
With more than 1 million followers on Instagram, Caitlin Covington goes by [@cmcoving](https://www.instagram.com/cmcoving/?hl=en). Therefore, she owns the fashion, beauty, and travel blog titled Southern Curls & Pearls. This is evident since; Caitlin follows a classic style and her posts about everyday life are easy to relate to.
## **4. Aimee Song**
Today Aimee Song, who has the username [@aimeesong](https://www.instagram.com/aimeesong/?hl=en), has 7.3 mln followers in Instagram. She is a fashion blogger and designer based in California and running her company called Song of Style. Aimee creates fashion, interior design, and travel-related posts to encourage more people to follow trends around the world.
## **5. Huda Kattan**
The beauty guru and cosmetic line founder [Huda Kattan](https://www.instagram.com/huda/?hl=en) has more than 4 million followers on Instagram. Her cosmetics business was initiated her through the establishment of “Huda Beauty” which is very successful internationally. Properly, Huda offers her viewers beauty tips, her experience and impressions of different beauty products, as well as lifestyle videos.
## **6. Adam Gallagher**
Adam Gallagher, known as [@iamgalla](https://www.instagram.com/iamgalla/?hl=en) on Instagram, has over 2 million followers. He shares men's fashion, travel, and lifestyle content. Adam's sophisticated style and global adventures have made him a top male lifestyle influencer.
## **7. Anna Nystrom**
[Anna Nystrom](https://www.instagram.com/annanystrom/?hl=en) is a Swede fitness model, and lifestyle social media star, whose Instagram account is followed by more than 7 million people. She posts fitness exercises, healthy tips, and fashion stuff hoping to encourage her followers to embrace a healthy lifestyle.
## **8. Amanda Stanton**
Internet personality [Amanda Stanton](https://www.instagram.com/amanda_stantonn/?hl=en), who starred in ‘The Bachelor’ fame has now over one million followers on Instagram. This woman posts fashion, beauty, and motherhood content but delivers the content in real and relatable posts.
## **9. Mariam Ezzeddine**
Mariam Ezzeddine, under the handle [@cookinwithmima](https://www.instagram.com/cookinwithmima/?hl=en), has over 2 million Instagram followers. She shares culinary content, including recipes and cooking tips, inspiring her audience to explore new dishes.
## **10. Brittany Xavier**
Brittany Xavier, who goes by the Instagram username [@brittanyxavier](https://www.instagram.com/brittanyxavier/?hl=en), currently boasts a following of more than 1.5m.
