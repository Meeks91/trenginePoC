## What are the differences between workflow automation and AI?
​It is common to get these two confused. Let’s define them clearly.
​Workflow Automation: This is "If-This-Then-That" logic. It is a rigid sequence of events. For example, if a user fills out a form, then send them a welcome email. It doesn't learn or change.
​Artificial Intelligence (AI): AI involves a machine simulating human intelligence to solve a problem. It can interpret nuance. For example, an AI can read a customer email, realise the customer is asking for a refund (even if they didn't use the word "refund"), and suggest the best response based on company policy.
​In 2026, an AI workflow automation tool combines both. It uses the structure of automation with the "brain" of AI to handle unpredictable data.
