A few months ago I wrote about [SUPER quick recipes ](https://thewellplannedkitchen.com/quickhealthydinnerideas/)– you know, the kind that can keep you from leaving the house for takeout. Those were about 20 minutes from start-to-finish. I've been using those recipes, and they've been SO helpful for crazy busy days, but I realized that I need a list of go-to recipes that I can prepare quickly, with just a few minutes of hands-on time, so I can get to other things before dinner. This collection of healthy, quick recipes has already been heavily utilized by my family over the past few months, and we are loving every recipe on this list!
For example, if my living room looks like Hello Kitty and Star Wars had a massive battle with many stuffed casualties, and I need to clean it before dinner, I can spend just a few minutes of prep and pop one of the recipes below in the oven and get to work on the rest of the house. Or on days that we have to run out for music lessons, I can spend just 5 minutes or so in the kitchen getting a crockpot recipe going, and run out the door. OK, so maybe I don't run out the door, but I walk fast. I don't think I've ever run out the door for real – except maybe the one time I overheard one of the kids telling a neighbor friend that mommy likes to drink moonshine (it was Trim Healthy Mama's MOCK moonshine, not real alcohol!). I had to clarify that ASAP.
