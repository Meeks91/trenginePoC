## What Makes a Top Social Media Agency?
Before we jump into the list, it's key to know why we've chosen these agencies. These are the criteria we’ve followed to put this list together, and what you should always look for when searching for an agency to work with:
### Proven Track Record
The selected agency should have a portfolio of results and case studies that demonstrate they’ve achieved the result you’re currently looking for, and for similar types of brands and industries to yours.
### Social Specialism
The agency should offer customized strategies tailored to each client's specific needs, rather than a one-size-fits-all approach.
Expertise across multiple social media platforms is crucial, as is the ability to create engaging, platform-specific content.
### Data-Driven
All good agencies back up their social, creative, influencer and paid strategy with data.
Not only are they able to demonstrate the ROI they're driving, but they showcase the logic behind why they’ve made particular decisions when tweaking the strategic approach to the scope of work they’re delivering for you.
### Industry Leaders
Agencies that have won multiple awards show signs that they’ve been recognised within the industry and that they produce quality work, which is recognised by industry people.
They should also be at the forefront of industry trends and social media platform changes, ensuring they and their clients stay relevant in the ever-changing market.

### 2. Scott Social
[Scott Social](https://www.scottsocialmarketing.com/) is a Phoenix-based boutique social media management agency that works with small businesses.
Their 5-person team specializes in social strategy and content creation, and their key services include social media management and influencer marketing.
They've earned recognition from the Phoenix Business Journal and work with clients like Elizabeth Clarke and Provision Coffee.
### 3. Homemade Social
[Homemade Social](https://www.homemadesocial.com/) is located in downtown Phoenix and positions itself as a social media marketing agency focused on creating authentic, community-driven content. The agency stands out for its storytelling approach, with a strong focus on food, beverage, and lifestyle brands.
Their team consists of around 12 people who specialize in content creation, particularly custom photography that makes their clients' social feeds stand out. They work with notable brands such as Tate’s Bake Shop, Cerup and Jazmine Beauty.
Their key services appear to include:
  * Social media management
  * Content creation and photography
  * Community engagement
  * Social strategy development
  * Organic growth campaigns


### 4. Zion & Zion
[Zion & Zion](https://www.zionandzion.com/pr-social-media/social-media/) runs their operations from Tempe, with a talented team of 50+ handling all things Social Media and PR.
They've won multiple ADDY awards and work with major brands like Arizona State University and Goodwill.
They are predominantly a comms agency but do have social media as a part of their offering, with social services including community management, content creation, and influencer marketing.
### 5. Agital
[Agital](https://agital.com/services/social-media-marketing/) is an eCommerce marketing agency, that has services dedicated to Social Media.
They have a team size of 150+ with offices around the US, one of which is in Phoenix.
Their social media services cover both Organic and Paid Social, driving significant growth for their clients such as Willow Boutique and Calini Coastal across the major social platforms.
### 6. Widsix
[Widsix](https://widsix.com/) is a digital agency that offers social media services, specifically Social Media Management.
Their headquarters is located in Scottsdale, and have particular expertise within businesses in the health and sports industry.
Their agency model focuses on remaining lean, to ensure their fees don’t bloat for their clients.
### 7. Overlap Interactive
[Overlap Interactive](https://www.overlapinteractive.com/services/phoenix-social-media-agency) is a full-service marketing agency that offers social media services to help brands with:
  * Research and strategy
  * Content creation
  * Community management
  * Paid Social
  * Reporting and analysis


Their team of 9 is agile and ready to take on your needs across the full digital spectrum.
### 8. V Digital Services
[V Digital Services](https://www.vdigitalservices.com/) is not a Social Media agency but a full-service digital agency. However, they do offer Paid Social services helping brands grow using Facebook and Instagram advertising.
They’re a larger agency compared to the rest of this list, and have a team size of 125+ in Phoenix Arizona.
### 9. Colling Media
[Colling Media](https://collingmedia.com/) knows Social Media and is a Meta Business Partner. Based out of Phoenix, they help brands grow on Organic and Paid Social.
They recently worked with the Musicians Institute and drove 9,000 leads using a combination of Meta and TikTok ads.
### 10. AdMix Social
[AdMix Social](https://admixsocial.com/) is a small Social Media Agency with a combined 35 years of experience in the advertising industry.
They not only help with social media services but look across your entire online marketing mix and support you in your next steps of growth.
### 11. KEO Marketing
[KEO Marketing](https://keomarketing.com/social-media-marketing/social-media-management/) is a B2B marketing specialist that offers Social Media Management as a service.
Their headquarters are in Phoenix, and they also have offices in Commerce City, Colorado.
KEO Marketing uses its award-winning skills to ensure your campaigns and messaging are visible across all social media platforms, groups and company pages.
### 12. Creatitive
[Creatitive](https://creatitive.com/phoenix-digital-marketing-agency/social-media/) is a leading social media marketing agency in the sports and athlete industries, as well as small businesses.
They offer social media management, paid social advertising, remarketing campaigns and page promotion.
Even though they are a team size of 2, they are still able to pack a punch in the results they deliver.
### 13. Blare Media
[Blare Media](https://blaremedia.net/services/social-media-marketing/) is a video production company that specializes in creating visually stunning social media video ads to build brand awareness and drive conversions on Facebook, Instagram and LinkedIn.
Whilst they create social media videos, it is definitely better suited to branded videos rather than social-first mobile-produced videos.
