## Should You Partner with a Renowned AI Influencer or Create Your Own?
For brands contemplating investment in virtual influencers, the choice between ownership and rental is a critical factor to consider.
**The decision revolves around control and reach in your marketing strategy.**
Renting a famous virtual influencer, like collaborating with Lil Miquela, grants instant access to massive audiences. **This “rented” approach suits quick campaigns, accelerating business growth through established credibility. This path fills business needs for authenticity but demands upfront investment in development and maintenance. For growth-focused companies, owned options shine in niche markets, while renting excels for broad exposure.
