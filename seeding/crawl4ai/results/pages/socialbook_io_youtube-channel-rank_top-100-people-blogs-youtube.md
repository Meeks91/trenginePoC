Q&A
How does SocialBook gain influencer data?
SocialBook builds its global influencer database through 3 primary methods:
  1. Data authorized by influencers
  2. Data from third-party partners affiliated with SocialBook
  3. Data and metrics generated by SocialBook's machine learning model


Please note that some predicted metrics may have a deviation of 10% to 20% from actual figures.
Do all influencer users sign with SocialBook?
Not all influencer users sign with SocialBook. SocialBook operates as an analytics tool and influencer marketing management software for both brands and influencers. However, we do have influencer users who sign up for accounts and claim their channel profiles.
How often does SocialBook update data?
SocialBook updates data on a daily basis and also allows users to manually update their data on their profile page.
What should I do if I can't locate certain influencers in the search results?
SocialBook’s database is regularly updated with public information from social media influencers worldwide. If you’re unable to find a specific influencer, you can submit a request to add their profile manually. For further assistance, please contact your SocialBook Sales representative.
Is a free trial available on SocialBook?
SocialBook offers a free trial to all users who sign up.
