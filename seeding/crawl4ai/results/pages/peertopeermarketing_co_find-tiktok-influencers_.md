## 7 Ways to Find TikTok Influencers
###  1.Use Fiverr to Find TikTok Influencers
[Fiverr](https://go.fiverr.com/visit/?bta=133372&brand=fiverrhybrid) is an online marketplace where you can find and sell freelance services. There are loads of different professionals on Fiverr with varying experience levels and offerings. Amid all these freelancers, your business can source TikTok content creators.
Source: [Fiverr](https://www.fiverr.com/)
Before you do this, create a Fiverr account. It’s simple to register on Fiverr, and all you need is your email address, Gmail, Facebook, or Apple account with a chosen password.
After registering an account, businesses must complete some personal information to give sellers an idea of who they are. The more details you provide, the more sellers can trust doing business with your brand.
Businesses can also verify their Fiverr information for credibility. As you trade on this platform and build a track record, sellers can check your reviews and rating. So, keep ethical and fair professional behavior on this platform to avoid scaring off talent.
You can find TikTok influencers on Fiverr in two ways:
  1. Create a Gig
  2. Manually Search for Influencers


Let me explain how each method works:
#### Creating a Fiverr Gig to Find TikTok Influencers
Creating a Fiverr gig is like putting out a job ad for talent. You can do this through these steps: 
  * Go to “My Profile” and click “Create a New Gig”.
  * Add a Gig title, description, service type, and search tags. You can also add Gig metadata to optimize your results.
  * Include your desired delivery time and ideal budget. 
  * Add an FAQ section if necessary.


When TikTok influencers are looking for jobs or gigs through Fiverr’s search engine, they can find your listing and apply. You can shortlist candidates and eventually hire the best influencers. 
#### Manually Searching for TikTok Influencers on Fiverr
In addition to creating a Fiverr Gig, you can manually look for TikTok influencers on Fiverr. Many of these influencers have created ads selling their services. You may even find someone offering a package of services to give your brand everything it needs.
For this method, ensure your profile is switched to “Buyer”. And now, use Fiverr’s search engine to find influencers by simply searching “TikTok influencers”, or a similar keyword.
You can browse through the search results until you find the best-suited candidates for your [TikTok marketing](https://peertopeermarketing.co/tiktok-marketing/) campaign. Reach out to these influencers by buying their package or service or inviting them to a project.

###  3.Use Google and TikTok to Search Organically
You can search for TikTok influencers through a TikTok or Google search.
#### Using TikTok Search to Find TikTok Influencers
You can find the best TikTok influencers via TikTok search engine. Here’s how to do it:
  1. Go to TikTok
  2. Search “influencers (insert your niche)”
  3. And bam! You’ve found a whole bunch of TikTok influencers in your niche!


But you’re not limited to only TikTok’s search engine. You can use Google too!
#### Using Google Search to Find TikTok Influencers
To source the top influencers through Google search, you must:
  1. Go to Google.
  2. Search the top “TikTok influencers for (insert your niche)”
  3. And there you go! You can browse through several listicles covering the top influencers.

Source: [Google Search](https://www.google.com/)
Whether you use organic search on TikTok, Google, or both to find influencers for your TikTok campaign, determine whether you want [macro or micro influencers](https://peertopeermarketing.co/micro-vs-macro-influencers/) for your business. 
It’s best to create a list of all the factors you’re looking for in a TikTok influencer and evaluate the influencers you find on Google and TikTok accordingly. Once choosing your influencers, get familiar with their content and the influencer campaigns they’ve impacted. 
Take some time to do this because the more you know, the better you can personalize your pitch to collaborate.
When writing your pitch, highlight the values and mission of your brand, and why you chose this influencer. 
If you can’t afford some of the most popular influencers, continue browsing until you find influencers with lower engagement rates and subscribers. And consider which businesses they work with. You’ll have to pay more for influencers working with global brands like Lego or Starbucks. 
###  4.Use Hashtags
TikTok, once again, makes it easier to find TikTok influencers on their platform through hashtags. Through hashtags, you can filter TikTok influencers based on your target audience. TikTok influencers use hashtags for their posts so users can find their videos. Fortunately, this makes it easy for businesses to find TikTok influencers.
Go to TikTok and type in a hashtag relevant to your brand. For example, let’s say you’re in the finance niche, search, “#financeinfluencers”, #influencersforfinance”, or simply, “#finance”.
Source: [Later](https://later.com/blog/tiktok-hashtags/)
Ensure you evaluate and find influencers the same way as in strategy 3. Take the same steps to personalize your approach before connecting with influencers.
If you can’t find enough information about the influencer based on a TikTok account alone, ask to see some of their previous work. Credible, high-quality influencers worth investing in will have a portfolio of TikTok marketing campaigns they’ve contributed to.
What’s fantastic about this strategy is that you’re more likely to find TikTok influencers engaged with your target buyers. If a TikTok influencer is using specific hashtags it’s most likely because users enjoy the content related to the hashtags they use.
In other words, rather than finding random TikTok influencers, this method allows you to work with influencers who have an in-depth understanding of your target buyers.
###  5.Finding TikTok Influencers By Country, City and Following
You may need TikTok influencers in a specific country or city. For example, if you’re promoting products from your in-store business, it would be better for your TikTok influencers to promote your brand from its location. Luckily, it’s simple to find TikTok influencers in countries or cities. 
You can use any of the tools we’ll discuss in the next strategy to filter your TikTok influencer search by city or country. However, this is also a strategic move if you don’t need in-person TikTok marketing. If you know the majority of your audience are in certain countries or cities, it’s best to work with influencers who understand those buyers.
By the same token, perhaps you want your ideal TikTok influencer to have a certain amount of followers. Remember, I mentioned that you need to know whether you want a macro or micro influencer? Once you’ve pinned that down, you can use TikTok to look for TikTok influencers based on how many followers they have. 
Here’s how to use TikTok to find TikTok influencers based on their following:
  1. Sign in to your TikTok account.
  2. Click the search button in the upper-right corner.
  3. Type in your niche.
  4. Click the “users” filter.
  5. Click the filter button in the upper-right corner.
  6. Click on the number of followers you want your ideal TikTok influencers to have.


While looking for TikTok influencers based on their followings, you can use more filters for accurate targeting too. Brands can choose the type of TikTok profiles they’re looking for and search for a preferred username.
