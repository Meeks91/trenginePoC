#### Frequently Asked Questions 
How long does it take to implement automate solutions? 
The implementation of automation solutions depend on a project’s complexity. For a simple workflow it can take 2 to 4 weeks, AI chatbot 4 to 6 weeks, complex automation of 8 to 12 weeks, and enterprise wide automation strategy for 3 to 6 months. 
What process are best suited for automation? 
Repetitive, time consuming tasks, error prone manual tasks, data-intensive operations, and customer facing interactions are the best suited for automation. 
How do AI agents differ from traditional chatbots? 
AI agents differer from traditional chatbots because they have contextual awareness, understands natural language, performs complex tasks, and improve through continuous learning. 
How do you ensure security and compliance with the automation? 
We implement secure authentications, role based access controls, end-to-end data encryption, detailed audit logs, and activity tracking to ensure security and compliance with the automations. 
Can automation integrate with our existing systems? 
Yes, our automation solutions are designed to support APIs, web services, pre-built connectors, legacy systems, RPA capabilities, database integration and more. 
How do you measure automation ROI? 
We can measure automation ROI through the hours saved on repetitive work, reduction in error, increase in work capacity, improvement in speed, and the impact on your customers experience. 
What industries benefit the most from AI agents and automation? 
AI agents deliver value across multiple sectors, especially in industries with high manual workloads or customer interaction—such as healthcare, finance, e-commerce, education, real estate, logistics, and professional services. Any business aiming to scale operations while reducing costs can benefit from automation. 
Do AI agents require ongoing maintenance? 
Yes. AI agents need periodic updates to improve accuracy, incorporate new business rules, adapt to evolving workflows, and stay aligned with your data systems. Regular monitoring ensures optimal performance and prevents issues before they impact business operations.
