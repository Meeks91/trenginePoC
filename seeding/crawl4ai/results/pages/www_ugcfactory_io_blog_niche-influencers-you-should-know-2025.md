If you're thinking about working with influencers, you might want to skip the A-list celebrities with millions of followers.
In fact,[ Hubspot reports that 86% of marketers](https://blog.hubspot.com/marketing/hubspot-blog-social-media-marketing-report?hubs_content=blog.hubspot.com%2Fmarketing%2Finfluencer-platforms&hubs_content-cta=social%20media%20trends%20survey) prefer teaming up with influencers who have **under 100K followers,** while only 17% go for the big names. And honestly, it makes sense.
Smaller influencers aren’t just more budget-friendly – they also tend to have a **very engaged, loyal audience** that actually listens to their recommendations. This is especially true when an influencer has a clear niche that aligns with your brand and products.
But here’s the tricky part: finding the right niche micro-influencer isn’t as easy as spotting a celeb. Since they don’t have millions of followers, they’re a little harder to track down.
That’s why we wrote this article!
We’re here to give you the names of the coolest niche influencers across social media, all neatly divided by their platform of choice.
But first, let’s break down what a niche influencer actually is.
## So, What Exactly Is a Niche Influencer?
A niche influencer is someone who’s built a following by consistently creating content **around a specific interest or area of expertise.** While definitions can vary, most niche influencers share a few key traits:
**They stay focused.** Unlike general lifestyle influencers who post about anything and everything, niche influencers **stick to a specific topic.** Their content rarely strays from their main passion.
  * **They know their stuff.** Niche influencers tend to have **deep knowledge about their subject,** whether through education, experience or just sheer obsession. Their audience values their insights and **trusts their recommendations.**
  * **They’re passionate.** Because they genuinely love what they do, niche influencers build strong connections with their followers – even if their audience is smaller.


That said, some niche influencers do manage to grow massive followings while staying true to their specialty. Let’s check out some standout examples!
