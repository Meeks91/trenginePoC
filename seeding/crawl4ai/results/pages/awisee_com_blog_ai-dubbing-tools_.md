## **2. Rask AI**
**Website:**[ rask.ai](https://www.rask.ai/)
Rask AI, founded by Maria Chmir in 2023, powers over 2 million creators with end-to-end automatic dubbing software. Known for its scalability and multilingual versatility, Rask is used by global agencies to translate and dub long-form videos efficiently.
### **Key Features:**
  * Upload or link videos from YouTube or Google Drive.
  * 130+ supported languages and accents.
  * Auto-detection for original language and number of speakers.
  * Voice cloning and transcription available.
  * Collaborative workspace for teams.


Its English-to-Hindi dubbing quality is outstanding, accurately maintaining tone and pacing. However, the lack of an Indian English accent gives some results a robotic edge. Despite that, it remains one of the top dubbing AI platforms for global brands.
**Ideal For:** International marketers, content producers, and enterprises needing automatic video translation across regions.

## **AI Voice Cloning vs. Traditional Dubbing Tool: What’s the Difference?**
Traditional dubbing requires recording new audio with voice actors, often making the process expensive and time-consuming. AI voice dubbing tools, on the other hand, replicate voices using machine learning, enabling accurate, emotion-rich translation within minutes.
### **Differences Between AI Voice Cloning and Traditional Dubbing:**
  * **Speed:** AI dubbing delivers instant translations versus manual studio sessions.
  * **Cost:** Traditional dubbing involves actor fees and studio rentals, while AI is subscription-based.
  * **Flexibility:** AI cloning adapts voices across accents and genders automatically.
  * **Scalability:** AI can localize hundreds of videos simultaneously without quality loss.


Thanks to AI dubbing technology, creators now have access to scalable, affordable localization that maintains quality comparable to human dubbing. It’s a future where voice reproduction is seamless, fast, and globally inclusive.
