# Top Canadian Female Fitness YouTubers To Follow
Last Updated
February 20, 2023
Originally Published
January 26, 2021
[ Written by Danielle Howson Associate Director of CRO ](https://theinfluenceagency.com/author/danielle-howson)
We're pretty sure you ended up here because you're looking for a fitness routine glow up—and you should totally give yourself a pat on the back for giving your health some well-deserved attention 💪. 
If, like me, one of your goals at the beginning of the year was to focus on your health and fitness, you may be looking for some of that extra motivation to keep you on track. That’s why I’ve compiled a short list of some of the top [**Canadian female fitness YouTubers**](https://theinfluenceagency.com/influencer-marketing/fitness) who are popular right now!
##  **1.** **Maddie Lymburner |**[**@MadFit**](https://m.youtube.com/@MadFit/about)
We’re starting off this list with my personal go-to fitness YouTuber, MadFit—AKA [Maddie Lymburner](https://www.cbc.ca/player/play/video/1.6730358). Based out of Hamilton, Ontario, Maddie started her fitness YouTube channel in March of 2018. Her goal with the channel was to create quick but effective workouts for people working out at home, which she undoubtedly achieved
‍
As at-home workouts have risen in popularity due to the pandemic, Maddie’s fitness YouTube channel reached 5 million subscribers in 2020—and currently has 7.69 million subscribers!
There’s no question that her popularity stems from her easy-to-follow workout videos. Don’t let that fool you though; Maddie makes sure you get your sweat on with types of workouts for everyone. From full-body HIIT circuits to TikTok-inspired dance workouts, you’ll fall in love with her content just like I did (while secretly hating her for making your body hurt for days afterwards).
You can also find Maddie on Instagram as [@madfit.ig](https://www.instagram.com/madfit.ig/?hl=en)!
##  **2.** **Gabby Scheyen |**[**@GabbyScheyen**](https://www.youtube.com/c/GabbyScheyen/featured)
Gabby is a Canadian female fitness YouTuber who makes videos revolving around her everyday life and her love for working out.
‍
On her channel, which has 400,000 subscribers, she posts real-time workout videos that viewers can watch and follow along to. “Gain strength, gain muscle, gain confidence” is Gabby’s motto as she advocates for physical wellness through a protein-packed diet and strength-based workout plan. If you’re not one to shy away from building muscle, Gabby’s fitness guides are made for you!
Don't forget to follow Gabby on Instagram—you can find her on the platform as [@gabbyschey](https://www.instagram.com/gabbyschey/?hl=en).
##  **3.** **Keltie O'Connor |**[**@KeltieOConnor**](https://m.youtube.com/channel/UC9zEqIwv7yPQ8IlzNYpB_Aw)
The next person on our list is Keltie O’Connor, a Toronto-based fitness YouTuber. Coming from a sports background growing up, Keltie wanted to find a career that could support not only her love of fitness but [video production](https://theinfluenceagency.com/services/videography) as well. She relocated from Edmonton to Toronto in order to pursue her YouTube channel full-time—a move that clearly paid off!
Keltie’s channel skyrocketed back in 2018, now grown to over 464K subscribers, her videos oftentimes gaining millions of views. In these videos, she tests out celebrity diets and workout routines, providing her subscribers with fitness tips and tricks along the way. Her biggest piece of advice when it comes to working out and staying healthy is to always try new things—eventually, you’ll find something that you love. It’s easier to stick to a [healthy lifestyle](https://theinfluenceagency.com/influencer-marketing/lifestyle) when you genuinely consider it enjoyable.
‍
If you want to follow this fitness YouTuber more closely, check out her Instagram [@keltieoconnor](https://www.instagram.com/keltieoconnor/)!
##  **4.** **Linda Sun |**[**@lindasunyt**](https://m.youtube.com/channel/UCpEcZL5B7i6gJYGfqLvMCbg)
Linda Sun is another feel-good, Canadian Female Fitness YouTuber encouraging her viewers to listen to their bodies and eat intuitively. Her channel promotes working out for the sake of how it makes you feel, not necessarily to achieve aesthetic results. Linda has lived all over Canada but is currently living in Ontario with her family, where she attends school at Western University.
‍
While Linda doesn’t post workouts that you can follow along with, the content she does post is all related to fitness, food, and how to maintain a healthy lifestyle. If you’re looking for a YouTube channel that will inspire you to do the same, Linda is your girl. With over 927,000 subscribers as of 2023, Linda has managed to create an uplifting community as they follow along on her health and fitness journey.
Follow Linda’s Instagram account, [@lindasunyt](https://www.instagram.com/lindasunyt/?hl=en), where she posts more about this fitness YouTuber’s life!
## 5. **Kelsey Ellis |**[**@HealthyWithKelsey**](https://www.youtube.com/@HealthyWithKelsey/videos)
You'll be off to a fantastic start when you decide to kickstart your journey to "healthy" with Kelsey by your side.
As a body-positive fitness coach, she's on a mission to inspire wellness through joyful movement and to empower people to step into their personal power.
‍
With all her workout videos being under 30 minutes, you can easily step up your fitness game without it having to take a huge chunk out of your daily schedule. And before you know it, you'll realize that a mere 30 minutes dedicated to effective workouts will make _all_ the positive difference in your life.
Follow Kelsey on Instagram at [@healthy_with_kelsey](https://www.instagram.com/healthy_with_kelsey/)!
##  **6.** **Chelsea Trevor |**[**@ChelseaTrevor**](https://www.youtube.com/@ChelseaTrevor/videos)
We all want a healthy and balanced lifestyle—but getting there? _That's_ always the hardest part.
Maybe all you need is a few tips to help you find that balance. Maybe you just need to watch a few of Chelsea Trevor's YouTube videos that do an amazing job of igniting productivity.
‍
Whether she's walking her viewers through pilates videos, sharing her travel habits, or diving into a decluttering spree, there's so much dynamic and realistic content to look forward to from this Canadian female fitness YouTuber.
Follow Chelsea on IG at [@chelseatrevor](https://www.instagram.com/chelseatrevor/)
##  **7.** **Kisha Alejandra |**[**@KishaAlejandra**](https://www.youtube.com/@KishaAlejandra/videos)
Another queen of productivity who's made it to our list of the top Canadian female fitness YouTubers is Kisha Alejandra
‍
Her channel features mostly routine videos that show her audiences how she fits gym sessions into her busy schedule throughout the week. From effective stretching exercises to weightlifting, this girl does it all!
On top of that, you'll also get a look at her recommended post-workout meals, grocery shopping trips, try-on hauls, and even cleaning routines!
Follow Kisha on Instagram at [@kishaalejandra](https://www.instagram.com/kishaalejandra/)
##  **8.** **Natasha Aughey |**[**@NatashaAughey**](https://www.youtube.com/@NatashaAughey/videos)
Natasha Aughey has been in the fitness training scene for the past ten years—so you can count on it that her expertise will propel you forward in your own fitness journey.
On her [YouTube channel,](https://theinfluenceagency.com/blog/how-to-grow-a-youtube-channel) you'll find core and back workouts, chest and shoulder training tips, hamstring and glute-focused videos, deadlift routines, and so much more.
Adding more interesting content to the mix are some hiking, camping, and skating videos that highlight her cool and calm personality.
Follow Natasha on Instagram at [@natashaughey_](https://www.instagram.com/natashaughey_/)
##  **9.** **Juliana Spicoluk |**[**@bohobeautiful**](https://www.youtube.com/@bohobeautiful/videos)
Juliana Spicoluk strongly believes in the saying that "your decisions today define your tomorrow" —and if that doesn't make you roll out your dusty yoga mat right now, we don't know what will.
‍
Through her YouTube channel, this Canadian female fitness influencer provides free yoga videos for the digital yogi community. Packed with a variety of health and fitness content, including pilates, vegan [food](https://theinfluenceagency.com/influencer-marketing/foodies) ideas, and guided meditation videos, @bohobeautiful has become a go-to space for anyone whose ultimate goal is conscious and mindful living.
Follow this female fitness YouTube on IG at [@juliana.spicoluk](https://www.instagram.com/juliana.spicoluk/)
##  **10.** **Kassandra Reinhardt |**[**@yogawithkassandra**](https://www.youtube.com/@yogawithkassandra/videos)
Get ready to dive into a rabbit hole of incredible yoga videos when you subscribe to Kassandra's channel!
‍
With 700+ classes and tutorials available, it has become a valuable resource for 2.18 million [Vinyasa](https://www.self.com/story/vinyasa-yoga) and [Yin Yoga](https://www.healthline.com/health/fitness/yin-yoga-poses) enthusiasts across the globe.
Catering to all experience levels, @yogawithkassandra will be there for you as you follow along at your own pace—as well as when you move up the yoga skill ladder!
You can follow Kassandra on Instagram at [@yoga_with_kassandra](https://www.instagram.com/yoga_with_kassandra/)
