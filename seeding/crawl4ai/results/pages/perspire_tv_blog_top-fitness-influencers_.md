**How often should I follow a fitness influencer’s workout plan?**
Most structured programs work best when followed 3-5 times per week, but this varies based on your fitness level and goals. Many influencers offer programs with built-in rest days and recovery sessions.
**Do I need special equipment to follow these influencers?** It depends on the influencer. Many on this list offer equipment-free options or suggest simple alternatives for home workouts. Check their content before committing to see if their equipment requirements match what you have available.
**How can I tell if a fitness influencer is promoting healthy habits?** Look for balanced approaches that include proper nutrition, adequate rest, and sustainable workout schedules.
