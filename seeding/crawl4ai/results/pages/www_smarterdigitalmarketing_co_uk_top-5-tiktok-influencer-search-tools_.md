## What Can TikTok Influencer Search Tools Do?
The TikTok influencer search tools will find the masters that are most relevant to your field, and to put it simply, they are the key elements of a successful [TikTok marketing](https://www.smarterdigitalmarketing.co.uk/tiktok-marketing/ "TikTok Marketing: Boost Your Brand’s Social Presence") campaign.
One thing that we know for sure is that the days of TikTok being a lip-syncing video platform for dancing are well and truly gone. You can now find influencers that share content on just about any topic with their audience.
By April 2020, the app was downloaded over [2 billion](https://sensortower.com/blog/tiktok-downloads-2-billion) times, and it is continuing to grow worldwide.
If you have decided that you want to use TikTok in your campaigns then these powerful search tools can do what you and your business cannot do.
They can gather account data and analyse just about any information to find the most fitting profiles to your search queries.
Some of the key metrics that are used within the TikTok influencer search tools are:
  * Location
  * Age
  * Gender
  * Audience analysis – Age, Gender, Location and Size
  * Engagement and growth rate
  * Account quality
