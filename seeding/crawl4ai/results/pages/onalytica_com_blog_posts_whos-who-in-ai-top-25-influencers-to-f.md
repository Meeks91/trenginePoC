# Who’s Who in AI? Top 25 Influencers to Follow in 2024
### by | Sep 18, 2024
As the conversation around AI continues to evolve, the team at Onalytica has been listening closely to our customers and the broader market. The influence of AI in driving innovation, efficiency, and strategic decision-making is undeniable. It’s a topic that resonates not only with our clients but with the global business community as a whole.
In response to your feedback, we’ve refined our approach to these ‘Who’s Who’ lists. While Twitter remains a valuable platform for engagement, our focus has shifted towards LinkedIn as the primary channel for influencer partnerships. This change reflects the current landscape, where LinkedIn provides a more targeted and impactful avenue for professional connections and thought leadership in AI.
To make this list even more actionable, we’ve condensed it to a more curated selection of the top 25 influencers in AI. Our goal is to provide you with a concise yet powerful resource to help you navigate the AI influencer landscape effectively.
