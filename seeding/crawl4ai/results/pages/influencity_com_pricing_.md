## Frequently Asked Questions
What Are the Influencity Pricing Plans? 
### A Solution for Every Business
Influencity offers 3 pricing options to cover all your needs. 
**Professional** : Perfect for businesses that carry out end-to-end influencer marketing campaigns throughout the year.
**Business** : The solution for brands and agencies that invest heavily in influencer marketing strategies. This plan allows you to carry out multiple campaigns at the same time and share reports and analyses from the platform.
Not the right fit?
**Enterprise** : Let us tailor a plan that meets your specific needs. Include campaign workflows, opt for unlimited searches, and analyze all the profiles you need within one flexible plan.
Can I Change to a Different Plan Once I’ve Subscribed? 
### Flexibility Is Key
Yes, you can upgrade at any time and your current payment will be put toward your new subscription costs.
How Can I Cancel My Subscription? 
### Cancelation Made Easy
You can cancel at any time from your account settings menu.
To do so, just click on 'Products & Billing' to open your subscription details. Right below your plan description, you'll find a button that says 'Cancel subscription.'
Keep in mind that once your subscription is canceled and your billing period expires, you won't be able to access your campaign data or influencer archives.
Is There Mandatory Account Permanence? 
### Don't Get Locked In
No, there is no mandatory account permanence. Influencity gives you total freedom to manage your account as you like. 
Please keep in mind, however, that if you cancel and then resume the service, you will lose the influencer data you have collected and stored.
What Payment Methods Are Accepted at Influencity? 
### Multiple Payment Options
Payment can be processed through credit or debit card or bank transfer.
Who Issues Payment Invoices? 
### Your Invoices, When You Need Them
Invoices are issued by Influencity. Find them at any time by going to Settings and selecting the Billing menu.
Does Influencity offer additional services such as campaign strategy, influencer hiring, management, etc? 
### Customer Support from Start to Finish
No, we are not a marketplace or an agency and as such cannot offer support in hiring or managing influencers. However, our Customer Success team is available to help you learn how to make the most out of our platform.
