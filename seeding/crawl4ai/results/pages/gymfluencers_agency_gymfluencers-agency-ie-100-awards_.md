#### Gymfluencers Claims Top Honours at the IE 100 Awards
Gymfluencers has been crowned the UK’s Health & Fitness Influencer Marketing Agency of the Year at the 2026 [International Elite 100 Awards](https://internationalelite100.com/).
As the premier health and fitness influencer marketing agency, Gymfluencers rose above stiff competition to claim top spot and secure the title.
“This award represents both recognition and validation for everything we have been building over the past five years,” said Jonathan Lundy, Gymfluencers founder and CEO.
“I always say this company works in dog years, and that’s because every day our output matches what most would do in a week.
“We have come a long way in a relatively short space of time, so it is an incredible feeling for Gymfluencers to be championed as the Health & Fitness Influencer Marketing Agency of the Year.
“Most crucially, it tells us we are achieving exactly what we set out to do: provide value for everyone we work with.”
The IE 100 Awards were established to honour outstanding achievements by global talent across all areas of business.
The awards recognise excellence, innovation and leadership on an international scale, celebrating individuals and organisations that drive progress in their respective fields.
“Our awards are designed to spotlight the extraordinary ability to lead from the front, recognising and honouring those who relentlessly pursue excellence,” said a spokesperson for International Elite 100.
“We celebrate individuals and organisations who go above and beyond, setting new benchmarks for quality and inspiring others to follow their example.
“Our merit-driven process guarantees that any successful candidates nominated for our award can be confident that their accolades are truly deserved.
“Furthermore, it provides an opportunity for them to further showcase their achievements through the global circulation of our magazine.”
Gymfluencers first launched in 2021 with Gymfluencers.com, a free-to-use resource built to provide genuine value for everyone — from personal trainers looking to fill their diaries to everyday gym-goers needing workout and supplement tips.
It wasn’t long before the website established itself as the UK’s most trusted platform for industry news, product reviews, discount codes and expert insight.
Gymfluencers Agency followed soon after, aimed at fixing the problem of brands paying huge retainers for little-to-no meaningful return.
“Our aim is to always over-deliver,” Jonathan added. “We have never looked at what other agencies charge; our focus is on providing maximum value for brands in every campaign.
“Whether we’re working with a start-up or an industry heavyweight, our approach remains the same.
“Everybody gets identical treatment, and that’s why so many brands come back time and time again when they need content with impact.”
