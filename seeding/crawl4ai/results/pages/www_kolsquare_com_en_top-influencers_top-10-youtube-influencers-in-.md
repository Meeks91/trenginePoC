## Who are the most famous influencers in the UK? Our methodology
The Kolsquare database stores data on influencers with 5,000 or more followers on at least one of the popular platforms, including Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Facebook, X and Snapchat.
The ranking is, therefore, based on the critical information stored in our database. For each KOL, we provide you with some essential data, including:
  * **Credibility score /100** : Kolsquare's [credibility score](https://www.kolsquare.com/en/blog/fake-influencers-how-to-identify-them) ranges from 0 to 100 and indicates how authentic an influencer's audience is. The higher the number, the more real people are subscribed to their channel. Anything above 75 is a good score.
  * **Engagement rate** : This figure reveals how much sway the creator has over their audience. [Engagement rates](https://www.kolsquare.com/en/blog/instagram-engagement-rate-definition-calculation-and-tips-to-boost-it) are calculated by analysing how many followers interact with their posts through comments, shares, likes, etc.
  * **Earned media value (EMV)** : EMV helps you create a budget for your influencer partnership. It is the expected media value you will gain by hiring a key opinion leader (KOL) on each platform, such as TikTok or Instagram Reels.
