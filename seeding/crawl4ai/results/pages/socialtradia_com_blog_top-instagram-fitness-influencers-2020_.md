## How to become a top Instagram Fitness Influencer?
Get fit. Get in. Grow on Instagram to be at the top.
But how to become a top fitness influencer?
In simple straight-forward words, it takes consistency, quality, and the right social media tools to grow as an Instagram health influencer. Naturally, people are more attracted to visual than reading the content.
A shortcut to start as a fitness influencer is to [buy a sport or gym Instagram account](https://socialtradia.com) from those who grow Instagram pages. Although it can be beneficial and give you access to thousands of followers overnight, it could be costly, and you have to make sure you post quality content. Without quality and informative content, it’s almost impossible to be one of the top influencers in the sports industry.
Furthermore, personal engagement and interaction is the key to success. People like influencers to start back and forth communication through comments. Besides, all brands and even [local fitness businesses](https://socialtradia.com/blog/social-media-for-local-businesses/) such as gyms hunt for a high rate of engaged traffic to pick out the _right_ _Instagram fitness influencer_.
In short, proper consistent efforts through visual engagement is the right path in becoming one of the top Instagram athletic influencers.
## How much does a Fitness Influencer make?
Surprisingly, bikini and muscles pay a good amount of money! It is not simple to calculate exactly how much top Instagram fitness influencers earn annually. But we can estimate based on the number of followers, total engagement rate, and per post price they charge.
Nowadays, it has become very easy to buy followers for Instagram. These fake accounts are not helpful to sponsor a particular brand. To gain the maximum potential from best Instagram influencers, brands now focus on engagement rates.
With this in mind, a fitness influencer with high engaged 2K followers can [make more money](https://socialtradia.com/blog/students-make-money-using-instagram/) than another with a low engaged rate of 2M followers.
However, fitness influencer accounts with less than 10,000 followers make between $100 to $1,000 per post. Likewise, others with more than 10,000 followers make between $1,000 to $10,000 per post. Shockingly, this rate shoots up to $50,000 per post if they have higher active followers.
