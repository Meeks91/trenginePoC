# 15 Group Fitness Influencers to Follow on TikTok and Instagram
October 20, 2022
Group fitness is a great way to build physical and social strength. With a heart-pumping workout and supportive classmates, you can sweat away the pounds and find a healthy lifestyle. Look to these group fitness influencers for inspiration or to collaborate with [top fitness models](https://izea.com/resources/top-fitness-influencers/) and creators with engaged audiences.
## 15 group fitness influencers for at-home workouts and motivation
### Cody Rigsby
Cody is a former backup dancer who has worked with many celebrities on “Saturday Night Live” and competed on “Dancing with the Stars” in 2021. Thanks to his moves and hilarious personality, Cody has become one of Peloton’s most popular instructors. As one of the top fitness influencers on Instagram, Cody treats his followers to [fun 2-for-1 rides](https://www.instagram.com/p/CjLfWq_rEmU/?hl=en), gets the crowd involved during a [festival workout](https://www.instagram.com/p/Chdjr8GNwLp/?hl=en) and [keeps the positivity flowing](https://www.instagram.com/p/CfrXnrltwMU/?hl=en) during a class. 
### Natalia Haigler
Natalia is a health coach, trainer, former dancer, and Pilates aficionado. For her Instagram followers, Natalia shares tons of tutorials on the best Pilates exercises to try out with your crew at home or in the studio, like a [38-minute reformer workout](https://www.instagram.com/p/CfTmrTkA0N5/?hl=en), a quick-but-effective [12-minute inner thigh routine](https://www.instagram.com/p/CfBsGmSAj3V/?hl=en) sans props, and an [8-minute workout to sculpt your arms](https://www.instagram.com/p/Cd5Zk-SsaZE/?hl=en) with hand weights. 
### Amanda Rae
Based in Iowa, Amanda Rae is serious about making fitness “fun, functional, and out of the ordinary,” which she does on a daily basis by running her own tire fitness studio, Tire Trained. On TikTok, Amanda, known to her followers as amandarae.fit, leads her class through a fierce [full body workout](https://www.tiktok.com/@amandarae.fit/video/7113986034290986286?is_copy_url=1&is_from_webapp=v1), sets the bar high by [running while covered in tires](https://www.tiktok.com/@amandarae.fit/video/7128446597775641902?is_copy_url=1&is_from_webapp=v1), and keeps the momentum at full-blast during a [grueling glute workout](https://www.tiktok.com/@amandarae.fit/video/7120028605299576110?is_copy_url=1&is_from_webapp=v1).
### Nikki Metzger
Nikki is a passionate fitness trainer and studio owner in Scottsdale, Arizona. On Instagram, Nikki takes her followers behind-the-scenes as she shoots a [fitness video](https://www.instagram.com/p/CfMfAR0JMTf/?hl=en), promotes her [workout wear collab](https://www.instagram.com/p/Cdqh48Ll2a7/?hl=en) for all genders and body types, and demos a great [“bestie” workout](https://www.instagram.com/p/Ca2XU6Ep-3N/?hl=en) guaranteed to whip you and a friend into shape in no time.

For his TikTok followers, Dante shares his infectious energy and blood-pumping workout routines to follow along with at home.
